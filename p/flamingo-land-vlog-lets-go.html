Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Hi and welcome to my new channel, as well as my very first official vlog!

On Monday, my husband and I made a trip to Flamingo Land where we were blessed with blue skies, good operations and a very quiet park.

All rides were open apart from the lost river ride, which was fantastic. We rode all the major attractions as well as plenty of re rides. We also even had time to have a leisurely walk around the zoo!
