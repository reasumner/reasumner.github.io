San Marino Motor Classic 2024 - Drive-Ins

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
The San Marino Motor Classic is always one of our favorite events to attend year after year. Lots of incredibly rare and unique classic cars from very diverse parts of the car community come together to participate in this event at Lacy Park in San Marino, CA. This video shows most of the cars rolling in and around the event. Thanks to all our friends that participate in this event for the great hospitality and to all those involved in making this event one of the best in Southern California!
