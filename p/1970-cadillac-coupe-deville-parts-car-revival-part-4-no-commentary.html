Published at : 12 Dec 2025
This is a 1970 Cadillac Coupe Deville that was purchased to be used as a parts car. The car originally came from Missouri, with the last registration showing 1984. After removing all donor parts needed for another project, it has sat in the same spot for 6 months. So now the time has come for it to receive some attention. Enjoy!
