Published at : 13 Dec 2025
In this video we have shown how to connect DP MCB, RCCB, SP MCB, Neutral Link, Earth Link from energy meter to MCB distribution box in single phase house wiring.

Full form :-
MCB - Miniature Circuit Breaker
RCCB - Residual Current Circuit Breaker
DP MCB - Double Pole Miniature Circuit Breaker
SP MCB - Single Pole Miniature Circuit Breaker
ELCB - Earth-leakage circuit breaker
