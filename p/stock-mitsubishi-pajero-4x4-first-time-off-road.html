Stock Mitsubishi Pajero 4x4 - First time off road

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Throwback video of a Weekend trip through Beachport a few years ago.

One of our first off road trips in the stock Pajero.

All the 4WD's were challenged that day and almost all the cars (Pajeros, Prados, Landcuisers, Patrols) were bogged and needed recovery at some stage.

Now a few years later, after some mods (as you can see in my other videos) the beast certainly doesn't look or drive like it does now!
