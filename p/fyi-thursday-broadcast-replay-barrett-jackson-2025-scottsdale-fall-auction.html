FYI Thursday Broadcast Replay // Barrett-Jackson 2025 Scottsdale Fall Auction // Thursday, Oct 16

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
Tune in for the FYI broadcast of Barrett-Jackson Live, featuring commentary from the hosts you love like @HooviesGarage, @aprilsgarage, @Joy-Riding and @rickdebruhlcars!

Thursday of the 2025 Scottsdale Fall Auction, presented by @GilaRiverResortsCasinos, kicked off in style as guests raced into WestWorld on Thursday, Oct. 16. The day unfolded amid a sea of collector cars and over 150 interactive sponsor and exhibitor displays, setting the stage for another thrilling day of auction action and automotive energy. Among the visitors taking in the excitement were Bill and Tom Kaulitz from the German @Netflix show, “Kaulitz & Kaulitz.”

As part of the celebration, several area car clubs were in attendance and on display in the Car Club Corral, including Phoenix Driven, Mercedes-AMG Owners Club of Arizona, Ferrari Club AZ and the Phoenix Ducati Official Club.

In the North Showcase Pavilion presented by E.D. Marshall, guests were treated to a behind-the-scenes look at the all-new Auction Pre-Show with @CraigJacksonCollection, Barrett-Jackson President Steve Davis and “Barrett-Jackson LIVE” host @aprilsgarage (April Rose). Broadcast immediately before “Barrett-Jackson LIVE,” the “Auction Pre-Show” was set among some of the most anticipated vehicles set to cross the block on Super Saturday. Other Barrett-Jackson hosts, including Tyler Hoover, @Joy-Riding (Mike Joy) and @rickdebruhlcars, were on set as well.

The afternoon shifted into high gear as the focus turned to collector cars. Bidders and enthusiasts filled the arena, standing together for the singing of the national anthem before the first cars of the day rolled across the block. Thursday’s all-No-Reserve docket featured nearly 200 exceptional vehicles, with standouts that included:

LOT 433 – 1966 @Chevrolet CHEVELLE SS 396 – $60,500
LOT 423 – 1956 CHEVROLET 150 CUSTOM COUPE – $59,400
LOT 430 – 1970 @ford F-100 CUSTOM PICKUP – $55,000
LOT 423.1 – 1965 FORD MUSTANG FASTBACK – $51,700
LOT 463 - 1965 FORD FALCON FUTURA CONVERTIBLE - $50,600
