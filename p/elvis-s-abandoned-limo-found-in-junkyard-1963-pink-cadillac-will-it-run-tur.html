ELVIS PRESLEY'S LOST CADILLAC?!

Join in with the Turnin Rust Crew as they cross the map into the hill country of Tennessee to lay eyes on one of the coolest finds of all time! Resting in a junkyard for years, sits this forgotten 1963 pink Cadillac limo that was begging to have its story told. The guys jump through obstacles to see what it will take to see this ol' jewel run again and if there is any life left in this rare piece of automotive history. Follow along as we unearth the mystery of whether or not this car belonged to Elvis Presley and if so, how did it find its way into a junkyard?
