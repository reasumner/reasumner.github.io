We divide the hair into zones: temporal, central (crown), occipital.

We divide the rows into small strands and curl it in the "Hollywood wave" technique (twist/flat wrapping).
We use a curling iron 28mm. Each curl is combed at the base of the strand and along the length with the powder.
Next, sprinkle with wax and clean the surface.
