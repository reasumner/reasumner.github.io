Man Builds His Dream Campervan - Start to Finish

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
This is the whole process I did inside the van to bring it to the way I was dreaming of. My big deal was to build it in a way it should look like that Iam inside a trunk of a tree, or a cabin this is why I chose those colors and the way I disposed everything, including small details like do not show any trace of screws and nuts this is why I attached the ceiling just by pressure for exemple, and nothing more. On the walls I used screws in specific places in a way they got hidden by other pieces. Every single detail was studied during my 8 month trip to the end of the world, where I discovered all the needs including one of my favorite features, the the drawer that turns into a table that turns into a sink that goes outside the car.
Just adding my passion of work with tools in this job to create my dream campervan by my own, the way I like it, the way I feel it has to be. Simple, funcional and aesthetic.
