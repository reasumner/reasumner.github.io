A super slow motion view of a stone hitting a pond where the water is filled with snow and ice. As the stone hits the water the floating snow is thrown aside leaving a dark hole in the water! I used the super slow mo function on my smartphone to record this. It was a problem to time it right; but I am happy with the way it turned out! In a few days this pond will be covered in snow and the water will be frozen to hard ice; but that is how winter is like in my place!
