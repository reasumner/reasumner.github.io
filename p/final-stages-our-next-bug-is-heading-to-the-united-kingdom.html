In this episode we dive into what is happening in the 3L Engineering workshop... And there is a lot.

We are in the final stages of completion of our next full vehicle going to the United Kingdom. It is an Audi 4.2l 40 valve V8 and she is a beauty.

But that's not all, we have 3 full vehicles in production for the United States. We have donor bodies all over the workshop waiting for their turn at the body shop and we have brand new Chevrolet LS3 crate engines getting ready to be installed. Plus some other stuff. It's all in this video.
