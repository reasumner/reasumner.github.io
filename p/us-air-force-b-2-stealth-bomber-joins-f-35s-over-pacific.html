US Air Force B-2 stealth bomber joins F-35s over Pacific

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
Stunning shots capture a US B-2 Spirit stealth bomber as it flies alongside Air Force and Marine aircraft over the Pacific.

The US Air Force B-2 stealth bomber has been taking part in Exercise Valiant Shield 2024, a multinational, biennial field training exercise focused on integrating interoperability in the Indo-Pacific region.
