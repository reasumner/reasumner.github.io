INEOS Grenadier Sound System Upgrade | Custom A-Pillar Build, 3D Scan & Subwoofer Box

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Experience the ultimate INEOS Grenadier sound system upgrade at Cambridge Car Audio! In this video, we take this rugged 4x4 and transform it with a custom A-pillar build, 3D-scanned speaker housing, and a precision-engineered subwoofer enclosure designed for maximum performance and sound clarity.

Using the Creality Raptor Pro 3D scanner @Creality3DScanner we created a perfectly fitted custom A-pillar to house Thesis midrange speakers and high-performance tweeters, giving the Grenadier a powerful and immersive soundstage. We also fabricated a custom subwoofer box for deeper bass, cleaner lows, and distortion-free output.

This video is perfect for car audio lovers, 4x4 audio upgrade enthusiasts, and anyone looking for inspiration on custom fabrication, 3D scanning, and premium sound system builds.
