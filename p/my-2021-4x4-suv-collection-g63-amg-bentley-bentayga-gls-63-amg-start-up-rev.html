My 2021 4x4 SUV Collection, G63 AMG, Bentley Bentayga, GLS 63 AMG. Start up, Rev off & Acceleration

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
A detailed look at my 4x4 SUV Collection with my G63 AMG, Bentley Bentayga and my 2 GLS 63 AMG's, one 2021 model year & the other 2019.

I took delivery of my new G63 AMG earlier this week. Previously I have had the G63 AMG Edition 1, when the car was launched in 2018, along with my Urus. I was missing the G-Wagon in my supercar collection as it is a great off-roader, and in addition very comfortable to drive. The G-Wagon does look really cool! So enjoy a quick look around, start up, rev off and acceleration.

I bought my Bentley Bentayga in December 2020. This is the new model of the Bentley Bentayga, it was updated late last year. Previously I have owned 2 Bentayga's, one was an Edition 1 when the car was first launched and the other was a W12. My current Bentayga is a V8, Bentley is only offering V8 in UK and the W12 has been deleted for the UK market. The Bentley V8 is a very responsive engine and in my opinion more fun to drive, with Bentley I actually prefer the V8 engine over the W12. Bentley Bentayga is very comfortable and luxurious but also very sporty to drive.

My GLS 63 AMG 2021 I took delivery of over at Christmas. The Mercedes GLS is one of my favourite models of SUVs, as it is a very versatile car. The technology in the new GLS 63 AMG 2021 is amazing: heated, cooled & massaging seats in front & rear; temperature control cupholders front & rear; wireless chargers in front & rear; 5 entertainment screens and the list goes on! In my opinion the GLS 63 AMG is great fun to drive, handling is amazing for it's size. This is my 15th GLS that I have owned, every car I have driven around 30K miles before changing it for a new one. It is a staple car in my Collection.

Lastly, my GLS 63 AMG 2019, a 5.4 Litre V8, an absolute monster. It sounds really great. Currently it is on 30K miles so I will be replacing soon with something else.
