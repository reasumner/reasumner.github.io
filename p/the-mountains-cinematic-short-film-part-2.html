THE MOUNTAINS - A Cinematic Short Film | PART 2.
There's nothing I could say that could describe how I really feel about The Mountains.
With this video, I though I might bring something to life, something that's been on my mind and in my heart for the last few months.
I know it may not seem like it, but these last few months have been extremely challenging for me. We live in a world driven by numbers, where we're constantly comparing ourselves to each other, looking at what other people are or have and we're often feeling less, than someone else.
I forgot what it's like to be inspired again, to create something that I love, something for me, not for brands, not for anyone else, just for that kid that was once happy and extremely motivated running around with a camera.

And this is the place that I go, when I feel demotivated. It's where I can be alone with my thoughts, with myself, figuring out what matters and what doesn't. The Mountains are and forever will be a Therapy for me.

Hope you guys enjoy this video just as much as I enjoyed making it.
Would love to hear what you think (see you in the comments).
