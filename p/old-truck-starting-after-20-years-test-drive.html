Old Truck Starting After 20 Years + Test Drive

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
In this video, we embark on an extraordinary project - the revival of the Praga S5T 1965, which has been constantly active for 20 years. Together we will go through all the steps from checking the engine, battery to the first attempts to start it. Will we manage to bring this Czechoslovak gem to life? the whole process and enjoy the sound of the engine after two decades of silence! Don't forget to subscribe and leave a comment with your impressions. If you like the video, support us with a like!"
