Ukraine Drones Attack on a Russian Helicopter — Then THIS Happened...! #ukraine

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
Ukraine struck before dawn, when radar crews were least alert. A single low-cost drone climbed through fog, locking onto a Russian helicopter at low altitude. Seconds later, impact—fire tearing through the rotor arc. Ukraine had planned the intercept for weeks, studying flight paths and weather windows. For Ukraine, this wasn’t luck but calculation. The explosion sent a message across the front: Ukraine no longer chases targets, Ukraine predicts them. And as footage spread online, Ukraine proved again that modern war belongs to those who think faster, not those who spend more.
