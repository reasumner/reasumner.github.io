2007 Mercedes GL450 review: 165k miles & still going strong

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
2007 Mercedes GL450 - what are the problems? How does it compare to other or newer SUV's? Is it expensive to own? Why are they so cheap? What should I be on the look-out for?

The 2007 Mercedes GL450 is a big, smooth, good-looking and nice-driving SUV that should be at the top of your search list for a luxury 3rd row SUV under $10,000

But there are some things to know and some things to look out for, like the Mercedes GL450 air suspension which can be expensive to fix. Mercedes GL450 maintenance is no big deal but GL450 oil changes require a special version of Mobil 1 synthetic oil. GL450 power is outstanding and you can still expect 18+ mpg combined driving.
