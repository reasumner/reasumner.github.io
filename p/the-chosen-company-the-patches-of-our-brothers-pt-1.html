The Chosen Company. The Patches of Our Brothers | Pt.1

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
This documentary covers the three day defensive operation of the Chosen Company during the Russian push in Donetsk oblast in the Pervomaiske region. What started as an assault attempt to relieve some of the friendly forces, quickly became a harsh and intense combat to deny the enemy their advancement. Usually the famous assault company chose their battles and dictate the pace. This time - the battle has chosen them.

This is the first part of the film. The second part will be released in a week. Subscribe to our YouTube channel and turn on notifications, so you do not miss it.
