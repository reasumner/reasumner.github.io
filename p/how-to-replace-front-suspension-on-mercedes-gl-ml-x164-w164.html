How to replace front suspension on Mercedes GL/ML (X164/W164)

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
A quick DIY on how to replace the front suspension components (upper control arm, ball joint and tie rod) on the 2006-2012 Mercedes-Benz GL and ML class SUV (X164/W164 chassis) without removing of the Airmatic suspension parts (shock and air springs).

Tools needed:
- Wrenches, screw drivers, hammer, pliers, pipe wrenches, torque wrench, breaker bar, torch
- #9 HEX (to remove the brake calipers)
- #36 socket (to remove the shaft nut)

Parts:
