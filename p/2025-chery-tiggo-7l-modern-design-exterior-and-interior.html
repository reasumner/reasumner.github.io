2025 Chery Tiggo 7L - Modern Design | Exterior and interior

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Chery Tiggo 7L is a city crossover with an expressive design, technological equipment and a well-thought-out level of comfort. The model combines a spacious interior with modern digital solutions and flexible space transformation options. Reliable suspension, an economical turbo engine and an expanded set of safety systems make Tiggo 7L a universal choice for the city and country trips.

Chery Tiggo 7L is available in three well-thought-out trim levels — Active, Prime and Ultra — each of which is focused on a high level of comfort, safety and technology.

Active is the optimal solution for everyday use. The package includes: cruise control, choice of driving modes, keyless entry, rear parking sensors, rear view camera, a full set of safety systems (ABS, ESC, airbags and curtain airbags), tire pressure monitoring system, automatic headlights and wipers, as well as the Isofix system.

Prime is a step forward towards comfort and extended functionality. The basic equipment includes an electric tailgate with contactless opening, a 540° all-round view system, front parking sensors, an automatic mirror adjustment system, an extended heating package and improved sound insulation. Electric seat drives and extended interior elements create a more premium environment.

Ultra is the top-end package, which includes a full set of modern driver assistants: adaptive cruise control (ACC), lane keeping assist (LKA), forward collision warning (FCW), automatic braking system (AEB), blind spot monitoring, assistance when changing and opening doors, intelligent headlights and traffic jam assistant. Acoustic glass, wireless charging, extended multimedia, voice control and memory of driver's seat settings are also added.
