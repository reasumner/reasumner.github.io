NEW Toyota Highlander Hybrid (2023) - Interior and Exterior Details

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
The new 2023 Toyota Highlander is a mid-size SUV that offers a combination of versatility, comfort, and reliability. It is designed to meet the needs of families and individuals who prioritize space, safety, and functionality without compromising on style.
The exterior of the Toyota Highlander exudes a robust and sophisticated presence. It features a sleek and aerodynamic profile with sculpted lines and a bold front grille. The LED headlights and daytime running lights add a touch of modernity, while the available panoramic moonroof provides an open and airy feel to the cabin. The Highlander's overall design strikes a balance between elegance and practicality.
Step inside, and you'll discover a spacious and well-crafted interior that can comfortably accommodate up to eight passengers, depending on the seating configuration. The seating is plush and supportive, ensuring a comfortable ride for long journeys. The second-row seats can be adjusted for legroom, and the third-row seats can be folded down to create extra cargo space when needed. High-quality materials, soft-touch surfaces, and thoughtful design details enhance the overall ambience of the cabin.
In terms of technology, the Toyota Highlander is equipped with an array of features. The standard infotainment system includes a touchscreen display, smartphone integration, and Bluetooth connectivity. The Highlander also offers multiple USB ports and power outlets, allowing occupants to stay connected and charged on the go.
The Toyota Highlander prioritizes safety and is equipped with a comprehensive suite of advanced driver-assistance systems. This includes features such as adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning with steering assist, forward collision warning with automatic emergency braking, and pedestrian detection.

Engine: 2.5 Hybrid 248 hp, 182 kW, 270 Nm
Top Speed: 180 km/h
0-100 km/h: 8.3 sec
