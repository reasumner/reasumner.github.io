These buns are driving the world crazyâ— Try them and be amazedâ—

Ingredients and recipe:
250 ml of warm milk.
60 g of sugar.
8 g of dry yeast.
mix well.
1 egg.
40 ml of vegetable oil.
5 g of salt.
mix well.
600 g of wheat flour.
mix well with a spatula.
40 g of butter.
cover and let rise for an hour.
300 g of cheese.
50 g of sugar.
7 g of vanilla sugar.
We grate the zest from a lemon.
mix well.
We divide the dough into 12 equal parts.
Form each piece of dough into a ball.
cover and leave for 10 minutes.
Roll out the dough with a rolling pin.
Make 4 cuts in the dough.
add the cheese filling in the middle.
We form the bun with the sweet cheese.
Place the buns on a baking tray lined with baking paper.
Prepare all the remaining pieces of dough in the same way.
cover and leave for 20 minutes.
Mix an egg yolk with a tablespoon of milk.
Bake at 180 degrees Celsius for 30 minutes.
