THE FIRST VIDEO - Roothy

Take a trip back in time to the days before 4WD Action when the mag was still known as 4WD Monthly and we were off on our first ever video trip.
I was still working as a tour guide back then and the whole filming thing was Pat Callinan's passion. He pushed for it, raised the sponsorship and set something in motion that's still rolling today, albeit in a lot more crowded space as everybody take a camera everywhere!
Off we went on a massive trip from The Simpson Desert to Fraser Island and all points bush tracked in between. Milo had just copped her 13BT engine transplant and I was carrying as much cooking gear as tools and parts, because there's nothing like a shake down run half way across a country the size of Australia.
Anyway here it is - Australia, big bold and beautiful and just waiting for you to go adventuring too - although life sure was simpler back then before the city based greens realised there was something beyond their cafe latays...
