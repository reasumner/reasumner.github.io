London to Brussels by BUS via Eurotunnel: What's It Like?

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
Watch this video to see what it's like to travel on a First Class Bus between London and Brussels UNDER THE SEA!

Have you ever been on a bus that goes on a train? Well, in this video, we are doing just that. As I spend 6 HOURS onboard a long distance bus across 3 countries and UNDER THE SEA!.

On this double-decker road trip from the UK to Belgium, we will utilise the Eurotunnel "Le Shuttle" railway shuttle service between England and France through the Channel Tunnel. This was an incredible experience. But would I do it again?

This is one bus ride that I will never forget!
