Extreme Ways - Pakistani Factory Recycles Used Tires & Cow Bones @TopWorks

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
This video reveals the most extreme recycling processes carried out in Pakistani factories. From producing oil through the pyrolysis of used tires to making buttons from animal bones, all are carried out by Pakistani workers using simple yet efficient techniques. This is a unique aspect of Pakistani industry, where waste is transformed into high-value commodities. Most of these products are actually made in Pakistan, forming a vital part of Pakistani manufacturing and thriving as business opportunities in many Pakistani factories that rely on the creativity of recycling. @TopWorks
