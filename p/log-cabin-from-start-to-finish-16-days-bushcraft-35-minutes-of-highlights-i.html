LOG CABIN from START to FINISH | 16 days BUSHCRAFT | 35 minutes of highlights in SHORTS video format

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
#bushcraft #survival #outdoors #logcabin
In this video, I have combined all the stages of building a log cabin in our bushcraft camp. Mostly I worked on it with my dad, but sometimes alone. We started building in the fall and finished when the first snow had already fallen. We set up a camping stove inside the shelter. The walls were insulated with moss. The door was hung on metal hinges. The roof consists of a log frame, thin branches woven together, polyethylene and spruce branches to hold snow.
My friend Andrei and my son Arseniy visited the house and contributed to its construction.
