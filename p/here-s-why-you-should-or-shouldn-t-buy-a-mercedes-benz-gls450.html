In this video I talk about why you should or shouldn’t buy a Mercedes Benz GLS450. So, the GLS450. Contrary to its prior generation, the GLS now has a lot of competition. Does it have what it takes to continue its dominance in the luxury 7 seater SUV category. We’re going to find out, in this review of the Mercedes-Benz GLS450
