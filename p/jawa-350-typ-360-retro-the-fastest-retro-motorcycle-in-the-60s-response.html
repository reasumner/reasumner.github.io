Those who are interested in rare motorcycles know that the 360 model has been produced since 1964. Java was distinguished by its beauty and uniqueness, so the lucky ones who had such a motorcycle were considered popular and advanced.

But when manufacturers began to produce a new generation of models that used new spare parts, Java 360 got the nickname "old lady". Nowadays, the rare model is valued for its appearance and characteristics.

After all, if you touch the smooth rims of the body, look closely at the chrome sides of the gas tank and inhale the mixture of gasoline and oil, you will immediately want to give preference to him, and not to an expensive motorcycle or a Chinese copy.
