Ingredients

For chicken mixing

1) boneless chicken strips - 500 gms thin strips
2) egg white - 1 egg
3) ginger garlic paste - 1 table spoon
4) soya sauce - 1 tea spoon
5) vinegar - 1 tea spoon
6) black pepper powder - 1/2 tea spoon
7) salt - as per taste
8) corn flour - 1/4 cup

For Chicken Frying

1) oil for frying chicken

for Chicken Chili cooking

1) Oil - 1 table spoon
2) garlic - 2 table spoon fine chopped
3) ginger - 1 table spoon fine chopped
4) green chili - 2 - 3 chilies cut in slits
5) bell peppers - red and green
6) onion - some
7) spring onion - some
8) red chili sauce - 2 table spoon
9) soya sauce - 1 tea spoon
10) vinegar - 1 tea spoon
11) black pepper - 1/2 tea spoon
12) sugar - 1/2 tea spoon
13) corn flour - 1 table spoon
14) water - 1/4 cup
15) tomato ketchup - 1 table spoon
16) sesame seeds - some
17 salt - as required

Chili chicken Cooking Process

- take boneless chicken strips in a mixing bowl and add egg white, then add ginger garlic paste, soya sauce, vinegar, black pepper powder, salt, corn flour, mix well all together.

- heat oil in a pan on medium heat, and chicken pieces in to it, fry chicken 7 - 8 minutes in hot oil

- in a wok or pan heat oil and add fine chopped ginger and garlic and saut√©, now add slits green chili and saut√©, add splash of water. now add bell peppers, onion, spring onion and saut√© all together. Now add fried chicken pieces, red chili sauce, soya sauce, black pepper powder, sugar and mix well. now take make corn flour mixture, with adding 1 table spoon of corn flour and 1/4 cup of water, mix it well and in to pan, mix all together well. now add tomato ketchup, sesame seeds, spring onion, salt if required and mix it well.

- now add some onion, bell pepper, for extra crunch and mix it well.

- Chili chicken ready to be served.
