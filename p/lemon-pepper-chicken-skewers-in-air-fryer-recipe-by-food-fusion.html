Lemon Pepper Chicken Skewers in Air fryer Recipe by Food Fusion

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
Another perfect air fryer recipe. Lemon pepper chicken skewers in air fryer. #HappyCookingToYou #FoodFusion #PakistaniCooking #airfryermagic #airfryerrecipes

Written Recipe: https://bit.ly/45yky81

Recipe in English:

Ingredients:
Prepare Butter Glaze:
-Makhan (Butter) soft 4-5 tbs (50g)
-Lal mirch (Red chilli) crushed ¼ tsp or to taste
-Kali mirch (Black pepper) crushed ½ tsp
-Lemon juice 1 tsp or as required
-Fresh parsley chopped 1 tbs
Prepare Chicken Skewers:
-Boneless chicken preferably thighs 500g
-Lehsan (Garlic) chopped 1 tbs
-Paprika powder 1 tsp
-Namak (Salt) 1 tsp or to taste
-Dried oregano ½ tsp
-Kali mirch powder (Black pepper powder) 1 tsp
-Onion powder 1 tsp
-Lemon juice 2-3 tbs
-Cooking oil 1 tbs

Directions:
Prepare Butter Glaze:
-In a bowl,add butter,red chilli crushed,black pepper crushed,lemon juice,fresh parsley,mix well & set aside.
Prepare Chicken Skewers:
-Cut chicken thighs in pieces.
-In a bowl,add chicken,garlic,paprika powder,salt,dried oregano,black pepper powder,onion powder, lemon juice,cooking oil & mix well,cover & marinate for 30 minutes.
-Skew marinated chicken in wooden skewers.
Option # 1:
-Air fry in preheated air fryer at 200C for 6-7 minutes,turn sides in between & continue air frying for 6-7 minutes (makes 6 skewers).
Option # 2:
-You can cook/grill in pan from all sides until done.
-Apply prepared butter glaze from all sides on chicken skewers & serve!

Recipe in Urdu:

Ajza:

-Lal mirch (Red chilli) crushed ¼ tsp or to taste
-Kali mirch (Black pepper) crushed ½ tsp
-Lemon juice 1 tsp or as required
-Fresh parsley chopped 1 tbs
Prepare Chicken Skewers:
-Boneless chicken preferably thighs 500g
-Lehsan (Garlic) chopped 1 tbs
-Paprika powder 1 tsp
-Namak (Salt) 1 tsp or to taste
-Dried oregano ½ tsp
-Kali mirch powder (Black pepper powder) 1 tsp

-Lemon juice 2-3 tbs
-Cooking oil 1 tbs

Directions:
Prepare Butter Glaze:
-Bowl mein makhan,lal mirch crushed,kali mirch crushed,lemon juice aur fresh parsley dal ker ache tarhan mix ker lein & side per rakh dein.
Prepare Chicken Skewers:
-Chicken thighs ko pieces mein cut ker lein.
-Bowl mein chicken,lehsan,paprika powder,namak,dried oregano,kali mirch powder,onion powder,lemon juice aur cooking oil dal ker ache tarhan mix karein aur dhak ker 30 minutes kliya marinate ker lein.
-Marinated chicken ko wooden skewers per laga lein.
Option # 1:
-Preheated oven mein 200C per 6-7 minutes kliya air fry karein,bech mein sides ko turn ker lein & mazeed 6-7 minutes kliya air fry ker lein (makes 6 skewers).
Option # 2:
-Cook/grill pan per tamam sides sa cook/grill ker lein.
-Tayyar butter glaze ko chicken skewers ko tamam sides per laga ker serve karein!
