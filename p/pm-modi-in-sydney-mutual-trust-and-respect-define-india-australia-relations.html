PM Modi in Sydney: Mutual trust and respect define India-Australia relationship

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Addressing the Indian diaspora at a grand gathering in Sydney, Prime Minister Narendra Modi highlighted the foundation of India-Australia relations, which he described as being built upon mutual trust and respect. PM Modi mentioned that the relationship was previously associated with the "3Cs" - Commonwealth, Cricket, and Curry. It later evolved to be characterized by "Democracy, Diaspora, and Dosti" (friendship). Some also emphasized the significance of "Energy, Economy, and Education" in the relationship. However, PM Modi expressed his belief that the bond between India and Australia goes beyond these aspects and is rooted in mutual trust and mutual respect.
