BRIGHTWELLS DECEMBER 2025 Car Auction Preview

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
I recorded this video, the Brightwells December 2025 classic car auction preview, on 2nd December 2025. The auction itself kicks off on Saturday 6th and runs through until the following Wednesday, with bids accepted via the Brightwells auction website.
The December 2025 auction comprises classic cars, modern classics, plus several motorcycles, bicycles and even a Leyland Comet lorry from the 1950s. In this video I have a good walk around the majority of the older vehicles present - please remember that mention of any classic car or other vehicle in this video isn't a recommendation to bid, as I always say these auction previews are just that - previews/introductions - and anyone interested in placing a bid should do their own research and inspections independent of anything I show or discuss on the channel.
As always there is a real mix of entries to this Brightwells classic car auction, with a good number of pre-war and vintage cars mixing it with later classics and retro/modern classic cars. What auction entries would I bid on? I try to pick a favourite, don't forget to let me know your favourite auction entries in the comments section.
Highlights for me this time include a fine Ford Model AA truck, 1927 Chevrolet tourer, 1924 Lanchester limousine, 1922 Morris Oxford, Daimler DB18, Riley Falcon, a very much "barn find" Lagonda 2.6 litre saloon, an immaculate Daimler Double Six (Series 3 shape), Standard Vanguard Phase 2, a pair of Rover P4s (plus an earlier P3), a pair of 1928 Austin 12/4 saloon cars, 1938 Citroen Traction Avant, and a race-prepared Austin A40 Mk1. All these cars and more feature in this, the Old Classic Car preview of the Brightwells Auction December 2025.
Thanks for watching, constructive comments welcome as always.
