Today, in this video you will learn how to make perfect crispy and juicy jalebi at home. popular recipe in india, we make this during all the special occasion and festivals.
Jalebi, also known as zulbia, jilapi, mushabak and zalabia, is an Indian sweet popular all over South Asia, in Egypt and the Middle East. It is prepared by deep frying refined wheat flour batter in circular shapes, then soaked in sugar syrup. serve hot jalebi with cold rabri.
