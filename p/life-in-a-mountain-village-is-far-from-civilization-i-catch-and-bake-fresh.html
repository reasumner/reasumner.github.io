Life in a mountain village is far from civilization, I catch and bake fresh trout.

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
In this movie, I will tell more about my everyday life and life in the "Wild Carpathian Forests". In this documentary you will see how I mow the grass and cultivate the pond, how I drive hay with a tractor, how I take care of the farm and grow vegetables in the garden, catch and bake trout, which I feed and grow myself. I think you will appreciate my efforts, thanks to all the good people who love the simple country life. This story is about how you can find true happiness and peace in simple things and the silence of nature.
