How to Restore Headlights via Hand Sanding

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Do you NEED a machine polisher/sander to restore headlights? Nope! The fact is, while time-consuming and strenuous, it is possible to get AMAZING results restoring headlight polycarbonate by hand to remove oxidation and yellowing from the sun's UV rays.

Watch my new guide taken from a recent LIVE online detailing class and learn the techniques necessary to uncloud those headlights and restore the polycarbonate to crystal clarity so you can illuminate the dark roads late at night.
