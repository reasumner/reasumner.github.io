Making This 40-Year-Old Interior Beautiful Again

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
In the last putting-togethering video of the JurboDiesel wagon, I demonstrate how I restore my innards.

Perhaps this will help you spice your old hooptie's interior too. Some stuff I do: headliner recovering, cracked dash restoration (with coverlay and without coverlay), carpet replacement, plastic trim restoration, power window repair, door trim restoration, prefrontal lobotomy, seat elastic repair, upholstery stitching repair, speaker installation, and a bunch of other stuff.
