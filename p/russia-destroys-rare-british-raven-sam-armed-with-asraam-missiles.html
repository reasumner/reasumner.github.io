Russia Destroys Rare British Raven SAM Armed with ASRAAM Missiles!

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
New frontline footage shows a burned-out British Raven SAM launcher — a custom-built air defense system equipped with AIM-132 ASRAAM missiles — destroyed inside a battlespace completely dominated by Russian precision weapons. ZALA reconnaissance drones from the 68th Guards Corps detected the system, fed its coordinates across the network, and soon after, a Lancet loitering munition finished the job. This same reconnaissance-strike cycle has repeatedly eliminated Western systems from Caesars to Bohdanas, leaving Ukraine’s air defenses increasingly hollow. As Ukrainian SAM coverage collapses, Russia has expanded its precision strikes on energy infrastructure across Chernihiv, Sumy, Nikopol, and Kherson, while responding to Ukrainian attacks with Iskander missiles and Geran UAVs. Meanwhile, Russian Buk-M2 units intercept HIMARS rockets, and additional Ukrainian assets — Buk systems, M777s, EW stations, depots, and vehicles — continue to fall. The destroyed Raven system stands as yet another example that Western improvisation cannot survive under Russia’s integrated reconnaissance-strike complex.
