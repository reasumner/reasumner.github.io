How did one 'ugly' little car, built in 49 days by a bankrupt company, become Eisenhower's #1 weapon?

Discover how this one "tiny object" became the hero of D-Day, the SAS's weapon in North Africa, and the only thing that could save soldiers freezing in the Battle of the Bulge. This isn't just the story of a car; it's the story of the machine that liberated a planet.
