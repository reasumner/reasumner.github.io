HOW TO GROW MINT IN PLASTIC BOTTLES USING THIS NEW TRICK | RE-GROWING MINT IN DIY RECYCLED PLANTER

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Growing mint herb is really easy using plastic bottles at home. This DIY planter made by recycling waste plastic bottles is built on unique concept of air pruning which helps grow your greens faster and healthier. This is a no-fuss – no-space – and low maintenance totally new way of growing your own fresh mint, right here in your kitchen.
Read full article here: https://www.MyGardenTips.com/how-to-grow-mint-in-plastic-bottles/

To get started all you need is an empty plastic bottle. Preferably, Not too large, as we plan to hang it. A small piece of PVC pipe and a good quality string/rope.
