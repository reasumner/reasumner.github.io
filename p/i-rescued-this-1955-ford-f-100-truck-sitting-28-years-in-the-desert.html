I Rescued This 1955 Ford F-100 Truck, Sitting 28 Years in the Desert

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
In this episode I attempt a revival of the original V8 engine of the 1955 Ford F-100, abandoned for 28 years. I clean out and pressure wash the truck and discover some history dating back to 1970. After some engine trouble and electrical problems discovered I make plans for my goal of turning this into the ultimate shop truck and long distance tow rig.
