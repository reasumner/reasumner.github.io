STORY
This magnificent palace, dating back to the early 18th century, stands as a testament to the grandeur of a bygone era. Its rooms have been meticulously preserved, serving as a time capsule that encapsulates the rich and vibrant life of a noble family who once called this place home over twenty years ago. However, today, a haunting silence fills the air as the descendants of this illustrious family have taken different paths, leaving their ancestral abode behind.

Every nook and cranny of this palace whispers tales of its former glory. Once notable figures in the community, the family amassed their wealth through vast land ownership. But their legacy extended beyond material riches. They were known for their benevolence and charitable work, lending a helping hand to the locals during times of hardship—a true testament to their compassionate spirits.

The patriarch of the family, João, was an accomplished electrical engineer, and remnants of his occupation still linger in his office, with documents strewn about. This grand palace was shared with his beloved wife and their four gifted children, each with their own unique story to tell.

As we step foot inside the palace, a world of marvel unfolds before our eyes. Each room reveals a captivating story, drawing us deeper into its embrace. The magnificent dining area, adorned with numerous chairs arranged around a breathtaking skylight, beckons us to gather and imagine the lively conversations that once filled the space. The exquisitely designed living area, undoubtedly the heart of the house, emanates an everlasting beauty that transcends time itself. It becomes abundantly clear that this place is a treasure trove, brimming with charm and allure at every corner.

Join us on this enchanting exploration as we peel back the layers and unravel the hidden mysteries that have been safeguarded for centuries within the hallowed halls of this noble palace. Prepare to be captivated by the untold stories and fascinating secrets that have remained locked away, waiting to be shared with the world.
