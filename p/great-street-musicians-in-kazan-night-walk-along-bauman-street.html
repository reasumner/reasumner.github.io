Great street musicians in Kazan! Night walk along Bauman street!

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
A night walk along Bauman Street in Kazan is a great time to listen to music and walk several times in a cozy atmosphere!

The video was filmed in August 2022.

Thank you for your support, likes and comments!

Lazy Walker is a small YouTube channel that will allow you to walk with me around Moscow and other Russian cities!

I spend a lot of time on my feet, so I can share with you numerous videos taken with the camera and the DJI Pocket 2 smartphone in different parts of Moscow. Interesting places and beautiful girls are waiting for you here, a very pleasant walk through the cities day and night! We will visit many beautiful streets and avenues, go to shops and shopping centers, go down the subway and ride the tram around the city!
