BMW Production in Germany

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Join us on a tour of the BMW Group production plants in Germany: Dingolfing, Leipzig, Munich, and Regensburg. These mega-factories produce most of the BMW brand cars and the compact X SUV models. In this video you can see the production of the following vehicles: BMW 2 Series, BMW 3 Series, BMW 4 Series, BMW 5 Series, BMW 6 Series, BMW 7 Series, and the electric variants: BMW i4, BMW i5, BMW i7. Also included is the production of the BMW X2 (and iX2 electric variant), BMW iX, BMW i3, and BMW i8 plug-in hybrid hybrid sports car. And on the historic footage front: the 1951 BMW 501 and the 1988 BMW Z1.
