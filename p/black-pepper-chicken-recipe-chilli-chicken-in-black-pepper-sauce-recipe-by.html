Black Pepper Chicken recipe ! Chilli Chicken In Black Pepper Sauce recipe by Just Smile And Cook

ingredients

for chicken marinade

1) boneless chicken - 400 gms ( from chicken thighs)
2) salt - as required
3) chopped garlic - some
4) cumin powder - 1/4 tea spoon
5) chili flakes - 1/4 tea spoon
6) black pepper powder - 1/2 tea spoon
7) ginger garlic paste - 1 tea spoon
8) vinegar - 1 tea spoon
9) soya sauce - 1 tea spoon
10) egg white - 1 egg only

for black pepper sauce

1) black pepper powder - 1/2 tea spoon
2) chopped garlic - some
3) vinegar - 1 tea spoon
4) soya sauce - 1 tea spoon
5) corn flour - 1/2 tea spoon
6) chicken stock - 1/4 cup ( home made) - you can use water either.

for chicken frying

1) corn flour - 2 table spoon
2) refined flour - 1 big table spoon
3) oil for frying chicken

for black pepper chicken final cooking

1) oil - 1 table spoon
2) chopped garlic - some
3) green chili - some cut in slits
4) green capsicum - some
5) onion - some
6) salt - pinch of or as required
7) spring onion - some roughly chopped


preparation and cooking method

- take chicken pieces in a mixing bowl and add salt, chopped garlic, cumin powder, chili flakes, black pepper powder, ginger garlic paste, vinegar, soya sauce, 1 egg white, mix it well and rest it for 30 minutes.

- now in a mixing bowl take black pepper powder, chopped garlic, vinegar, soya sauce, corn flour, chicken stock and mix it well and keep aside.

- after 30 minutes add in chicken corn flour, refined flour and mix it well, and our chicken is ready for frying.

- heat oil and fry chicken in medium flame till chicken is well cooked. remove chicken from oil and fry second batch of chicken if frying in batches.

- fry all chicken one more time in high flame for about 2 minutes, and remove it.

- now in a wok add oil, then add chopped garlic and fry for 2 minutes on medium heat. now add green chili slits, green capsicum, onion and fry it for 1 minute on high heat.

-now add prepared black pepper sauce and mix it well and cook for 2 minutes on medium heat.
now add salt and mix well. now add fried chicken pieces, spring onion and mix it well.

black pepper chicken is ready to serve, serve it hot.
