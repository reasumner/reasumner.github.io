G-Wagon Buyer's Guide: Experts Reveal Best Models to Buy

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Looking to buy a used Mercedes G-Wagon but don't want a rusty nightmare? You've come to the right place! In this ultimate G-Wagon buyer's guide, we travel to the birthplace of the G-Class – Austria – to get expert advice from two G-Wagon gurus: Richy, a military 461 Wolf daily driver, and Bernie Paltram, a Mercedes dealership owner who restores and sells G-Wagons.

What You'll Learn in This G-Wagon Buying Guide:

Which G-Wagon models are best for daily driving & off-roading on a budget. (Richie recommends 90s 463 G500 & mid-90s 350/320 petrol models).
The absolute WORST thing about buying a used G-Wagon (and how to avoid it!): RUST!
Expert tips on exactly where to check for rust on both classic 461 and luxury 463 G-Wagons.
Reliability insights on popular G-Wagon engines like the 5-cylinder turbo diesel (OM602, found in 461s like the 290GD and 250GD) and naturally aspirated V8s (G500).
Maintenance expectations for older G-Class vehicles.
Why pre-2019 G-Wagons (463 & 461) are considered the "real" G-Wagons compared to the newer, more luxurious models.
The truth about G-Wagon mileage and why it might not be as important as you think.
How to potentially find a great used G-Wagon for under $35,000-$40,000 USD.
Whether you're looking for a rugged military G-Wagon (461 Wolf), a capable yet comfortable 463, or just want to understand the G-Class market, this video is packed with essential information from those who know these vehicles best.
