BRABUS 850 6.0 Biturbo CLS63 S AMG - INSANE REVS & DRAG RACE!

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
This video features the BRABUS 850 6.0 Biturbo CLS63 S participated in this years SCC500's event 'Rolling50 Deutsche Meisterschaft' on Black Forest Airport in Lahr, Germany. This event is all about dragracing. 1000m, a rolling start at 50km/h and insane cars!

The 850 is not really the ideal car to use on a racetrack or drag race, but it was quite impressive to see in action! What a ROCKET!! The 5.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 gives an output to a whopping 850 hp, while the torque is electronically limited to a massive 1150 Nm!
