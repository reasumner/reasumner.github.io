The ANCAP technical report for the 2026 Toyota Hilux (model series AN2) covers all variants except the Rugged X, and applies to vehicles sold from November 2025 in New Zealand and December 2025 in Australia. Standard equipment includes dual frontal airbags, side chest and head-protecting airbags, a driver knee airbag, plus a centre airbag, alongside Toyota Safety Sense with autonomous emergency braking across multiple scenarios and lane support functions.
