Should you buy the last Mitsubishi Pajero / Montero / Shogun | Owner review in 2021

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Mitsubishi Pajero (aka Montero, Shogun) is just about to ride into the sunset as Mitsubishi will stop production of this iconic car in 2021 after 39 years. It's not too late to snap one up though - the question is just, should you? LIKE WHAT YOU SEE? Join us on Patreon at https://patreon.com/ownerdrive

Also, check out our new website - https://ownerdrive.com!

Is the Mitsubishi Pajero still a worthwhile new-car purchase? We speak to Andy, who has owned (multiple Mitsubishis, including) a Pajero for the past three years to get his honest insights and recommendations.

Andy drives the top-of-line Pajero Exceed, which includes creature comforts like electronic sunroof, leather seats, and a Rockford Fosgate sound system.
