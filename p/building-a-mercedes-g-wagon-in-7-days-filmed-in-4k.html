Building a Mercedes G-Wagon in 7 days [Filmed in 4K]

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
In 1979, Mercedes Benz crafted the beginning of one of the strongest legacies in off road history, the G-Wagon. While most of the world is aware of their true nature, they've been relegated in America as mall crawling status symbols. Follow along as I attempt to shed proper light on this amazing platform, taking this 2010 G550 and unleashing its true potential over an action-packed 7 day build.

Watch this G-Wagon go from stock to complete expedition vehicle in stunning 4K.
