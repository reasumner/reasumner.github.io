MONDAY BLUES KILLER: 1995 Mitsubishi Pajero 2.5L 4x4 - The BEAST Goes Off-Road!! | EvoMalaysia.com

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
THIS WAS SO MUCH FUN! It's been a long time since I've ventured off-road, especially in one of my OWN cars, and having vaguely remembered this location from previously doing a photoshoot for a truck or SUV here (can't even remember what it was) I decided it would make a suitable locale for the first (of hopefully many) off-road shoots, this time with a GoPro instead of a DSLR.

One difference though, this time I wanted to venture further in. This is not to say the other test vehicles I've had here in the past weren't capable, it's just that when it comes to test-cars, I'd rather return them in one piece, and not just return one-piece, #geddit? However, since this Pajero is ALL MINE, I was gonna try, or at least attempt some off-roading, otherwise there was no reason to be here or even buy the damn thing in the first place, right?

We all said and done, as seen in the video, the Pajero performed admirably despite its age, and I was thoroughly impressed! For less than rm15k, I now have myself a bona-fide, proven 4x4 that's not shy to take on some rough-stuff when the opportunity arises. I can't wait to hit the trails again, cos I haven't had this much fun on 4-wheels in a LONG time!

Stay tuned for more to come folks, I'm just getting started ;)
