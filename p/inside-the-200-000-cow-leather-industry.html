Inside the $200,000 Cow Leather Industry

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Ever wondered how raw cow hides are transformed into the soft, luxurious leather used in bags, shoes, jackets, and car seats?

00:00 - Receiving and sorting raw salted cow hides
01:15 - Soaking and liming to remove hair and flesh
03:40 - Fleshing, splitting, and trimming for perfect thickness

08:10 - Drying, conditioning, and softening the leather
10:30 - Dyeing, finishing, embossing, and quality checks
12:45 - Final luxury leather ready for high-end products
