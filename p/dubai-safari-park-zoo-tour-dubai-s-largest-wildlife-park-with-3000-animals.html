Today we're visiting Dubai Safari Park, the largest wildlife park in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

Total Animals: Approximately 3,000

Dubai Safari Park is an eco-friendly safari park located in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

The park has six main areas: African Village, Explorer Village, Asian Village, Kids Farm, Arabian Desert Safari, and Al Wadi.
