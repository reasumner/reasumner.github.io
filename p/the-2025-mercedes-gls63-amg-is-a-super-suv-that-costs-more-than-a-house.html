The 2025 Mercedes GLS63 AMG Is A SUPER SUV That Costs More Than A HOUSE

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Once in a great while you run across a single car solution with mind blowing horsepower numbers, enough seats to haul the entire family, and every option you could ask for. The 2025 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG is one of those vehicles. With AWD, 600 plus horsepower, and a nearly $5,000 audio system it's a super SUV dripping with luxury, and it could be your single car solution... if you're lucky the neighbors won't know the price tag unlike a G-Wagen.
