Do you want to please your guests with Bavarian food (BAKED PORK legs)? Then try to cook pork legs according to this very simple recipe and all the guests will leave You in complete delight! PORK LEGS baked IN the OVEN are very fragrant, delicious and tender! Write down the recipe!
Pork legs (4 PCs) are washed, cleaned and put to cook for 2 hours over medium heat.
Prepare the coating: mix mayonnaise (150 gr) with adzhika (to taste), add garlic (2 cloves), squeeze it out with a garlic chopper. Mix everything together.
This mixture is spread on the legs, which were previously laid out in a baking dish.
Then put in a preheated t=200 gr. oven for 10-15 minutes.
When ready, we take out the pork legs and put them on plates, add sauerkraut, herbs, and pickles to taste (cucumbers and tomatoes). BON APPETIT!
