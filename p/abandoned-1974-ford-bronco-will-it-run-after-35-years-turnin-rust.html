Join in with the Turnin Rust crew as they attempt to bring some life back into this forgotten 1974 Ford Bronco. After uncovering a rat-infested engine, the team digs in and diagnosis what it will take to hear this abandoned jewel up and running again. Will this neglected Ford run again or has nature taken their final claim on this ol' girl?
