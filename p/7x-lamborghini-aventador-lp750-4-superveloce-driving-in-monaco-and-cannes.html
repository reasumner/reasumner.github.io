7x Lamborghini Aventador LP750-4 SuperVeloce driving in Monaco and Cannes !

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
During our holiday in Monaco and Cannes in the summer of 2016 we saw several Lamborghini Aventador LP750-4 SuperVeloce driving around and making some noise. Most of them have the stock exhaust system, but in this video you can also see one with a Capristo Carbon Edition exhaust and the Lamborghini Race exhaust.
