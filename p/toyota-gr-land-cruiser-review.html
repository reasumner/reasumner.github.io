Toyota GR Land Cruiser REVIEW!

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
This is the new Toyota Land Cruiser GR Sport!

It’s an absolutely colossal 7-seater, and is the first brand-new Land Cruiser to be released in 16 years! So has it been worth the wait?

Well when it comes to the design, it’s unmistakably a Land Cruiser, with its wide body and huge grille up front. This GR Sport edition also comes with a few upgrades over the standard edition, with sporty front & rear bumpers coupled with unique alloys and GR badging both outside and in.

Step inside, and the interior is very ‘Toyota’. It’s nothing outlandish, not ugly - it’s all pretty safe. You get a number of physical buttons across the dash, and you’ll find good-quality materials throughout most of the cabin. The build quality is exceptional, and it comes with a touchscreen infotainment screen on the dash. Unfortunately, though, the system isn’t all that good. But thankfully you get Apple CarPlay and Android Auto as standard.

Only three engines are available, with the range-topper being a 3.5-litre twin-turbo V6 that produces 415hp.

The Land Cruiser range starts from just over £52,000, rising all the way up to around £89,000 for this GR Sport edition. But there’s one big catch… We’re not getting it in the UK! The question is, if it were on sale here, would you choose it? Or would you pick a different 7-seater? Let us know in the COMMENTS!
