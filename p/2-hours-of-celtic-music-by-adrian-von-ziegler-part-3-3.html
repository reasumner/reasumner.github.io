I was hoping so much that I could finish this very special video before the end of the year, and I'm so glad I made it.^^ This year has been so painful for so many people, and I truly hope that this can give you all some well deserved relaxation and positive energy.
And honestly thank you so much for everyone who has supported me through all the years, you have no idea what a huge difference you make for me. When I started this channel I was 19, and now this Christmas I will turn 31, which is just insane to me, it's been over 11 years! This will sound cheesy but I don't care, it's just the truth, I owe you guys everything. Because of this channel, which basically means because of you, I quite literally turned from a very depressed and lonely teenager into a happily married man with hope for the future. So when I say thank you, you can't even imagine how deeply I mean those words. Thank you guys, so much.
