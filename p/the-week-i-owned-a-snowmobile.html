The Week I Owned A Snowmobile

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
- Turn on subtitles/CC's if you'd like explanations on what I'm doing throughout the video.
- This is my first snowmobile. Purchased off Facebook Marketplace, it's a 1996 Arctic Cat ZRT 800 with a Thundercat 900 motor sitting in it. The Thundercat 900 was known to be one of the fastest snowmobiles for its time, so I was quickly drawn to it. The seller told me all it needed was a few nuts and bolts to finish the swap, but it turned out it needed a lot more. Once I got it up and running, me and my friend took it a couple hours up to northern Minnesota where the conditions were near perfect for a day of snowmobiling. Unfortunately, I got to ride it for about 25 minutes in total before we stopped to gas up and I left the brake lock on. 5 minutes later, everything under the hood overheated. Flames followed and in seconds, it was too late to do anything. Thus my first snowmobile quickly came to an end. I feel like quite a dope for this beginner's mistake that ruined a nice sled but lesson learned and life moves on. Thanks to the company of a great friend, we thoroughly enjoyed our day still and have yet another story to remember forever. And to boot, this Thundercat 900 engine may live again.
