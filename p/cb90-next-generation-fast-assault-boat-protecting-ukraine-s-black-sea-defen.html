CB90: Next-generation fast assault boat protecting Ukraine's Black Sea defences

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
The CB90, originally developed by Saab for the Swedish Navy, is a fast military assault craft, which can carry up to 20 troops and cargo, in shallow or open waters.

Three of these combat boats have been donated to the Ministry of Defence of Ukraine to help the country with its defences in the Black Sea.
