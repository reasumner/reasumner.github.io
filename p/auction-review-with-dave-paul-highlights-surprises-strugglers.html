AUCTION REVIEW With Dave & Paul - Highlights, Surprises & Strugglers!

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
Join Dave and Paul for a full review of this month’s auction as they walk through all the showrooms and pick out some of the key cars from the sale. They talk through what performed well, where bidding surprised us, and why a few entries struggled to get early interest.

One of the main talking points in this review is reserve strategy. Setting an estimate too high can put buyers off before they even start bidding. Even when a car is genuinely worth its reserve, sometimes you need to attract interest with a lower starting point and let the auction take it up to its true value. Dave and Paul explain how this played out with several cars in the sale.

If you want a clear and honest breakdown of how the auction went, including highlights, insights and lessons for both buyers and sellers, this review covers it all.
