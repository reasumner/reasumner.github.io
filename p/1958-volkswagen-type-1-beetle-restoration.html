1958 Volkswagen Type 1 Beetle Restoration

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
Join us as we bring new life to a classic 1958 Volkswagen Type 1 Beetle, one of the most beloved and recognizable cars ever built. In this restoration project, we take a Beetle that has spent decades in storage and carefully return it to its former glory. Classic oval-window era styling cues transitioning into later designs

Distinctive chrome bumpers and trim
Minimalist interior with vintage German simplicity
Lightweight body and unforgettable driving character
