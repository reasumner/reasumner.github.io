So STUCK we needed 2 winches! 10KM in 3 DAYS to NT Fishing & Camping PARADISE!

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Graham MIGHT have sent it a little too hard... Join part 1 of our EPIC trip to the Northern Territory! Up Savannah Way, through to Borroloola and to the untouched wilderness of Lorella Springs! Floods, intense muddy bog holes & a snapped chassis try to stop us - but we still manage a remote camping & 4WD touring trip we'll NEVER forget!

Join Shaun Whale in the Dirty 30, Graham in the D-MAX, Tim from MITS Alloy in
his 79 series Landcruiser and Reuben in the DMW chopped Y62 Nissan Patrol for one incredible offroad adventure in the northern territory!
