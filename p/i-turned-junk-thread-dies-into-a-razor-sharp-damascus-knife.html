I Turned Junk Thread Dies into a RAZOR-SHARP Damascus Knife

#DamascusKnife #KnifeMaking #Forging #Bladesmithing #ThreadDie #DIY #Metalworking #CustomKnife #ToolSteel #Satisfying

In this build, I'm tackling a true craftsman's challenge: forging Damascus steel from hardened thread-rolling dies (likely D2 tool steel). I combined them with 15N20 high-nickel steel to create a dramatic contrast in the final pattern. You'll see the entire process, from the risky forge welding at over 2300¬∞F, to drawing and twisting the billet to develop the pattern, all the way to the final grinding, polishing, and etching that brings the beautiful Damascus waves to life. The result is a unique, fully functional piece of art with an incredible story, proving that beauty can be forged from the most industrial of materials.

If you enjoyed watching this transformation, please like the video and subscribe to the channel for more bladesmithing projects and metal recycling challenges! Let me know in the comments what other unusual materials you'd like to see me forge into a knife next.
