Published at : 12 Dec 2025
This video details the process of making a cart from used drums in a traditional mechanic's workshop in Pakistan. Through the skilled hands of Pakistani workers, discarded materials are recycled into strong and functional work tools. Pakistani industry, particularly small-scale Pakistani manufacturing, has a way of creating a variety of work tools using simple techniques yet producing extraordinary results. Made in Pakistan with minimal equipment, this process demonstrates the creativity of Pakistani workshops in transforming used materials into valuable new products. This is a true glimpse into how the manufacturing process works and how local innovation is developing in Pakistani industry. @TopWorks
