Watch over 100 different US Army trucks travel in convoys to participate in Exercise Saber Junction at the Hohenfels Training Area in Germany. Featuring several Stryker Infantry Carrier Vehicles, M-ATV Mine Resistent Ambush Protected units, Joint Light Tactical Vehicles, Humvees, Medium Tactical Vehicles and Heavy Expanded Mobility Tactical Trucks of many different variants. And, British Army Land Rover Revised Weapons Mounted Installation Kit, MAN HX60 Logistic Support Vehicles and a Pinzgauer utility vehicle.

Exercise Saber Junction is a large-scale US Army-led exercise held by the 7th Army Training Command at the US Army Bavaria garrison in Germany. The primary objective is to test the ability of US Army forces based in Europe, such as the 2nd Cavalry Regiment at Vilseck, and participating allies. Also featured is the 1st Armored Division Combat Aviation Brigade, 501st Aviation Regiment from Fort Bliss, Texas, who have been deployed in theatre. And additionally, the 18th Military Police Brigade, 709th Military Police Battalion based at Vilseck, and the British Army.
