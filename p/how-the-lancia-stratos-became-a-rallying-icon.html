How the Lancia Stratos Became a Rallying Icon

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
The world's very first road car designed to rally, the Lancia Stratos's legendary status is assured. Delivering Lancia three consecutive World Rally Championship titles in the 1970s, today it's the weapon of choice for 2024 British Historic Rally champion Seb Perez. He shows us around the car he will use on this year's Roger Albert Clark Rally.
