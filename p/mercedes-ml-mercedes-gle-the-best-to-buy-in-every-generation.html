Mercedes ML / Mercedes GLE - the best to buy in every generation

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
The Mercedes ML became the Mercedes GLE in 2015, and generations include the W163, W164, W166, and W167. Made from 1997 and with engines from the M133 V8 and OM612 diesel in the first generation, to the m273 AND M156 AMG engine in the second generation accompanied by the OM642 diesel there was plenty of choice. Add in the third generation M276 V6 and.M157 5.5 V8 aside the diesel OM642 and OM641 and finally the fourth generation with the M256 mild hybrid, and OM256 plug-in hybrid nearly matching the acceleration of the GLE63 but with efficiency closer to the OM656 diesel and OM654 diesel and the choice gets even tougher Avoid buying a broken Mercedes ML or GLE in any generation. Find the Mercedes ML that can go off-road and on-road without breaking the bank. Avoid the mistakes of buying a broken Mercedes GLE as a project and attempting to fix a broken Mercedes GLE or ML. This buyer's guide covers the W163, W164, W166, and W167 generation. Owners of ML and GLE AMG vehicles may also find our guide helpful for its list of things to look out for including how reliable the car is.

The reliability of a Mercedes GLE and Mercedes ML can be a never ending discussion but with this guide you'll have a better chance of having one with few common faults.
