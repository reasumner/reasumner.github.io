Ford Ranger Raptor vs. Toyota Hilux GR-S: High-performance Pickup Big Test | Top Gear Philippines

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
The Ford Ranger Raptor has stood alone in its segment for years since its arrival. But a few years ago, Toyota dipped its toes in the high-performance pickup market with the Hilux GR-S. Now, after a long wait, we were finally able to take it out for a proper Big Test.

In this episode, we take both pickups through the usual tests and see which one is worth the premium pickup money. Will the Hilux GR-S dethrone the mighty Ranger Raptor? Or will Ford remain the pickup to beat? Watch the full video to learn more.
