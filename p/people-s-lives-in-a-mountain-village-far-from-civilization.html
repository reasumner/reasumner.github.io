People's lives in a mountain village Far from Civilization.

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Hello everyone! I am Mykhailo, abbreviated as Misha, a resident of the "Wild Forests of the Carpathians" and this is my daughter Evelina with whom I visited Aunt Galina. In this documentary I will reveal more about my lifestyle in remote mountain villages, my way of life, and show you wild mountain berries, flowers, and legumes. If you like my videos, please continue to subscribe to my channel. I will continue to share my life in the Wild Forests of the Carpathians, far from Civilization. I wish you a happy life and enjoy delicious food every day.
Feel free to share this video with your friends or on social media.
