The 2025 Mercedes-AMG GLS 63 is a luxury performance SUV that combines high-performance engineering and luxury. It features a handcrafted AMG 4.0-liter biturbo V8 engine, a 48-volt hybrid system, and a 48-volt hybrid system, producing 603 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. The SUV has an AMG-specific grille, sleek LED headlights, and muscular contours, showcasing sports-car agility. The GLS 63 rides on standard 21-inch AMG 10-spoke wheels, with the option to upgrade to 23-inch forged wheels for a more assertive look.

The interior offers a sanctuary of luxury and advanced technology, with exclusive Nappa leather upholstery and a 64-color LED ambient lighting system. The spacious interior can accommodate up to seven passengers across three rows, with an optional six-seat configuration featuring captain's chairs for the second row. The Mercedes-Benz User Experience (MBUX) infotainment system, wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration, and the Burmester Surround Sound System cater to various entertainment needs.

In terms of performance, the GLS 63 is equipped with an AMG SPEEDSHIFT TCT 9-speed automatic transmission, AMG Performance 4MATIC+ all-wheel drive system, and AMG ACTIVE RIDE CONTROL suspension. Safety and driver assistance are paramount, with standard features like Active Brake Assist, Active Distance Assist DISTRONIC, and Active Steering Assist. The SUV also comes with the Surround View System for 360-degree views and a towing capacity of up to 7,700 pounds.

Fuel efficiency is good, with an estimated 14 mpg city and 18 mpg highway. Mercedes-AMG offers customization options, including exterior colors, interior trim, and optional packages like the AMG Night Package. The 2025 Mercedes-AMG GLS 63 sets the benchmark in the luxury performance SUV market, combining power, advanced technology, and unmatched comfort.
