NEW 2024 Mercedes CLE Coupe - Interior and Exterior Walkaround

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
The new 2024 Mercedes CLE Coupe is a captivating fusion of timeless elegance reminiscent of classic models like the W123 and the modern sophistication characteristic of Mercedes-Benz. Positioned as a standalone model line, the CLE aims to bridge the gap between the C-Class and E-Class coupes, delivering a unique blend of dimensions and powertrains. All combustion models incorporate Mercedes EQ Boost mild hybrid technology, providing additional torque for a smoother power delivery. On the road, the Mercedes CLE distinguishes itself as a comfortable cruiser rather than a sports car. The four-wheel steering enhances maneuverability, but the CLE prioritizes a relaxed driving experience, with little emphasis on cornering dynamics. Inside, the CLE showcases a dashboard borrowed from the C-Class, featuring an 11.9-inch touchscreen that dominates the center console. The touchscreen controls major functions, including HVAC, and though lacking physical controls, it stands out as well-designed and user-friendly. The 12.3-inch driver display offers clear visibility, complemented by big, supportive front seats with ample adjustment options. The rear seats, while less spacious, accommodate a six-footer comfortably. The 420-liter boot provides practical storage, making the CLE suitable for both daily use and occasional getaways.

Engine: 2.0 L, 197 hp, 145 kW, 440 Nm
Top Speed: 238 km/h
0-100 km/h: 7.5 sec
Color: High-tech Silver
