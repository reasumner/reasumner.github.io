Investment in luxury condo Park Origin Ratchathewi Bangkok

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
Park Origin Ratchathewi is a condo project developed by Origin Property Public Company Limited, Park Origin Ratchathewi has 41 floors and contains 10 total units, Units range from 1 bedroom to 2 bedroom. Park Origin Ratchathewi at Thung Phaya Thai, Ratchathewi has the following facilities: Swimming pool, Gym, Security, Car park and Office.
Local amenities include Transportation close to public transport stations of BTS Ratchathewi.

Surrounding area
Park Origin Ratchathewi is conveniently located just 70 m from 7-Eleven, which can be reached within 1 min. by foot.

There are many other shops situated in the area of Park Origin Ratchathewi, the 3 closest being:
- Subin is 200 m away (about 3 mins. by foot)
- Siamchai, Banthat Thong Branch is 270 m away (about 3 mins. by foot)
- Good Noodle Company Limited is 280 m away (about 4 mins. by foot)

The surrounding area, where Park Origin Ratchathewi is situated, offers a wide variety of restaurants. See the closest of them below:
- Tokyo Lunch Box is 0 m away (a walk of about 1 min.)
- Kanom Jeen Je Cheei is 30 m away (a walk of about 1 min.)
- King Phet Restaurant (Perb Phitsadan) is 70 m away (a walk of about 1 min.)

Phetchaburi Sip Medical Clinic is the nearest health facility, which is 170 m away (you can walk there in 2 mins.) from the project, while the closest schools in the proximity of Park Origin Ratchathewi are:
- Kingphet School is 60 m away (about 1 min. by foot)
- Siam Singapore International School is 50 m away (about 1 min. by foot)
- Wiitthayalai Khru Phet Buri Demonstration School is 80 m away (about 1 min. by foot)

Suvarnabhumi Airport is located 31.7 km from Park Origin Ratchathewi and it takes about 33 mins. by car or taxi (depending on the traffic) to get there.
