Published at : 12 Dec 2025
This video shows the repair process of a Pakistani truck axle that was severely damaged due to overloading. In a Pakistani workshop, Pakistani workers and local mechanics demonstrate how they disassemble the axle, inspect the internals, and meticulously repair it. This is a glimpse into the hard work of Pakistani industry, from restoring precision components to the crucial role of Pakistani lathes in finishing worn parts.
