EICMA 2025: Moto Guzzi

This is my video from the Moto Guzzi stand at EICMA 2025. Nothing new, but I do love the bokes so thought I'd show you around anyway! The V85TT's are looking fabulous in the red and the green, and the V7's too (except for the LCD dash... I know it's more complicated than what I'd like, but if BSA can put analogue clocks on a Goldstar, could Guzzi do the same on the V7's? Even a modern matt, round dash that looks like a classic clock would be better than the grey LCD, which looks a bit 1990's calculator...)

Apologies, lost myself for a moment then! I do still think the V7's are great bikes, but in my opinion, the dash needs work.

I also take a look at the V100 Mandello S and lastly at the Selvio Duecento Tributo which I think looks fabulous!

More information is available here:
https://www.motoguzzi.com/
