Draining the Radiator Coolant in a Volkswagen Golf Mk5

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Draining and refilling the radiator coolant in a 2004 to 2007 Volkswagen Golf Mark 5. If you drain the coolant to carry out work on the radiator or another part connected with the cooling system then you can refill the old coolant back into the system, providing it is clean and free from any contaminants. Make sure that the container that you catch the old coolant in is spotlessly clean.
According to the manual, the coolant capacities are:
1.4 litre non-FSi engines - 5.6 litres
1.4 litre FSi engines - 3.2 litres
1.6 litre SOHC engines - 8.0 litres
1.6 litre FSi engines - 5.6 litres
2.0 litre engines - 8.0 litres
