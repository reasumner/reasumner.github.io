6 Simple Snowflakes - How to make Snowflake out of Paper - Christmas Decorations

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Tutorial on how to make snowflakes out of paper. Easy to DIY Christmas decorations. Simple Snowflake using letter paper or A4 paper, this allows for easy cutting.

On my youtube channel you can find a lot of paper crafts ideas. I'll show you how to make great decorations and gifts out of paper for different occasions (Christmas, Easter, Fall , Halloween, Mother's Day, Birthdays, etc.)
Most of the ideas are easy and no special tools needed - perfect for a tight budget.

Thanks for watching my video.Hope you'll enjoy this craft. :)
