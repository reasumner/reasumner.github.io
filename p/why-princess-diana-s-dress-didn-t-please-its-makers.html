Why Princess Diana's dress didn't please its makers

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
The designer of Princess Diana's wedding dress was "horrified" when she saw it. Lady Diana Spencer's wedding dress is one of the most iconic fashion looks in history.It was created by designers Elizabeth and David Emmanuel. The dress took months to prepare. The dress reportedly cost around ¬£9,000 and consisted of six different fabrics, classic puffed sleeves and a total of more than 10,000 pearls. There was 23 metres of silk taffeta, 90 metres of tulle crinoline and 137 metres of netting for the veil.It was followed by a 7.5 metre hand-embroidered silk train. During an interview with ITV, Elizabeth Emmanuel said: "We knew it would wrinkle a bit, but when I saw it arrive at St Paul's Cathedral and we saw the pleats, I actually felt faint. I was horrified because it had quite a few pleats on it.It was a lot more than we thought it would be." They had a dress rehearsal beforehand but used a different material, so it was nowhere near as crumpled as it looked on the day.
