Crispy Potato French Fries Recipe | Easy Potato French Fries | Potato Snacks Recipe

Ingredients :

Egg french fries
Potato 3 medium size
Wash well
Dry on paper towels
2 eggs
Salt 1/2 tsp
Chili powder 1 tsp
Garlic 1/2 tsp
Oregano 1/4 tsp
Mix well
All purpose flour 1/2 cup
Hot oil
Let it fry for 5 mins (meanwhile do not touch it)
Separate the fries
Fry for 10-12 mins until golden brown low flame
