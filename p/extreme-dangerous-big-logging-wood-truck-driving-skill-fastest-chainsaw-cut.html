The sheer size and weight of these trucks demand skilled operators who possess expertise in maneuvering through challenging terrain while ensuring safety and efficiency. Despite the inherent risks, wood logging trucks are indispensable assets in the timber industry, facilitating the transportation of timber crucial for various global industries.
