Harvesting Winter Wheat & Planting Double Crop Soybeans | Darke County Ohio

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
In this video I am out in the field with a farm in Darke County Ohio as they harvest their 2025 winter wheat crop in early July. After the wheat is harvested they are no-till planting soybeans into the harvested field.

They are harvesting the wheat with their Claas Lexion 740 combine with a Claas Convio draper head. Running in the field with the combine is their Fendt 1038 Tractor pulling a J&M X1112 grain cart. I also go along as they haul a load of wheat back to their grain bins.

Later on in the video I show them planting double crop soybeans with their Challenger 1042 Tractor pulling a Fendt Momentum 36 row planter.

I filmed this in early July 2025 and the first field is near Eldora Speedway near New Weston Ohio. I recently uploaded a video of this same farm and equipment harvesting corn and if you have not seen that video I'll have the link to it at the end of this one.
