Life Million of Years Ago | We Are Alien Experiments

This feature-length silent cinematic compilation explores Human Evolution across millions of years, weaving together scenes of early hominins on the African savanna, mysterious visitors from the stars, and giant prehistoric creatures. From the first uncertain steps of our ancestors to terrifying encounters with towering predators and colossal chickens, every moment turns survival, hunger, and curiosity into a raw visual study of Human Evolution under extreme pressure.

Without dialogue or narration, the film invites viewers to watch behavior, posture, and reaction the way a field scientist might, following desperate escapes, risky hunts, and strange alien experiments that may have shaped Human Evolution. Each chapter becomes a living timeline of Human Evolution, blending speculative alien intervention with the harsh reality of prehistoric life, so that by the end, every frame feels like a wordless record of Human Evolution written in fear, adaptation, and resilience.
