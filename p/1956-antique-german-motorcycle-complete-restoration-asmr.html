1956 Antique German Motorcycle Complete Restoration ASMR

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
This video worth your time. This is a Zundapp Motorcycle Back from 1956. Zündapp was a major German motorcycle manufacturer founded in 1917 in Nuremberg by Fritz Neumeyer.
This is Zundapp 433 Combinette Moped Pedal Bike. It is a Barn find, the complete restoration video took about 3 months to be done I hope you will enjoy this effort.
