Soviet Admirals Were Horrified to See WWII Battleships Return with Nuclear

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
In the 1980s, the US Navy did the unthinkable: they reactivated World War II-era Iowa-class battleships. Soviet Admirals initially mocked these "museum pieces" as obsolete targets. They were wrong.

This documentary reveals the classified strategy behind the return of the USS New Jersey and the terrifying secret hidden inside the armored boxes on her deck. Discover how a 40-year-old ship became a nuclear nightmare that the Soviet Union couldn't solve, couldn't sink, and couldn't afford to counter.
