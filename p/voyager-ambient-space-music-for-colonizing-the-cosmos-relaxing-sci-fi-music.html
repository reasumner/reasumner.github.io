They call us The Great Heroes. Space faring colonizers sent to expand the diplomatic reach of our honourable Earth among the stars. Ever since I was a child I idolized The Great Heroes that went before us. Like superstars they would board the ship of their launch year with the rest of their crew, waving to an overjoyed barrage of cameras and reportermen. I never thought I would get the chance to follow in their footsteps, yet here I am. I said farewell to my crewmates just three suns ago. Five members, sent to pave the way for future colonization on five of the most habitable planets in the galaxy. If my efforts prove fruitful (and the planet survivable), I may receive colony members within three to five Earth years. Until then I build, forage and research. Doing everything in my power to establish a veritable home for our kind. This is my duty, my calling. For I, am A Voyager.
