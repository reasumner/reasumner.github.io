A remote mountain forest cabin far from civilization. The hard life of my ancestors.

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
In this video I will show you the remote forest house of my grandfather's sister. Grandfather asked me to visit this house and clean it if possible, since no one has been there for more than 20 years.
It's autumn in the yard, and my grandfather told me that the chance of meeting a bear is very high, so I should be careful in general, bears roam in our places, I often see tracks on the ground and fur on trees. My grandfather's sister's forest house is about 15 kilometers from my grandfather's house. Earlier, as a child, my grandfather drove me twice so that I would remember the road. And I myself wanted to visit this house far away in the mountains, once sheep, cows, horses grazed there, it was fun. I took "Rocky" and "Tom" with me and in a few hours I reached the forest house. The house, I thought, needed cleaning, first I lit a fire, because bears live in that forest, and the smoke and crackle of the fire scares off predators such as wolves and the bear itself, whose tracks I noticed at the beginning of my hike. While I was cleaning the house, I went down to the stream, as I know that there are trout swimming there in the fall, and I made no mistake when I went down, I caught a trout that I caught to fry. In total, I stayed in this house for a day and a half, of course I spent the night and lit the stove, which was working, but I did not freeze at night, as it was warm and comfortable. It was -3 degrees of frost outside in the morning. Everything I didn't tell you, you will see in the video, of course grandpa will be happy if I show him his sister's house filmed on video, and also grandpa will be happy about your feedback, comments and subscriptions.
