Learn to make the right RUNS as a WINGER!

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
In this video you will learn how to play as an inverted winger. Very detailed tactical and technical analysis of an inverted winger in soccer. Winger positioning and movement is incredibly important especially if you want to play as an inverted winger! Knowing how to position yourself as a winger will change the way you play as a winger. Become the best winger in the world and dominate the flank with my tactical analysis of a winger positioning in soccer. Inverted winger runs will allow your fullbacks to overlap and you will be outnumbering the opposition as well as you will be stretching the opposition centrally and defensively. You will be able to find all this in this video.
