It’s time for a Germany vs USA showdown!

We’ve got Mat in the incredible Mercedes-AMG G63, and it’s going up against the absolutely huge Ford F-150 Raptor R! So, can German power take down American power? Let’s check out the stats.

The G63 is powered by a 4-litre twin-turbo V8 that produces 585hp and 850Nm of torque. Power is sent to all four wheels via a 9-speed automatic gearbox, and it tips the scales at 2,550kg. As new, it costs around £185,000.

Next up, we have the F-150 Raptor R. It’s packing a 5.2-litre supercharged V8 that creates 720hp and 868Nm. Power is sent to all four wheels via a 10-speed auto, and it tips the scales at 2,699kg. If you’re looking to buy one in the UK, it’ll cost around £168,000.

So will the extra weight prove to be the undoing of the F-150? There’s only one way to find out… LET’S RACE!
