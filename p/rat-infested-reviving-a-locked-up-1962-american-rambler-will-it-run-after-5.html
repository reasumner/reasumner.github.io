RAT INFESTED: Reviving A Locked Up 1962 American Rambler | Will It Run After 50 Years? | Turnin Rust

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
After stumbling across an abandoned 1962 American AMC Rambler left for dead in an overgrown field, the guys take a gamble and see if there is any life left in this ol' jewel. Upon further investigation, they find one of the biggest rats nest under the hood and attempt to clean it all up and dig into the challenge. Determined to see this thing run after 50 years, the team fights through a locked-up engine, chewed up wires, and pounds of rat poop in hopes to see this dead classic brought back to life.

This video is a compilation of a 3-part series we did on our Restored channel. We've pieced together the content to show a full start to finish rebuild. We hope you enjoy the video!
