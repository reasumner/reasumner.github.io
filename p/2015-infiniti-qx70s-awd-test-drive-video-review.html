2015 Infiniti QX70S AWD Test Drive Video Review

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
http://www.autobytel.com/infiniti/qx70/2015/?id=32972 The Infiniti QX70 (formerly known as the FX) still holds a unique spot among luxury SUVs. The base model starts at $45,850 but the model tested here was equipped with AWD, Technology Package, Premium Package, and the Sport Package which brings the as-tested-price up to $59,095.

Under the hood is a 3.7 liter V-6 engine that makes 325 horsepower and 267 lb-ft. of torque. The transmission is a 7-speed automatic that features down-shift rev-matching. The AWD model is rated at 16 MPG in the city and 22 MPG on the highway.
