The Mercedes-Benz has been a strong seller from 2010 until 2015! Now today its's know under the name GLC. The added option could make this one expensive! But give it a few kilometers and add a few years and down goes the price! It was powered by a 3.5-liter V6 with an output of 268 horsepower and 258 pound-feet of torque. A seven-speed automatic transmission with Comfort, Sport, and Manual settings is standard. Rear-wheel drive is standard, and all-wheel drive (known as 4Matic) is available and with it, it becomes an off-road warrior! Even if a few percentages of the buyers will bring it into the wild! It might not be a class-leading cabin space or fuel mileage king, but it sure is a robust and capable SUV!
