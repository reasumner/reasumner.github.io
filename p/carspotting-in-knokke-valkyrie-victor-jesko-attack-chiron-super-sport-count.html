Carspotting in Knokke - Valkyrie, Victor, Jesko Attack, Chiron Super Sport, Countach, Ferrari F40

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
We visited Knokke a couple weeks ago to attend the 2025 Zoute Grand Prix. During this weekend many supercars and sportscars will drive on the streets of Knokke. We saw a lot of awesome cars including a Veyron Grand Sport Vitesse, GT3 Rennsport, Ford GT, Ferrari F40, Porsche Carrera GT and much more. In this video you will see some of the cars that we filmed!
