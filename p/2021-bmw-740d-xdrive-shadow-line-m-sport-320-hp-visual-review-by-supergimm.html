2021 BMW 740d xDrive Shadow Line M sport (320 hp) - Visual Review by Supergimm

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
2021 Bmw 740d xDrive Shadow Line M sport with 320 hp in Black Sapphire color.This 7 series has almost anything you could wish for and trully is luxury limusine!
It also has Exclusive Nappa leather with extended upholstery and black- Mocha stitchin in the interior..The price for this car (brand new)is arround 166.000 eur.


Bmw 740d xDrive Shadow Line M sport specs:
Engine: 2993 cm3,Diesel,turbocharged Inline 6 cylinder engine
Power: 235 kw / 320 hp @ 4000 rpm
Transmission: All wheel drive (xDrive) with a 8 speed Sequential Automatic gearbox
Torque: 680 Nm or 501 lb.ft @ 1750 rpm
Acceleration 0-100 km/h: 5.3 s
Top speed: 250 km/h or 155 Mph
Fuel consumption-combined: 5.6 L/100km or 42 mpg US
Curb weight: 1890 kg or 4167 lbs
Trunk-boot space: 515 L
Fuel Tank Capacity: 78 L or 20.6 US gallons
CO2 emissions: 148 g/Km (BMW)
