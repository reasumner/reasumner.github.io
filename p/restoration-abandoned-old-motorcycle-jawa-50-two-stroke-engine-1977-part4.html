Restoration Abandoned Old Motorcycle Jawa 50 two stroke engine 1977 - PART4

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
PART4
Yes it's done! The motorbike works! Plus, it looks better than new! The Restore Machines team has done the final part of the renovation of the 45-year-old Jawa 50 motorcycle. This time you will see the complete assembly of the renovated parts and testing of the motorcycle. We gradually repaired all parts and replaced some with new ones. After 7 months of work, Restore Machines restored the old Czech-Slovak motorcycle to its original appearance. Of course, you will see a few interesting specialties in the video as always. @Restore Machines wishes you a pleasant viewing experience.
