What I found at the scrap metal yard – you won't believe it!

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
I went to a huge scrap metal yard and found incredible things! A Soviet-era bronze horse weighing several kilograms, a vintage meat grinder in perfect condition, copper coils, electronic boards with precious metals, and many other finds.

In this video, I'll show you how to search for valuable items among scrap metal. Many people throw away items that can be sold to collectors, turned in for precious metals, or used as vintage decor. Aluminum radiators, copper wire, Soviet figurines, old electronics — all of this has value.

Main finds: a bronze horse statue weighing several kilograms, a decorative box with a lion, a vintage oven door with a USSR star, electronic boards with gold and palladium, copper coils from motors, a Soviet meat grinder in excellent condition.

I show how to determine the value of metal, what can be taken for recycling, and what can be sold at a flea market. I talk about precious metals in electronics, the value of bronze and brass, and why Soviet items are popular among collectors.

If you're interested in finds at dumps, treasure hunting in scrap metal, or just want to know what valuable things can be found among trash — this video is for you. Subscribe to the channel, like it, and write in the comments what you would take from this dump!
