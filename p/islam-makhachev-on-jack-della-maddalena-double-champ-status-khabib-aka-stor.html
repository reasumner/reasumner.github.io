Islam Makhachev on Jack Della Maddalena, double champ status, Khabib & AKA stories | Daniel Cormier

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
Islam Makhachev IS BACK on the Daniel Cormier Check-In series ahead of his MASSIVE UFC 322 FIGHT at Madison Square Garden in New York City, where he looks to become a champion in two weight classes against Jack Della Maddalena. Islam talks to DC about training with Khabib Nurmagomedov and his most recent camp in preparation for Jack Della. Makhachev reveals what he needs to do to beat JDM, and how he'll achieve double champ status. Plus, don't miss DC and Islam talk about their training sessions, share stories from American Kickboxing Academy, and where Islam ranks against Khabib Nurmagomedov, Cain Velasquez, and some of the greatest fighters from AKA.
