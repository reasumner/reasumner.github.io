BMW E28/E30 Plastic Trim Restoration & Chrome polish || Garage Build PT6

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Quick video of all the exterior trim getting a cleaning/repaint/polish. I even went ahead and applied clear coat to the taillight lenses to bring back a shine. Using a sealant even seemed to fill in a few of the small brittle cracks known as spider cracks or crazing. But overall it worked great on the taillights and the fog lights.
Door trim, hood trim, and Spoiler look fantastic. SEM Trim Black really works great on brittle as well as porous plastic and rubber; this paint acts more like a dye and just gets absorbed by whatever your painting.
Headlight trim wasn't restored since Im going to be converting to euro headlights very very soon.
