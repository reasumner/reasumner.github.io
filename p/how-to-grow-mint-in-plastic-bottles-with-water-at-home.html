Peppermint is a very healthy spice and it is often present in your family's meals. Peppermint has the effect of stimulating digestion, reducing fat, stimulating taste...
Growing mint at home is very easy with a plastic bottle combined with automatic watering, you will not have to worry about the plant being short of water.
To increase survival, you use mint stem to soak in water after 5 days for it to take root, then you plant it in the ground, after 1 month you can harvest mint as a seasoning for meals.
