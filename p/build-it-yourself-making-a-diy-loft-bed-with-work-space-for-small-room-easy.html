Build it Yourself! Making a DIY LOFT BED with Work space for Small Room - Easy and Budget

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
In this video I will show you How to make a simple and sturdy space saving Loft Bed using simple tools! Check the complete list of materials on the description below.

Small room?? Eto ang space saving Loft bed para sa mga gusto mag-DIY. Paano gumawa ng loft bed na matibay! with list of materials

Loft bed gives extra space for small rooms, a perfect space saver!!
Great setup for gaming Room, work from home and online school!

#DIYTatayDan #DIYLoftBed #HowtomakeLoftBed

Room Detail
Room Area- 4m2
Ceiling height - 2.4m
Bed size - single Bed
