Concorso d'Eleganza Villa d'Este 2025 - Sunday Street Parade - OSI Silver Fox Prototype, F1 GTR, F40

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
On Sunday, 25 May 2025, I was present in Cernobbio to watch the parade of cars of the Concorso d'Eleganza Villa d'Este, which, like every year, travels along a short stretch of road that leads it to Villa Erba. This is the only opportunity, free for the public, to see the splendid cars in motion during the road transfer and therefore be able to hear the sound of their engines. As always, the level of the vehicles in the competition is very high. This year, the Alfa Romeo Tipo B P3 triumphed as "Best of Show", the 1934 Grand Prix single-seater that at the time was managed in the race by Enzo Ferrari's Scuderia. Many wonderful Ferraris, including the Dino 206 S, the 121 LM Scaglietti, the 375 MM, but also amazing models of other famous Italian brands such as the Lancia Astura Convertible Pinin Farina and the Alfa Romeo 8C 2500 SS with bodywork by Touring. Impressive was the section dedicated to the most recent supercars, including McLaren F1 GTR, Mercedes-Benz CLK GTR Roadster, Ferrari F40 GTE, and Maserati MC12 Corsa, to name a few. My heart was indeed stolen by the extravagant, nay, shocking 1967 OSI Silver Fox Prototype with a shape decidedly inspired by a catamaran. Enjoy! üòâ
