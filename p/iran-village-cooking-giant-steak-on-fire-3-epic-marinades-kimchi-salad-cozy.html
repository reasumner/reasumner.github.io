Tomahawk Steak on Fire | Three Marinades, Kimchi Salad & Cozy Outdoor Cooking

In a calm and peaceful countryside setting, where fire crackles gently and flavors take their time, a bold and satisfying dinner is prepared under the open sky.

In this episode, giant Tomahawk steaks are brought to the fire and seasoned in three unique and rich marinades. One steak is flavored with fresh tomato and Mexican-style spices, another is marinated in a tangy pomegranate paste and soy sauce, and the third is coated in a creamy milk and cream sauce for a soft and indulgent taste.

All spices are hand-ground in a traditional stone mortar, releasing deep aromas before the steaks are grilled over open fire and finished slowly in the oven for perfect tenderness.

Alongside the steaks, a famous and flavorful Kimchi salad is freshly prepared, adding spice and crunch to the meal. Mushrooms and onions are breaded and fried the traditional way, golden and crispy.

To finish on a warm seasonal note, an autumn cauliflower dessert is cooked and served with a milk and cream sauce, bringing comfort and balance to the feast.

Everything is enjoyed together in a peaceful outdoor atmosphere, where fire, food, and calm moments create a truly satisfying experience.
