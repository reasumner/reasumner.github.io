The 2026 Toyota Grand Highlander Platinum Is WORTH Your Consideration

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
The 2025 Toyota Grand Highlander is a midsize three-row SUV that stands out for its spaciousness and versatility. It is designed to compete with other popular three-row SUVs such as the Kia Telluride, Honda Pilot, and Ford Explorer. The Grand Highlander offers a hybrid option for those seeking improved fuel efficiency, and it comes with several new features, including a 12.3-inch multimedia display, Traffic Jam Assist, and a Digital Rearview Mirror.

The 2025 Grand Highlander is available in three trim levels: XLE, Limited, and Platinum. Each trim level comes with a 2.4-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 265 horsepower and is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Platinum trim also offers a 362-horsepower MAX Hybrid powertrain, which includes a 0-60 mph time of 6.3 seconds and a towing capacity of 5,000 lbs.
