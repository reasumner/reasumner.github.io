Lonely 92 year old grandfather lives out his life in the mountains far from civilization

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
In this film, we will show you the isolated life of Vasyl, a lonely 92-year-old grandfather, living out his years in the remote Carpathian village of Kosmach, high in the Ukrainian mountains - far from cities, roads and the noise of modern life.
With no family around and no one to visit him, he survives only with his memories, a small hut and the silence of nature. His days are slow, marked by chopping wood, cooking on an old stove and sitting quietly by the window, watching the wind rustle in the trees. There is no internet, no heating, no comfort - just raw life, as it was lived a hundred years ago.
This is not a romantic escape. This is a true story of human resilience and loneliness. It is about what it means to grow old in a place where no one comes anymore. Where time stands still, and where every breath is a small act of strength.
