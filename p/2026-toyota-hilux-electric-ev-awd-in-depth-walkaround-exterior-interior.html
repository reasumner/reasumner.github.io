The ninth-generation Hilux was introduced on 10 November 2025 simultaneously in Thailand, Europe and Australia. The exterior was designed by Toyota Australia. In Thailand, this hilux is called Hilux TRAVO. this variant is a 2026 Toyota Hilux TRAVO-e Double Cab with All-wheel drive.

This 2026 Toyota Hilux Travo-e is a electric pick-up truck (EV) which is the first time ever for Hilux. it come with two synchronous electric motors (front-rear) make it all-wheel drive (AWD) total power output of 196 PS (144kW) and additional of 59.2 kwh Lithium-ion battery capable to do 315 km (NEDC) in one fully charged.

Price - 1,491,000 Baht (TH) or around 45,800 US Dollar
Color - Platinum White Pearl
