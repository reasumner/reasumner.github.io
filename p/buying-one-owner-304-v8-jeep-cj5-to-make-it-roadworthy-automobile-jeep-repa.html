Buying One Owner 304 V8 Jeep CJ5 To Make It Roadworthy #automobile #jeep #repair

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
This 1976 Jeep CJ5 popped into my line of sight long before I even starter turning wrenches. It was one of those cars you would see growing up in a small town from time to time. Well, 25 years later I finally got to call it mine. The old jeep of course put up a fight. From a completely locked up motor, transmission/clutch issues and of course ZERO brakes. It's all the classic ran when parked stuff you have to do to really get a rig back on the road.
Join me on the adventure to get this rusty Jeep CJ5 rolling again.
