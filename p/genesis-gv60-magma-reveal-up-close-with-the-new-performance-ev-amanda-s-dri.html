Genesis GV60 Magma Reveal — Up Close With the New Performance EV | Amanda’s Drive Vibes

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Automotive journalist Amanda Cline from HotCars tests out the latest SUV from Genesis.

“I spent the day watching Genesis peel back the curtain on the GV60 Magma, and it’s immediately clear this isn’t just a quicker GV60. This is the car that sets the tone for every future Genesis performance model. It still carries the elegance the brand is known for, but beneath that smooth exterior is a sharper, more emotional driving feel, directly from the Magma development team. They kept emphasizing the idea of “effortless performance,” and after riding shotgun and digging into the details with the engineers, that description makes complete sense.”
