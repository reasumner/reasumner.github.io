UK Wheat Harvest - Grain Storage & Tillage at AgReserves Farms | 3x John Deere X9 1100 - 9RX 640 +

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
In this video we visit AgReserves Ltd UK in Cambridgeshire during a busy harvest day in August. This is a 25,000-acre modern well- equipped farming operation. There machinery fleet consists of 3 John Deere X9 combines, 4 John Deere 9RX 640, two JD 8RX 410 and many 6R series tractors with Bailey trailers.

Timestamps:
Combining with the 3 X9's
Grain storage with JCB telehandlers
Primery Tillage with a 9RX640 and Kuhn Performer 7000
