Make An Amazing Electric Boat With Pepsi Cans and DC motor - DIY BOAT

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Hi , In this video we will learn how to easily make an Electric Boat at home .

Tools :
1. Pepsi Cans
2. Grill stick
3. DC Motor
4. 9V Battery
5. Switch on/off
6. Soldering Iron and Wire
7. Connecting Wires
8. Super glue
9. Hot Glue Gun
