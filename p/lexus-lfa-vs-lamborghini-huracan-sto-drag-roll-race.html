Lexus LFA vs Lamborghini Huracan STO // DRAG & ROLL RACE

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
Enter at https://www.omaze.com/throttlehouse22 for your chance to win a Corvette Stingray, and support a great cause, The 500 Festival Foundation.

The Lexus LFA is a V10 legend. It has a blistering 9k rpm redline and an exhaust that was acoustically designed by Yamaha. Beside it sits another V10 monster, the road-legal race car the Lamborghini Huracan STO. Thomas has taken the helm of the STO and James is behind the wheel of the LFA. Although both horsepower and torque favour the Lamborghini, giving it the best shot at victory, no one can lose when you put two screaming V10s beside each other. Watch as Thomas and James smile ear to ear throughout this drag race. We hope you enjoy the episode. Thanks for watching! SUBSCRIBE!
