Complete Restoration Of Old Scooter.(Piaggio x9 Scooter)

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
We are getting our old abandoned scooter back on the road. The scooter, which had been dismantled in a garage for many years because it was not working, finally found itself in the Restoringo garage. It is enough to watch the video to witness the revival of the scooter, which has been waiting for years in the depths of the garage, as it cannot be repaired because it does not work in this adventure.
In this restoration process, since the scooter did not work for many reasons, especially engine pressure loss, ignition failure and fuel problems, all of them were repaired one by one.
The rings of our engine around the piston have been changed, and valve training has been done to prevent valve leaks.
Due to the high cost of the existing CDI ignition failure parts, a universal CDI system was adopted.
