94 Year Old Wing Chun Grandmaster Ip Chun | Martial Diaries_013

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Welcome to this exciting episode where I take you on a journey to the Ving Tsun Athletic Association in Hong Kong, where you'll have a chance to witness the legendary Grandmaster Ip Chun teaching Wing Chun in the same Kung-fu school established by his late father, the great Grandmaster Ip Man.

Throughout the video, you'll get a glimpse of the daily classes held by Grandmaster Ip Chun, who has dedicated his life to spreading the knowledge and wisdom of Wing Chun to the next generation of martial artists.

This video is a must-see for martial arts enthusiasts, Wing Chun practitioners, and culture lovers. So join me on this journey to the heart of Hong Kong's martial arts scene and discover the art of Wing Chun through the eyes of Grandmaster Ip Chun.
