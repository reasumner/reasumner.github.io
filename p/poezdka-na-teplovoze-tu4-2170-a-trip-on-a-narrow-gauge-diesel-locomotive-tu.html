A trip on a TU4-2170 diesel locomotive to load peat on the 58th field of the Pischalsky peat enterprise. The Pishchalskoye peat enterprise was established in 1963, when peat extraction was started in the Orichevsky district of the Kirov region. The narrow-gauge railway was built for the removal of peat and the transportation of passengers, for the transportation of workers to the peat bogs. The Pishchalskoe peat enterprise managed to survive the hard times and survive, the narrow-gauge railway operates, there are prospects for work. It works around the clock and every day, performing 3-4 cargo flights a day from two peat sites. Peat is overloaded on a wide track and transported to Kirov and Sharya to a thermal power plant. There is passenger traffic, transportation of workers to the peat bogs.
