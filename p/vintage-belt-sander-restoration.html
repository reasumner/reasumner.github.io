Vintage Belt Sander Restoration

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
I bought a vintage belt sander from a used tool store. Through my research, I discovered it was manufactured by Makita, model 9400, in Japan. When I found it, the shaft was seized and it was quite rusty. In this video, I will restore its functionality. I hope everyone enjoys the video
* Belt Sander Information *
- Brand: Makita
- Model: 9400
- VOLTS: 100-110V
- Power : 880W
- Curent : 8A
- Frequency: 50/60 Hz
- Belt Size: 100 x 610 mm (4 x 24")
- Belt Speed: 400 m/min
- Net Weight : 8kg
