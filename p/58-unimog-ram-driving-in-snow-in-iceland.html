58" Unimog Ram Driving in Snow in Iceland

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
In this video we go on the first proper snow test drive of the 58" Unimog Ram as well as joining briefly for the vehicle inspection. Since this was the first real test drive of the truck we wanted to see everything working and try not to break anything just yet. Joining us for this trip were two other Ram trucks. One 58" Unimog Ram with similar modifications as the blue 58" Ram, and then another Ram on 44" Nokian Hakkapeliitta tires. I hope you enjoy this video!
