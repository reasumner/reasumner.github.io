Take a walk through 2/29 Collins St Yokine.

Welcome to 2/29 Collins Street, Yokine — a stylish 3 bedroom, 1 bathroom (two-way) rear home that’s perfect for first home buyers, executive couples, young families, or investors looking for a low-maintenance lifestyle in a prime location.

Tucked away on a generous 380m² block with no strata fees, this private residence offers space, comfort, and practicality. Inside, enjoy a flexible floorplan with separate lounge and dining zones, a handy study nook, and modern updates throughout.

Step outside to your own private courtyard — complete with retractable awning and ideal for entertaining or relaxing. The two-way bathroom adds convenience, and all three bedrooms are generously sized with excellent natural light.

Situated close to local shops, parks, public transport, and within the highly sought-after Mt Lawley Senior High School catchment, this home delivers unbeatable value in a thriving suburb.
