Can the BYD Shark Actually Off-Road? A Head-to-Head with the Ford Ranger Raptor

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Join Ciro De Siena and Kaalvoet Swys as they put the all-new BYD Shark and the formidable Ford Ranger Raptor to the ultimate off-road test! In this video, we delve into the capabilities of both bakkies, tackling challenging terrains such as steep inclines, side slopes, and water crossings.

Discover how the BYD Shark's innovative blade battery performs under pressure and see if its electric power can rival the traditional might of the Ford Ranger Raptor. We'll explore their unique features, test their limits, and provide an honest comparison to help you decide which bakkie reigns supreme off-road. Don't miss this epic battle of power and technology!
