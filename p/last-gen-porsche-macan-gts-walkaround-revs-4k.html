2024 Porsche Macan GTS. This high-performance SUV delivers an exhilarating driving experience, featuring a twin-turbocharged 2.9L V6 producing 434 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque, paired with Porsches quick-shifting 7-speed PDK transmission and all-wheel drive for dynamic handling in any condition.

Finished in a sleek and sophisticated exterior color, this Macan GTS boasts an aggressive stance with GTS-specific styling, black exterior accents, and sporty 21-inch wheels. The premium interior is appointed with Alcantara and leather upholstery, carbon fiber or aluminum trim, and 14-way power-adjustable sport seats, ensuring both comfort and control.
