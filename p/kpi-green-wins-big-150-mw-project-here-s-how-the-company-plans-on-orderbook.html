KPI Green Wins Big 150 MW Project; Here's How The Company Plans On Orderbook Expansion

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
KPI Green Energy has received a letter of intent to set up wind power projects with a total capacity of 150 megawatt in Gujarat. The letter of intent has been awarded by Gujarat Urja Vikas Nigam Ltd., the company said in an exchange filing. Faruk Patel, CMD, KP Group, shares his insights on the big order win and how the company plans to expand its order value by 2026. Watch the complete video to know more.
