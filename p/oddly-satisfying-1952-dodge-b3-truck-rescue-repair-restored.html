Oddly Satisfying 1952 Dodge B3 Truck Rescue & Repair | RESTORED

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
Join in with the Restored crew as they dive into this abandoned 1952 Dodge B3 truck. After discovering this classic from a local field where it had set for over 50 years the team dives into the obstacles of getting this mean green machine alive and back on the road again. With Christmas being less than 3 weeks away, the guys decide this would be the perfect candidate for their upcoming town parade. Follow along as they transform this forgotten beauty into a head-turning, flame-throwing Grinch Mobile, that truly is a mean one!
