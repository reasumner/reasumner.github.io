Christmas Market in Warsaw, Poland. Christmas atmosphere and street food in the center of Warsaw!

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
#Warsaw #sausages #christmas #streetfood #christmasmarkets #Poland #ChristmasInPoland
This video is dedicated to one of Warsaw's most magical winter traditions‚Äîthe Christmas market. This year, it opened in a completely new location, right in the heart of the city, next to the Palace of Science and Culture. This location makes the market more than just a festive event, but a true magnet for residents and tourists looking to experience the spirit of Christmas in Poland. Strolling among the wooden houses, you'll instantly find yourself in a winter fairytale. The air is filled with the scent of cinnamon, hot mulled wine, roasted nuts, and traditional Polish delicacies. Vendors offer everything from handmade decorations and toys to a variety of delicacies, including buckwheat groats, baked sausages, traditional pies, and honey sweets. Every corner is filled with music, laughter, and a sense of the approaching holiday. The new location next to the Palace of Science and Culture adds a special touch to the fair: the majestic building creates a beautiful backdrop for strolling, and the festive illumination transforms the square into a bright and inviting space. It's here that families, friends, travelers, and simply those who appreciate the beautiful urban atmosphere gather in winter. Everything around seems perfect for taking warm photos, sharing moments, and creating new memories.
In this video, you'll see what life is like at the fair in its new location, which locations and dishes are worth trying, what the festive decorations look like, and why this place has become one of Warsaw's most popular winter spots. If you're planning a visit to the Polish capital during the holidays, this fair is a must-see. Enjoy watching and immersing yourself in the atmosphere of a true Christmas miracle!
