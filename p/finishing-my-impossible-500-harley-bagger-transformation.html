In this final episode of my impossible $500 Harley Bagger build, I wrap up nearly every detail: muffler brackets, custom windshield install, and electrical fixes to make this bike a reliable everyday rider. This old Electra Glide has officially become “Ford”—my parts runner, my old work truck. The paint’s not perfect, but it’s mine. I’ll keep tweaking through winter, and when spring comes, I just may take you along for that ride.
