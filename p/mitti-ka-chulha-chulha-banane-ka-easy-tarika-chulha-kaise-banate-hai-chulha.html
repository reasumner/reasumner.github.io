In this video, we'll show you how to make a
traditional Indian clay chulha from scratch. a Chulha is clay oven used for cooking delicious food, and it's a great way to connect with our cultural heritage.

Follow these easy steps to create your own clay
chulha at home:-

1.) Collect the necessary materials: clay, sand water and mold.
2.) Mix the clay and sand to create a dough-like
consistency.
3.) Shape the mixture into a cylindrical from using the mold.
4.) Smooth and shape the surface, creating a narrow opening at the top.
5.) Allow the chulha to dry completely.

Watch till the end to see the final result and how to use your new clay chulha for cooking!
