CX500 Scrambler Build Timelapse!

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
Honda CX500 Scrambler build timelapse! This video shows start to finish of my latest creation which is this 1982 CX500 Custom. This bike was built for a customer who wants to do ADV rides with his friends all over the country. To meet these goals I focused on fuel capacity for a target 200 mile range, suspension to handle the various terrain, and gear carrying capability for all the necessities when traveling by motorcycle. Overall I feel this turned out fantastic and is an absolute BLAST to ride! Now I just need to build another one!
