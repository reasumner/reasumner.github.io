üéµ Binaural Beats Explained
Binaural beats are auditory tones that are created by playing two slightly different tones in each ear. When your brain hears these two tones, it creates a third tone, which is the binaural beat. Different frequencies of binaural beats are associated with different brainwave states:

Alpha (8-13Hz): Relaxation and creativity
Beta (13-32Hz): Alert focus and concentration
Gamma (32-100Hz): Peak awareness and cognitive function
