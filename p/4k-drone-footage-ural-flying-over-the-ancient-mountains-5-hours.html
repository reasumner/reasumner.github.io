Fly over the most ancient mountains of the planet. Shooting with a drone and 4K UHD resolution of this ambient drone film will allow you to take a break from the routine and enjoy stunning views of the ancient mountains from a bird's eye view. This majestic aerial video in 4K UHD quality, about the nature of the Southern Urals will provide an opportunity to see a glorious miracle created by nature more than 4 billion years ago. Covered with snow and dense spruce forests, the Ural Mountains are impressive and stunning. Unworn paths, steep rocky slopes, wild vegetation, and sonorous trills of forest birds will make you forget about problems and plunge into the world of clean air, frosty freshness, and picturesque mountain peaks.

This is not the end of the world, but the place where two parts of it meet. Here rises the oldest mountain of the planet Kara-tash and it is more than four billion years old and hides traces of ancient civilizations. Here they find gold nuggets weighing more than two pounds and people disappear without a trace. Previously, this place was simply called Stone, but in the modern world, it is known as the South Urals.
