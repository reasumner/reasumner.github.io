'Caribbean Blue' is a song from Enya's 1991 studio album, 'Shepherd Moons'. This official 4K music video features the track, which is structured as a waltz and built upon the synthesizer textures and layered vocals that define the new age and celtic sound of Enya. The music for the song was composed by Enya, with lyrics by Roma Ryan and production by Nicky Ryan.
