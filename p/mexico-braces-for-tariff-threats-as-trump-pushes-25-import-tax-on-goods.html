Mexico braces for tariff threats as Trump pushes 25% import tax on goods

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
Mexico is facing the threat of a 25% import tax on all goods heading to the United States as President Trump continues to pressure the country. In response, Mexico is boosting its internal market by promoting products proudly made in the country, leveraging long-standing national pride in domestic manufacturing.

Al Jazeera’s John Holman reports from Mexico City.
