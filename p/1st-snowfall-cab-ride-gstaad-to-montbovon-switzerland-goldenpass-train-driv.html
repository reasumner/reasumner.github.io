1st SnowFall: Cab ride - Gstaad to Montbovon Switzerland | GoldenPass Train Driver view | 4K 60p HDR

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
First snowfall in Switzerland and we present the train driver view of the journey between Gstaad and Montbovon stations, boarded on Goldenpass panoramic train of Montreux Oberland Bernois (MOB) railway. This video offers majestical views and stunning natural surroundings of the Swiss villages covered under Snow passing through beautiful chalets and mountainous landscapes of Switzerland. Enjoy the snowy winter magic :)
