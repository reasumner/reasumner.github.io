In this video, I break down why putting all your money in SIP investments can be risky and how a balanced approach is crucial for long-term financial success. I explain the (100-X) rule, where X% of your savings (based on your age) should go into safe assets while the remaining should be invested in equity with a proper balance between large cap, mid cap, and small cap funds.

I also highlight the role of gold as a hedge against market crashes, showing how a simple 80-20 or 70-30 equity-gold portfolio can significantly reduce volatility while maintaining solid returns. Through practical examples and Excel calculations, I demonstrate how rebalancing your portfolio every 5-10 years can protect your investments from market downturns, especially when you need the money most.
