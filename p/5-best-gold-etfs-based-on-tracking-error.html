What is a Gold ETF?

A Gold Exchange Traded Fund (ETF) is a mutual fund that invests in physical gold of 99.5% purity, and each unit represents roughly 1 gram of gold. It trades on stock exchanges like shares and reflects the price movement of gold.
Gold ETFs offer liquidity, transparency, and convenience‚Äîideal for investors who want the benefits of gold without the hassles of physical ownership.
