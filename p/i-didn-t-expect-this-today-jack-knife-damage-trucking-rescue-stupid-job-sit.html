I'm an independent owner/operator, hauling flatbed freight in and around the Western United States.

Stretch trailer load! Let's take a 72' load to Oxnard, CA, what could go wrong? I tear stuff up, pull off Super Trucker moves to get into the building, and our friend loses the engine in his truck. It's now a rescue mission back home.

Hanging on to old school traditions and methods in a 21st century trucking world that's tracked and monitored. I own a truck, and I make a living with it.

This is how I make a buck with a truck.

On this channel, I'll share stories of loads hauled, places delivered to and some of the people I meet, while doing what needs to be done to earn a living as an independent owner/operator.
