I took my Porsche 911 on a 2000KM Roadtrip through the Dolomites!

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Endless Mountain Passes – 4-Day Dream Road Trip Through the Italian Alps

A big thank you to the Werk96 team for organizing this incredible tour and for letting us join the group even though I wasn’t driving a 996.

Our days in the Dolomites were unforgettable. Not even the changing weather could dampen the atmosphere. Beyond the stunning roads and impressive cars, it was the people who made this trip truly special – a group of passionate drivers who enjoy their cars in every situation and don’t mind a bit of rain.

I was genuinely impressed by how well the Carrera 4 handled the conditions. Yes, the all-wheel drive naturally helps in bad weather, but it’s still a sports car with 305s at the back, carving down narrow mountain passes covered in water. The confidence it gave me was remarkable and made me appreciate the 991.1 C4 even more.

The naturally aspirated sound, the balance, and the ability to perform in almost any weather make it a fantastic sports car for every situation. After all, it’s still a 911.
