1985 Lada 2106 Automatic Low Rider. From Stock to Slammed

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
This is a1985 Lada 2106 Low Rider project. A full transformation from a simple Soviet classic into a clean, slammed and retro street cruiser. This video covers the full import story. Why I bought the car, the mods, the paint, the stance and the first drives.

This 2106 is an automatic, which is extremely rare for a Lada of this era and almost unheard of in the UK. The build includes a custom air ride setup, classic deep dish wheels, a full respray, restored chrome details and a complete interior refresh. The Lada 2106 is rare enough in the UK, but a low rider version with an automatic gearbox is something you never see on the road.

More retro car projects are coming. From Ladas to UAZ vans, Unimogs, 6x6 trucks and unusual overlanding rigs. I source and import unique vehicles worldwide and document the builds along the way.
