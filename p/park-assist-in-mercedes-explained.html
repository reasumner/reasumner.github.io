PARK ASSIST in Mercedes EXPLAINED!

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Heard of the Mercedes-Benz Active Park Assist or Parking Pilot? It basically assists the driver with parking in either parallel parking or bay parking.

This video showcases Active Park Assist in a bay parking space. Parallel parking follows the same procedure but not all cars have the ability to do it.

*IMPORTANT!*
As mentioned at the end of the video, due to United Nations Regulations (UN-R 79) not all cars have "Active" Park Assist. Some may not have it all, sone may only offer bay parking and/or only parallel parking. Speak with your local Mercedes and they can confirm if your car has this feature.
