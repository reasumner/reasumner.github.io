How to Think Like a Successful Investor | The Money Mindset ft. Maneesh Dangi

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
In this episode of The Money Mindset, we are joined by Maneesh Dangi, a deep thinker and student of the stock markets for the last 25 years. He is the CEO and Founder of Mosaic Asset Management and previously served as the Chief Investment Officer of Aditya Birla Sun Life MF for 15 years.
With over two decades of experience in investing and research, including more than 15 years in the asset management industry, Dangi has an extensive track record in managing portfolios. He is also the author of Booms, Busts, and Market Cycles. We spoke to him about how to train your mind to think like a successful investor.
