Land Rover Discovery: Battle of Generations | Discovery 2, Discovery 3 & Discovery 4

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
This is a long-awaited comparative test of the Land Rover Discovery of different generations. Cars will have to drive the Rock Gate trail, which consists of a series of obstacles: a steep climb with diagonal suspensions, the Rock Gate itself, a S-shaped turn with large rocks and a long sandy hill.
