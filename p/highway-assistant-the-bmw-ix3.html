Highway Assistant | The BMW iX3.

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Enjoy relaxed driving on the highway with the optional Highway Assistant for the BMW iX3. It supports you with keeping the speed, distance and lane as well as with changing lanes. On approved highways it allows you to position your hands comfortably up to driving speeds of 130 km/h.
