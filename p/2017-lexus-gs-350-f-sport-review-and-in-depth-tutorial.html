The GS 350 is one of my all time favorite Lexus vehicles. It has been around for a long time in the Lexus family of cars and is well-known for performance, style, and reliability. The F Sport model takes everything that the GS is and ramps it up to another level. As you may have already figured out, I am IN LOVE with the aggressive spindle grill that adorns the face of this car.
Lets take an in depth look at this amazing and luxurious performance machine from Lexus...the 2017 Lexus GS350 F Sport.
