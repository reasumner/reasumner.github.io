The First New Defender 130 On Black Bear Pass!

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Every off-road enthusiast knows about the famous Black Bear Pass. But for the past two years, the trail has been closed due to extensive road damage resulting from weather-induced mudslides and rockslides. No one knew the status of the trail until recently when the county surprisingly repaired, cleared the snow, and opened it up in July 2025! We knew we had to come back to conquer this dangerous but beautiful trail, this time in our New Defender 130. We believe we are the first! So sit back and enjoy the ride.
