What does it mean? Colt Series 70, 80 & 90 1911s w/ comparison of 1911 vs. 1911A1

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
In this video I compare and contrast Colt 1911s, 1911A1, Series 70, 80 & 90 pistols. The nomenclature and definitions have changed overtime and some of these terms can apply to 1911s made by other manufacturers. So what do they all mean? I do my best in this tutorial video to share what I know.
