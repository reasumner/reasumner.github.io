“Show Us Your Feet” – German Women POWs Shocked by a Strange Order from American Soldiers
Step inside the hidden stories of World War 2. This video uncovers one of the most shocking and deeply human moments of WWII history — when terrified German women POWs were ordered by American soldiers to do something they never expected: “Show us your feet.”

In a muddy U.S. POW camp near the Rhine – like those at Camp Hearn and other U.S. POW camps – 47 female POWs braced for revenge, torture, and humiliation. Instead, they met medics with warm water and bandages. What followed brought Auschwitz tattoos into a wooden tent, turned enemies into nurses, and ripped apart years of Nazi lies.

This is a cinematic WWII documentary–style story built from WWII archives, eyewitness accounts, and hard historical facts. You’ll see German prisoners treated with unexpected care, learn how foot infections killed more soldiers than bullets, and watch how simple medical acts became powerful true war stories of wartime humanity.
