This absolute beast is the Brabus G800… And Mat’s got his hands on one for an all-new in-depth review!

The Brabus G800 is a maxed out version of the Mercedes-AMG G65. And if you thought a new AMG G-Wagon was expensive when new, well trust us, it’s got nothing on the G800's price tag. When new, one of these would have set you back £600,000!!

So of course, with a price like that, you know the quality of the G800 is going to be out of this world. There are a whole host of upgrades compared to the standard G65, with massive air intakes and 23” alloys on the outside, to a load of soft leather and carbon fibre found throughout the cabin.

The upgrades don’t just stop there though. Under the bonnet you’ll find a monumental 6-litre twin-turbo V12 which can pump out 800hp & 1,100Nm of torque!!

It’s one of the most imposing cars on the road… But can it really justify the £600,000 price tag? You’ll have to stick with Mat to find out!
