Published at : 12 Dec 2025
After sitting untouched in a barn for a decade this gorgeous 1951 Chevy Deluxe was dropped off for a top-to-bottom detail!
Owner Jeff, who previously brought me his incredible 1957 Chevy Panel Truck barn find trusted me to bring the paint, chrome, and interior of this Deluxe back to life. We spent hours scrubbing away years of dust and grime to reveal the classic beauty underneath.
But the real magic happens at the end: Owner Jeff surprised us all by doing some quick work to the engine right here at the shop! Watch as we transform this Deluxe and celebrate its first start in ten years!
