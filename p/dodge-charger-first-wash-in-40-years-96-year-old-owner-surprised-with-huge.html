Dodge Charger First Wash in 40 Years! 96 Year Old Owner Surprised With Huge Transformation

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
Go to http://www.squarespace.com/AMMONYC to get a free trial and 10% off your first purchase of a website or domain. This 1968 Dodge Charger was left in a storage unit for over 30 years. The 96 year old enthusiast owner Peter upgraded the engine to the coveted 440 V8 with 6 pack carburetors. I power wash years of dust and grime off the red single stage paint before a full paint correction, interior detail, and wax. Meanwhile, Tom and Tom Sr. from Ace Performance rebuild the carburetors, replace the fuel system, plugs, ignition and more to get the Charger running. Afterwards we surprise Peter with his beloved Charger clean for the first time in 30 years with new American Racing Wheels wearing Vredestein Quatrac Pro Tires. Enjoy the insane detail transformation and mechanical restoration of this iconic American Muscle Car. Huge thanks to Ace Performance, Vredestein Tires, Holley Performance, Pertronix, American Racing, Liqui Moly, and my neighbor Steve for all the parts, upgrades, and tools.
