Shadow Fight 2 is a fighting game published by Nekki and developed by Banzai Games. The game includes fascinating adventures of a man, who turned into shadow by opening the gates. He defeats all the demons: Lynx, Hermit, Butcher, Wasp, Widow, Shogun, - who were freed from the gates. After the closure of the gates, May, Shadow's close companion, he needs to open the gates and free her from the main antagonist, and the destroyer of the worlds - Titan. Through extensive training in hideout, stone grove, spaceship, factory, incubator, and after defeating several important figures ( Ancient, Cypher, Shroud ) he manages to get into Titan's citadel and when all his bodyguards are defeated, he finally manages to find May, but she's hypnotized by Titan. After winning May, she manages to return her mind. And the last, but the most important battle is against Titan. Whether Shadow defeats Titan and saves entire worlds is a question of time.
