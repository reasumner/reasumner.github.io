[4K HDR]2025 Chengdu Auto Show, there are many new electric cars and beauties

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Founded in 1998, the Chengdu International Auto Show (CDMS) is an A-level automobile exhibition jointly sponsored by the Chengdu Municipal People's Government and the Automotive Industry Branch of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, founded in 1998, held in the Western China International Expo City from August to September every year, with an exhibition period of 10 days
The exhibition is the largest automotive industry event in western China and certified by the International Association of the Exhibition Industry (UFI), ranking among the four major A-level auto shows in China, with 27 editions as of 2024 and an exhibition area of 200,000 square metersWith the theme of "Tidal Rongcheng, Intelligent Leadership of the Future", the 2024 exhibition will focus on the field of new energy and intelligent networked vehicles, attracting 420 car manufacturers at home and abroad to participate in the exhibition, bringing more than 1,000 exhibition cars to the exhibition, during which there will be new car test drives, intelligent interconnection experience, and China Automobile Pioneer Forum and other activities
