LEGO Minecraft's Biggest Rivalry: Villager vs. Illager!

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
This LEGO short showcases the biggest conflict in the blocky world: the Villager VS the Illager! We're building the two opposing factions of the overworld, the peaceful citizen and the aggressive, axe-wielding raider. These are essential minifigures for understanding the core dynamics of the Minecraft world.

The Villagers are passive NPCs, known for their trades and odd vocalizations, who live in peaceful settlements. The Illagers, a faction of malicious, grey-skinned mobs including Vindicators and Pillagers, live in Woodland Mansions and outpost towers. They regularly organize destructive raids on the Villagers, making this rivalry an existential conflict in the game.

Let's look at the LEGO minifigures. The Villager is instantly recognizable with the unique, molded nose piece and large green torso. He wears a brown robe, and his head is printed with either simple, friendly eyes or angry eyebrows depending on the variant. The Illager (specifically the Vindicator or Pillager) is defined by its grey, scowling face, dark blue-grey robes, and usually comes armed with a massive axe, making him look imposing next to the humble Villager.

Both the Villager (like min010) and the Illager (like the Vindicator, min058) have been widely released. The Villager has been in numerous sets since 2014 (like 21115 "The First Night"). The Vindicator/Illager first became widely available in sets like the 2019 set 21159 "The Pillager Outpost," providing fans with an army of these crucial minifigures for their raids.
