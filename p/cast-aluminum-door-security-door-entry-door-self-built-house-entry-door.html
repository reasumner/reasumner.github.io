Cast aluminum door security door entry door self-built house entry door

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Cast aluminum door is made of solid cast aluminum as raw material, through modern high-tech processing technology, the whole cast molding category of products, with the following characteristics:
### Advantages
- ** Durable ** : Cast aluminum has small density and light weight, but excellent strength and hardness, strong impact resistance, the door body is not easy to deform, can effectively resist external damage, and provide reliable protection for home safety. And it is born with good corrosion resistance, not afraid of wind and rain, in the rainy and humid south or the dry and windy environment in the north, it can also be used for a long time without rust, and the service life is far beyond the ordinary material door.
- ** Good anti-theft performance ** : its own structure is strong, with high-level lock core (such as C-level lock core), multiple anti-theft design, greatly improving the safety factor of the family.
- ** Good sound insulation ** : thick door body plus efficient filler (such as aluminum honeycomb, water-based foam materials, etc.), can effectively isolate external noise, reduce the spread of sound, and create a quiet indoor environment; At the same time, it can keep the indoor temperature stable, play a good thermal insulation effect, and reduce energy consumption.
- ** Strong aesthetics ** : exquisite workmanship, through fine carving, relief and other processes to create a unique texture, exquisite carving, novel and diverse styles. Can combine traditional culture with modern technology, can also design a simple atmosphere or artistic style, suitable for a variety of decoration styles, such as modern simple, Chinese, European and so on.
- ** Low maintenance cost ** : surface flame retardant and stain resistant, daily cleaning, oil stains, stains can be wiped clean with wet cloth, easy maintenance.
- ** Can be customized ** : can be customized according to customer requirements of style, size and size, to meet the individual needs of different consumers.
- ** Short production cycle ** : integral casting, one molding, no lengthy welding process, improve production efficiency.

### Flaws
** High price ** : Due to the high cost of raw materials and production process, compared with stainless steel doors, steel doors, etc., the price of cast aluminum door is generally high, and the market positioning is more suitable for high-end users, for ordinary families with limited budgets, the price ratio is not dominant.

### Category
According to the different structure, the cast aluminum door can be divided into solid wood cast aluminum door, solid core cast aluminum door and plywood cast aluminum door:
- ** Solid wood cast aluminum door ** : made of solid wood processing, there are all wood, half glass, all glass three styles. From the perspective of wood processing technology, there are two kinds of finger jointing wood and log, finger jointing wood performance is more stable than log, can effectively prevent door deformation, giving people a stable and elegant feeling.
- ** Solid core cast aluminum door ** : solid wood frame, two sides with decorative panel bonding, door interior filled with insulation, flame retardant materials. There are no half glass and full glass models, but small pieces of glass can be set. The panel shape is rich, the insulation and sound insulation effect is similar to that of solid wood doors, the price is lower than that of solid wood doors, and it has fire retardant function, giving a sense of stability when used.
- ** Plywood cast aluminum door ** : made of solid wood frame, both sides are made of decorative panels glued to the frame. Light quality, low price, good decorative effect, most of the panels are imported materials, giving people a simple and lightweight feeling, more applications in home decoration.
