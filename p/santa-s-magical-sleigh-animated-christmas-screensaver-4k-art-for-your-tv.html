Sometimes the simplest of concepts for a video can be the hardest to make. If I told you how many iterations it took to get this visual exactly as I envisioned it, you wouldn't believe me. The golden reflective lights adorning the tree and sleigh, the girth of the reindeer and their heavy fur coats, the forest in the background gently lit by the reflective qualities of winter's moonlight, all are intricate details that serve to create a captivating image. But those details pale in comparison to what it took to get Santa's expression spot on right. It is, in my opinion, what sets this artwork apart and makes it truly special. In this capture, you can sense that Santa Claus knows he's being observed. The look on his face with his eyes gazing toward the viewer has this unique expression that vacillates between letting you know that you shouldn't be seeing him, while also having a smirk of mysteriousness as if to suggest that he will vanish before you can fully comprehend what you are witnessing. The gentle snow falling in the foreground adds an atmospheric touch to create a Christmas moment filled with anticipation and wonder. It's a perfect Christmas vibe to have playing on your TV or digital display throughout the holiday season to bring a serene and magical ambiance to your room and space.
