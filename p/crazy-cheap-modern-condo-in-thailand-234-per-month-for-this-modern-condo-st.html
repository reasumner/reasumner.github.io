Discover The Escent Condo in Chiang Mai | Full Tour with Stunning Rooftop Pool

Welcome back to another exciting condo tour! Today, we're exploring The Escent Condo in Chiang Mai, a modern building conveniently located near Central Festival. Join Miss Lena and me as we take you through all the fantastic features and amenities this condo has to offer.

Introduction and Overview of The Escent Condo
We start our tour at the bustling main road, with Central Festival and Supalai building in sight. Once inside the secure premises, the noise fades, revealing a peaceful and well-guarded environment. Miss Lena, our enthusiastic guide, shares her first impressions and invites us to explore the condo together.

Unit Details and Pricing
The unit we're touring is 31 square meters, available for rent at 8,500 baht per month (around $250). Miss Lena and I discuss utility costs, with electricity billed at government rates and water typically costing around 200-300 baht per month. Despite some playful banter about showering habits, it's clear that The Escent Condo offers great value for money.

Lobby and Common Areas in The Escent Condo
We step into the modern lobby, which features meeting rooms and multi-purpose rooms that residents can book. Miss Lena points out the laundry facilities and the unique main water dispenser, where residents can fill large bottles for just one baht per liter, making living here both affordable and convenient.

Exploring The Escent Condo
The unit is cozy and well-equipped, featuring a compact kitchen with a fridge freezer, hob, space for a microwave, and Thai pans for cooking. Miss Lena demonstrates her culinary skills and shares some favorite dishes like fried rice and pad krapow. The balcony offers stunning views of Doi Suthep and the bustling Central Festival area.

Bedroom and Bathroom in The Escent Condo
The bedroom is spacious, with a comfortable queen-sized bed and a dressing area. The bathroom is modern and functional, with a shower, sink, and ample space. The living area is neat and can be made homely very quickly, perfect for a couple or a small family.

Rooftop Pool and Gym at The Escent Condo
The highlight of our tour is the rooftop pool on the 26th floor. With breathtaking views of Doi Suthep and the surrounding areas, it’s a perfect spot to relax and unwind. The adjacent gym, equipped with various machines and weights, offers residents a place to stay fit while enjoying the panoramic scenery.

Recap and Final Thoughts about The Escent Condo
We recap the key points: the unit is 31 square meters, available for 8,500 baht per month, with affordable utility costs. The high-speed internet options make it ideal for both working professionals and leisure seekers. Miss Lena emphasizes the spaciousness and convenience of the unit, making it a great option for those seeking comfort and a vibrant lifestyle.
