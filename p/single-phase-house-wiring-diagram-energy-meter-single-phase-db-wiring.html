Single Phase House Wiring Diagram / Energy Meter / Single Phase DB Wiring

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
In this video, I try to explain the concept of single phase house wiring system with intituled electrical components. And also this video describes how electricity reaches our homes from the electric pole, single phase energy meter, main isolation switch (DP MCB), RCCB and SP MCB, switches, lights, socket outlets and how electricity works in the house.

220 to 240 volts are used in our single phase house wiring. SP MCB automatically switches off during an abnormal condition of the house electrical network means in overload condition as well as faulty condition.

DP MCB - Double Pole Miniature Circuit Breaker
RCCB - Residual Current Circuit Breaker
SP MCB - Single Pole Miniature Circuit Breaker
DB - Distribution Board

If you have any questions about single phase house wiring please comment below the video. I will answer your questions.
