BUYING ANOTHER ABANDONED BMW FROM M539 RESTORATIONS! WILL THIS 750 HIGHLINE RUN AGAIN?

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Sreten mentioned in one episode he is thinking of selling his Project Karlsruhe BMW 750iL. At this moment I lost my mind and directly called him, because I was also searching for new project. We made a deal quickly and he asked me if I am interested in this crispy and abandoned 750 Highline. Of course I said yes and took the both cars with me.

As you can see I wanted to fix the Highline at first, so that I can move the car in the workshop, and to check all other electrical and mechanical equipment.
This car is off the road from aprox. 2010. Surprisingly engine and Transmission are working fine, but all the other electrical stuff is in bad shape.

You can ask for the parts but i'm not going to sell anything right now because I need the parts for the black one.

If you have some questions about the car, fell free to comment down bellow, I am more then happy to answer all your questions and help you with your projects.
