Automotive star power (without the star) depends on exclusivity and heritage ‚Äì traits the Mercedes-Benz G-Class has in generous measure. From its origins 40 years ago as a Spartan utility vehicle to its current luxury-laden form, the G-Class has remained defiantly boxy and thoroughly capable off-road. Relatively small production and steep pricing compared to Mercedes-Benz‚Äôs other SUVs make the G-Class (especially the range-topping G63) a natural fit for the famous ‚Äì and famously rich.

The 2019 G63 is on sale now at a starting price of $148,495 (including destination).
