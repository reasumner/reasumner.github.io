Product Overview
The Fenix C7 Flashlight is a high-performance flashlight that is the perfect tool for industrial and maintenance operations. Delivering up to 3000 lumens, the Fenix C7 flashlight can throw that light a maximum distance of 1542ft (470m). Always know the battery’s status with the battery level indicator and keep the included 5000mAh battery charged via the USB Type-C port. The magnetic base on the Fenix C7 allows you to attach the flashlight to most metal surfaces for hands-free lighting. Waterproof, dustproof and impact resistant to 1m, the C7 Flashlight is built tough enough to withstand anything your job site can dish out. *Does not have a tail switch.
