Can an AMG beat a Skoda OFF-ROAD?!

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Thanks to Darkside Developments for lending us their Yeti: @DarksideDevelopments
Thanks to Q Drive Performance for lending us their AMG: https://qdriveperformance.co.uk/

Which will be better off-road - a £174,000 Mercedes-AMG G63, or a £2,000 Skoda Yeti? Well, we’re about to find out!

So let’s see how the two compare. Starting with the AMG, it’s powered by a 4-litre twin-turbo V8 which can deliver 585hp and 850Nm of torque. It’s also built on a ladder frame chassis with 3 lockable diffs, and it’s pretty heavy, tipping the scales at around 2,500kg.

In comparison, the Skoda Yeti comes in a whole lot lighter, at around 1,500kg! But it’s also way down on power, with its tuned 2-litre TDI Diesel engine producing 181hp and 407Nm. However, it’s also been lifted and had some underfloor support added to it, so it’s definitely prepared to go off-road!

So how will this one play out - will it be an easy win for the G63? Or could the Yeti snatch it? You’ll need to stick with Mat to see for yourself!
