“Resentment in Hassan’s Heart; The Revival of Saleh and Masoumeh’s Old Love Beneath the Village Roof

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
Hassan and Fariba stay at Grandmother’s house, carrying a deep resentment toward Ali’s family in their hearts.
Behind the silence, Hassan begins to weave dark plans—waiting patiently for the right moment to strike and take revenge.

Meanwhile, Saleh feels an intense longing for Masoumeh and Sadegh.
He returns to Arslan’s house with Shadi and Shahrukh, trying to keep the children warm and safe as the cold days approach.

There, Sadegh and Masoumeh reveal the full truth about Hassan and Fariba to Saleh.
A quiet moment follows…
Saleh and Masoumeh lock eyes—old memories, buried love, and unspoken emotions rise once again after all this time.

At the same time, Atlas feeds the sheep, while Shadi and Shahrukh play joyfully with the little goats—brief moments of innocence in the middle of growing darkness.

As plaster workers begin decorating the ceiling of the house with delicate patterns, beauty and tension collide.
Love awakens…
But hatred is also growing—silently, dangerously—ready to change the fate of everyone in the village.

Winter is near…
And not every storm comes from the sky.
