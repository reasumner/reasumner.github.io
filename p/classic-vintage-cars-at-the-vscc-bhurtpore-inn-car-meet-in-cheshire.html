CLASSIC & VINTAGE cars at the VSCC Bhurtpore Inn car meet in Cheshire

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
The VSCC Bhurtpore Inn evening car meet has been established for many a year. Organised by the local VSCC (Vintage Sports Car Club) area rep, it's open to anyone - member or not - with an interesting old car, whether vintage or classic. As a result there have been many interesting old vehicles there over the years. This Cheshire car meet is held on the first Thursday evening of the month.
We decided to pay it a visit in our 1954 Renault 750 (4CV) and the MX-5 Mk1.
Cars in attendance on this occasion included a variety of Austin 7s, Riley RM, modified MG TC, vintage Aston Martin, Morgan, Alvis and others, all parked in the field behind the Bhurtpore Inn and representing both vintage and classic cars. The Bhurtpore Inn - a landmark in the area - has been trading since the mid-1800s.
