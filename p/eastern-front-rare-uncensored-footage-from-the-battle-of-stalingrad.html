Eastern Front: Rare Uncensored Footage From The Battle Of Stalingrad

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
Uncover the devastating truth behind Operation Barbarossa, Hitler's ill-fated invasion of the Soviet Union. This documentary reveals how strategic miscalculations and a brutal ideology of annihilation doomed the Nazi war effort from the start. Featuring rare color footage and expert analysis, we delve into the savage realities of the Eastern Front, the vital contributions of Soviet women, and the pivotal Battle of Stalingrad, exploring why this became the psychological turning point of World War 2.
