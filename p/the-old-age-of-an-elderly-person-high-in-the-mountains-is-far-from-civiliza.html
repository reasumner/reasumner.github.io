The old age of an elderly person high in the mountains is far from civilization.

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
This story is about people who live high in the mountains far from civilization, some of the houses in general do not have electricity, as the house is high in the mountains. In this episode, our neighbor Yaroslav, who is about 70 years old, will visit our grandfather, and we will also visit our neighbor Halyna. You will get to know their daily life, where they grow food, preserve their culture and the heritage of their ancestors, and take care of animals. And they take care of their health in harmony with nature. This is the territory of Ukraine, the Boyki ethnic group.
