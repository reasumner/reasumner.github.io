Amazing Global Skills shows Complete Wooden Factory Process of ice cream sticks (popsicle sticks). From raw wood to perfectly shaped and polished sticks used in ice creams and frozen treats around the world.

Watch as logs are cut, processed, steamed, sliced, dried, and shaped into millions of identical sticks with amazing speed and precision. This video showcases modern machinery, skilled workers, and a fully streamlined production line.
