HOW TO MAKE YOUR RIMS LOOK LIKE NEW ON A BUDGET

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Equipment:

Lacquer Thinners
Spray paint (of your choice)
Flathead screwdriver
Bucket
Brush
3 Microfibre towels (1 for degreaser, 1 for drying, 1 for Thinners)
Your favorite Degreaser

Directions:

Cleaning;
1. Take your hose pipe and water your mags to remove all dirt and grime
2. Take your mag wheel cleaner or degreaser and dilute it with water (follow directions of your product)
3. Remove the wheel badge
4. Take your towel and degrease your mags
5. Take your brush to remove any lose spray paint and anything you have missed
6. Take your hose pipe and spray your degreaser off
7. Dry your mags, or go have lunch and relax while they air dry.

Spray painting;
1. Take your spray paint and start with the first coat (the first coat must always be the lightest coat)
2. Start with the second coat (the second coat must be heavier than the first coat, try cover everything with the second coat)
3. After you have waited for the mags to dry start with the last coat (very heavy, because itâ€™s the last coat)

Ending;
1. Take some Thinners and wipe of the places that you have over sprayed
2. Put back your Badge on your mags
