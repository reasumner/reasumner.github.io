Building THE ULTIMATE Engine Swapped 4WD in 20 minutes!

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
#build #4x4 #offroad
Catch up on all the episodes with this 20 minute compilation! This Patrol has turned out to be insane with the 4jj1 Isuzu engine conversion pulling an impressive 270hp on 35" tires, that's well into the 300hp on stock wheels! All being pushed through a built automatic on a Compushift TCM!
The rest of the car is setup to tackle some very hard 4wding tracks and off-road parks, as well as reliably tour around Australia!
This is only phase 1 of this GU build so stay tuned for more mods and episodes! If you have any questions leave a comment and ill try and get back to you!
