ABANDONED Tree Row Big Block Ford F250! Will it Run and Drive After 11 Years?

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
This Big Block Ford F-250 Pickup truck has been sitting in the trees for 11 years now. She's got a 460, and 4 wheel drive. Once a beloved truck, after some rust issues surfaced, it was parked for something more reliable. I fight mice eating wires, a stuck engine, locked wheels, no brake lines, and a host more on this one! Think this sleeping giant will start and run once again? **All debris and garbage were picked up before departing. Ruts caused by the tires were backfilled and tamped**.
