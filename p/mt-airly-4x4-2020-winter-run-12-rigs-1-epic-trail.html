Mt Airly 4x4 -2020 Winter Run - 12 Rigs 1 Epic Trail

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
In this 4x4 video we drive the commonly named Mt Airly Trail up to Genowlan Point in the Mugii-Murrum-Ban region between Capertee and Glen Davis NSW.

Mt Airly is a challenging track with great scenery. The track works its way up to Genowlan Point and is known as Mt Airly in 4wd circles. Named after the property where the track starts and not to be confused with Mt Airlie which is also in the vicinity.

Use the Time Stamps below for easier navigation to your favourite rock climb or 4x4.

We have a good mix of vehicles, 12 4wd's in total on this trip we have a few Jeep Wranglers including a JK, JKU, JK8 and a TJ as well as a Jeep Cherokee XJ and the new Jeep Gladiator JT. Also in the line up there is a GQ Nissan Patrol, a GU Nissan Patrol with LS1 conversion, 2 Toyota Landcruiser 80 Series, a Land Rover 109 Series 2a and a Land Rover 110 County (Defender).

The track is by no means extreme yet its certainly technical and in wet conditions is very slippery adding to the difficulty.
