Overlanding for Warriors 2025 – Wounded Warrior Project Event | Lone Pine, CA

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
We left cold, rainy Northern California and found 75° sunshine at the base of the Sierra. Camped just south of Lone Pine before the event, then rolled into Diaz Lake for Overlanding for Warriors 2025.

This event was planned and organized by  @395jnky to support of  @woundedwarriorproject  - huge thanks to him for putting it together.
What you’ll see in this video:

• Pre‑event camping with a front-row seat to the eastern Sierra
• Sunrise, sunset, and warm weather in late November
• Group camp at Diaz Lake - every kind of set up, not just Jeeps
• Day trip to Alabama Hills and Cerro Gordo ghost town
• The Alabama Hills ride was led by  @TrailNewbie  and  @GoldensGoneWheelin 
• Live music with @countryvisionband, good food, and laid‑back camp time
• Hwy 395 road miles with beautiful Eastern Sierra views

Special thanks:@395Jnky - event organizer @Trailnewbie and @goldensgonewheelin- Alabama Hills ride leads -(Check out their channels for more perspectives from this trip.)

If you’re into real overlanding, storytelling, and down-to-earth camp life, this one should hit the mark. Drop a comment if you’ve run Hwy 395 or have a favorite Sierra spot.
