House Democrats release photos of Trump, Clinton from Epstein's estate

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
U.S. House Democrats released a selection of photos from the estate of Jeffrey Epstein on Friday, including some of President Donald Trump and former U.S. president Bill Clinton. It's unclear exactly who took the photos and when and where they were taken, and the photos do not suggest anything criminal.
