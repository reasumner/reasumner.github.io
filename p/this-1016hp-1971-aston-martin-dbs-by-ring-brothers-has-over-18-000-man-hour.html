This 1016hp 1971 Aston Martin DBS by Ring Brothers Has Over 18,000 Man Hours Behind It

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
The @Ringbrothers never cease to amaze me. Each and every year, come SEMA time, I'm always finding myself picking up my jaw from seeing their insane builds. They're famous for reimagining American classics, their way. The cars always end up being a 1 of 1 build, making each one of them a truly unique piece of machinery. This time, things have changed quite a bit. This is their first debut at The Quail, marking a stark contrast from their usual SEMA debuts. While this isn't their first European car, it's definitely one that stands out in 2025. This carbon-clad art piece comes with all the 007 references that you'll see throughout the video.
