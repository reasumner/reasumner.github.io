Can we get this '59 Impala running, driving, and back to all its previous owners? | Driveway Finds

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
When a 1959 Chevorlet Impala falls in your lap you just can't pass it up. This week Dustin buys an Impala that's been sitting ignored for nearly a decade and Brito can't believe he even had to think about it. A gentleman passed away and the opportunity arrived at Dustins door. Unfortunately, The family can't find any of the old paperwork or history that came with the car. It's up to Dustin and Brito to put on their detective hats and see what they can track down.
