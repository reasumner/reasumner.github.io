Inside Factory Process Of Recycling Waste Cardboard Into High Quality Yarn Paper Cone

#cardboardcraft #recycling #papercraft



In this video you see how to recycle waste paper cardboard and make yarn paper cones


Remove any labels or tape from the cardboard Flatten it out if necessary
Cut into Shapes
Base Cut a circle slightly larger than the desired diameter of your cone
Sides Cut a rectangle The width should be equal to the circumference of the base and the length should be the desired height of the cone
Create the Cone Shape
Roll and Shape: Roll the rectangle into a cone shape

Apply Glue: Apply a generous amount of glue to the bottom edge of the cone
Attach Base: Center the base on the glued area and press firmly Hold until the glue dries.

Adjust the size of the cardboard pieces to create cones of different sizes for use
For added stability reinforce the base with a cardboard circle or a piece of sturdy fabric glued to the bottom.

By following these steps you can transform discarded cardboard into functional and stylish yarn cones
