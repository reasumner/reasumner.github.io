The Story Of Embraer: The Past, Present & Future

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
There are not many large aircraft manufacturers that have survived alongside the Boeing and Airbus duopoly. Over the years, most have moved aside or been acquired.

Embraer stands out as one of the largest independent manufacturers today, offering a range of smaller private and regional commercial jets. With a recent failed merger with Boeing, it looks set to maintain its independence.

We explore its past, present, and future.
