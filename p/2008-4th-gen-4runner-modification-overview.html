2008 4th Gen 4Runner Modification Overview

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
Vehicle Details:
2008 4th Gen 4Runner SR5 V6 Engine, 2WD, with 120,000 miles

Modification Details:
Tires: 275/70R17 BfGoodrich Ko2s Load Range E
Wheels: Black Toyota TRD Pro Rims PTR2035110BK
Lift: Daystar 2.5/1.5 Spacer Lift
Front Upper Control Arms: Freedom Off-Road
Wheel Spacers: Front is 1.25” Spidertrax and Rear is 1.5” All Pro
Tint: 15% Xpel XR Ceramic on sides, 5% on rear windshield
Stereo Head Unit: Joying 10.1” Double Din JY-UQD10N4G-DA
Roof Rack Lights: I have provided a write up on the forums with and put the STL for the parts on thingiverse, see link below.
