Eva's lonely life in the mountains. Far from civilization.

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
In this episode, you will see the continuation of the documentary about the lonely life in the mountains of a 93-year-old woman, Eva, who, despite her advanced age, continues to work and enjoy life. Eva is used to doing everything herself, from time to time Eva calls her neighbor Ivan to help her with her work. Eva sometimes jokes and says that she has a second wind and that she is starting to live a second time. In general, in this documentary you will see that age is not an obstacle to live and work, and work heals and gives life. Also, Eva thanks everyone for watching and wishes you all health and longevity. This documentary is based on real events and has authorship.
