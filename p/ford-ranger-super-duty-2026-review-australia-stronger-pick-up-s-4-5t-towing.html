Ford Ranger Super Duty 2026 review Australia: Stronger pick-up's 4.5t towing blows Toyota HiLux away

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
The full review of the Ford Ranger Super Duty gives Australian buyers a clear look at this mid-size pick-up and how it fits into a crowded segment. Tom White walks through the range, covering key model grades, core equipment and design changes that shape its position against rivals such as the Toyota HiLux, Nissan Navara and Isuzu D-Max.

He examines cabin layout, storage, rear seat space and tray usability to show how the Ranger Super Duty handles day-to-day work and family duties. The review also outlines safety tech, infotainment features and towing claims to establish what owners can expect in real use.

Tom rounds out the test with driving impressions on sealed and unsealed roads, along with a clear view of fuel use and ride quality. Pricing and value discussion helps frame where the Ranger Super Duty sits in the market without leaning on assumptions.
