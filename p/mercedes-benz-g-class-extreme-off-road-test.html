Mercedes-Benz G-Class: Extreme Off-Road Test

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
In the summer of 2016, videographer Johannes Schlörb of fünfkommasechs and his friends were invited to experience the Mercedes-Benz G-Class on its original trial route near Graz, Austria. Erwin Wonisch himself, the legendary head of the G-Class testing team, took them on a journey up the Schöckl Mountain and down memory lane.
