Engine Block Line Boring and Align Honing Main Bearing Bores to Achieve Factory-Level Precision

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
In this video, we show the complete process of engine block line boring and align honing main bearing bores to achieve factory-level precision in engine rebuilding. Proper alignment of main bearing bores is critical for reducing friction, improving crankshaft rotation, and ensuring the long-term reliability of high-performance engines.
You’ll see step-by-step how machinists use specialized equipment for main bore alignment, ensuring exact tolerances that maximize engine performance, durability, and efficiency. Whether you’re rebuilding a diesel engine, gasoline engine, racing engine, or heavy-duty tractor motor, this process is key to preventing bearing failure and maintaining oil clearance accuracy.
This detailed demonstration is ideal for mechanics, machinists, automotive engineers, and anyone interested in professional engine machining techniques.
