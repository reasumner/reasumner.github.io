How To Make Tea Tree Soap | Learn To Make Your Own Tea Tree Soap At Home

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
If you love to make homemade soap from scratch and want to learn how to make tea tree soap, this DIY soap video is for you. We are using the natural cold process soap making method and have included the Tea Tree Soap recipe in the description for you below.

Tea tree essential oil is well known for its great antibacterial, antifungal, antiseptic properties and is popular in soaps. Sometimes when you are making handmade soap at home you just want a few bar soaps rather than really large slabs, so we have done a half-sized batch that will make 5 to 6 bars and uses 500g oil oils. If you want to make a larger amount you can simply double the recipe to make up a full kilo batch.

TEA TREE SOAP RECIPE

150g Coconut oil
100g Olive oil
100g Sweet almond oil
100g Cocoa butter
50g Shea butter
71g Sodium hydroxide (lye)
150g Distilled water
10g Tea tree essential oil
