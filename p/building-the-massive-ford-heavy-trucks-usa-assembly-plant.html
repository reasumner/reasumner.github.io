Building the Massive Ford Heavy Trucks - USA Assembly Plant

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
In this feature on FRAME, we will have a look at several Ford assembly plants where the pick-up truck models are manufactured. Join us to discover all the secrets about the Medium Duty 650, the Super Duty 2023, and the F-150.
