They Live Under a Bridge in Manila Philippines - Off the Beaten Path Walk [4K HDR]

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Living under a bridge in Tondo Manila Philippines. Take an off the beaten path walk around an unseen area in Tondo Manila Philippines. This community surprisingly lives under a bridge. They seem to be happy and content, in fact, I saw a big screen tv and a computer inside one of the homes. Although an informal settlement, they seem to live large with electricity and protection from the hot sun and downpours. I do believe that this area is prone to massive floods, so living here is no doubt a high risk. Aside from the bridge, we explore the surrounding areas as well. Enjoy!
