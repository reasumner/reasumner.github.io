Audi A5 (2025) - How the New A4 Was Born and How it is PRODUCED

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
2025 Audi A5 Sedan / A5 Avant Car production. #audi #audia5 #carfactory

Audi is opening the next chapter of its success story in the mid-size segment with the new A5 family. Launched 30 years after the first Audi A4, the sharp design language of the latest generation, now called the Audi A5, captivates with premium proportions. Both body styles, Sedan and Avant, perfectly embody the sporty essence of the Audi design philosophy. A new design language in the interior creates a generous feeling of space and places the displays on a digital stage. The new operating concept increases interaction with the vehicle. Efficient, partially electrified combustion engines and emotional S models round out the range.

With the expansion of its model range, AUDI AG has restructured the naming of its vehicles from 2024. The numbers in the names of the electric and combustion engine models are the differentiating element. In future, even numbers will indicate electrically powered models, odd numbers will indicate vehicles with a combustion engine. With the debut of the latest generation, the long-time bestseller Audi A4 will be called the Audi A5 and will be built in Neckarsulm. It is offered in four new variants: as the A5 Sedan and S5 Sedan2 and as the A5 Avant and S5 Avant1. These versions are the first models to be launched on the Premium Platform Combustion (PPC).

The new models are growing in length and width. Audi is also expanding its standard equipment, positioning the new Audi A5 in the upper mid-range segment. The new user experience based on version 1.2 of the E¬≥ electronic architecture fulfills this aspiration. The optional equipment is grouped into various packages.
Dynamic, muscular exterior design

Even at first glance, the completely redesigned A5 family has a powerful, clear design. The modified proportions with a long wheelbase, large wheels and the low, sporty body embody progressive dynamism and premium standards. In the sedan, the sporty, compact greenhouse extends to the rear in a wide arc, flows seamlessly into the low rear window in a coup√©-like manner and ends in the visually short tailgate with a striking spoiler lip.

The tailgate, new in this vehicle segment, offers a convincing symbiosis of design and functionality. It opens with the rear window and its dimensions make access to the luggage compartment significantly easier. This results in a completely new sedan concept for Audi. In the Avant, the dynamic, taut roof line merges into a perfectly integrated roof spoiler that extends across the sporty, flat rear window. The steeply raked D-pillars emphasize the dynamic side view of the A5 Avant.

The front is dominated by the wide and significantly flatter Singleframe with a three-dimensional honeycomb structure. Together with the slim, precisely drawn headlights, it shapes the face of the vehicle and gives it a striking, concentrated expression.

The striking and distinctive rear combines sporty and emotional design with intelligent technology. A combination of sculptural geometry and a continuous, three-dimensional, staggered light strip gives the rear of the new Audi A5 presence and visual dynamism.
