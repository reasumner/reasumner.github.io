DELICIOUS MUSHROOM CAVIAR WITH GARLIC! This video shows a VIDEO recipe for making a great COLD APPETIZER for a festive table! Cooking caviar from OPYAT is a simple and not complicated process, and the result will be appreciated by ALL YOUR GUESTS. INGREDIENTS:
Mushrooms mushrooms - 1 kg;
Onion - 1-2 PCs.;
Carrots - 1-2 PCs.
Garlic - 3-6 cloves;
Salt, Pepper and Vegetable oil.
Recipe: boil the Mushrooms for 15 minutes, twist them in a meat grinder. Cut the onion into small pieces, and three carrots on a grater. Heat the pan, add the vegetable oil, then add the onion and pass it, add the carrots and fry everything together in the pan (5-10 minutes on medium heat). Add the twisted honey mushrooms to the passioned onions and carrots, mix everything and fry. Add salt and pepper to taste, fry for another 5-10 minutes and remove the pan from the heat.
Let the whole mass cool down well, and then squeeze out the garlic. Mix everything well and let it brew (1-2 hours), so that the garlic gives its smell and taste to the mushrooms. Feeding a snack it is best cold! Spread caviar on bread and YOU will NEVER FORGET this taste! BON APPETIT!
