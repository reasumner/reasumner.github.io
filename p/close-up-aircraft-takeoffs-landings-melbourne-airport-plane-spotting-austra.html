FEATURED AIRCRAFT: A380s, 747s, A350s, B777s, A330s, B787s & MORE!

Welcome back to Australia's second busiest airport, Melbourne Tullamarine Airport. This video focuses on highlights I have filmed this month. December has started off strong with new flights by Delta Airlines to Los Angeles 3x a week, China Airlines have upgraded their flights to 777s, Qantas have been running some of their singapore flights with A380s. Along with this, we have Shenzhen Airlines and Hong Kong Airlines starting flights in a weeks time. Emirates have been sending a lot of their special A380s on Melbourne flights, Qatar sent the new F1 liveried 777 on the forst Virgib Australia code share flight and its that season to try and catch the Asiana and Turkish A350s in the last rays of daylight! Suffice to say, it's been a busy month.

This video also features some airside footage I was able to get whilst being right beside the runway for the Delta inaugural flights!
