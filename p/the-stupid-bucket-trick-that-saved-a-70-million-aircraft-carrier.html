The "Stupid" Bucket Trick That Saved a $70 Million Aircraft Carrier

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
The USS Bunker Hill was the most advanced warship ever built. But after two Kamikaze hits, its pumps failed, and it began to sink. Modern technology had failed. The engineers were out of ideas.

Then, a 21-year-old sailor raised his hand with an idea so "idiotic," officers almost threw him out of the room: "Why don't we use buckets?"

This is the incredible true story of how Seaman Joseph McCaffrey organized a "human pump" to save a burning super-carrier. Discover how a primitive bucket brigade beat the failure of modern engineering and proved that sometimes, the smartest solution is the one that looks the most stupid.
