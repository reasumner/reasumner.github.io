Honda CRV 1.5L Engine Teardown After 30,000 Miles With No Oil Changes

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
What happens if you just... don't change your oil, ever?
The owner of this 2018 Honda CRV found out the expensive way!
Once purchased used with about 120K miles, the owner drove this car without changing the oil, EVER! They drove this car until it barely pulled itself into the driveway.
Today we are tearing down the L15BE 1.5L Turbo engine from that 2018 CRV with 153K miles. So how did this engine fair in the depths of neglect? Thats what we find out as we tear this engine all the way down to see the extent of the damage caused by not changing oil.
