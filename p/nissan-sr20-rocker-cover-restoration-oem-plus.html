Nissan SR20 Rocker Cover Restoration ( OEM PLUS )

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
Well as the title says this weeks video is extremely satisfying so sit back relax and enjoy as a transform this old and scabby black top SR20 cam cover into a gorgeous NEO VVL cam cover.

So the first job was to get the cover all cleaned up by washing it in the parts washer to remove all of the old oil and carbon deposits that had built up not to mention all the grease and dirt that was on the outside too. After drying the cover off it was taken into the spray booth and covered in paint stripper to remove the original paint finish one this had done its work it was cleaned and dried once again.

Then it was into the media blaster to blast the surface back to clean metal as there was a lot of corrosion lurking in the crevice's, I used garnet for this process. After blasting it was then over to the work bench to heat and remove the vent pipes from the cover this is a fairly easy process as long as you heat the cam cover up to a suitable temperature for it to release from the steel pipes.

I then loaded the cover into the booth again this time for a coat of etch primer followed up by 3 of high build so that i can flat back any defects. While the primer was curing i set about cleaning up the vent pipes with the sketchy bench grinder ready for a fresh coat of zinc to protect them from corrosion, i went for a clear passivate rather than the standard yellow as i feel the silver tones in better with there rest of the colors.

Once the zinc plating was done it was time to wet flat the primer in preparation for the cam covers fresh coat of metallic blue paint, it got 5 coats of blue for a nice deep finish and then was removed from the booth so that i could clean up the lettering and apply the OEM finish again. The cover was then put back into the booth and clear coated for that ultra glossy finish.

Then it was simply a case of re-assembling the cover and applying my custom made NEO VVL plate.

So anyway I hope you enjoy this little restoration and will join me again next time for another fun filled project.
