This line has been largely overhauled and track is covered with fresh white ballast on some parts. Although it looks like train could travel much faster, that's not the case. The repaired sections of the railway line have not been officially handed over from the contractors to the Serbian Railways. These are standard and very common problems of Serbian and other Balkan railways as well. Trains must run slowly for days and months until all legal issues are resolved. Along the way, train pass many signals indicating that further driving is prohibited. In most cases, this is a common situation because the railway stations along the way have long been closed and abandoned. Unfortunately, carelessness, neglect, non-maintenance of railway infrastructure and abandonment of tracks and stations are increasingly present on railways in this region.
