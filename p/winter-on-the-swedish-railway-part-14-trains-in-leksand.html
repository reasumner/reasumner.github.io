Winter on the Swedish Railway part 14 - Trains in Leksand

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
Filmed in Leksand, in Dalarna, Sweden, on the 22nd of November 2025. Siljansbanan runs through Leksand, and connects Mora with Borlänge. The video contains the trains Rc6, X51, Rc4, Rd2 and engine 243. The trains were in service as SJ InterCity, Tåg i Bergslagen (TiB), Green Cargo freight train and Hector Rail freight train.
