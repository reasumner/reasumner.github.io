Forget all the recipes‚ùïThis is the easiest thing I've ever made‚ùï

Ingredients and recipe:
125 ml of warm milk.
125 ml of warm water.
40 g of sugar.
8 g of dry yeast.
mix well.
2 eggs.
7 g of vanilla sugar.
5 g of salt.
mix well.
550 g of wheat flour.
mix well with a spatula.
60 g of butter.
cover and let rise for an hour.
140 g of ground walnuts.
40 g of brown sugar.
mix well.
Divide the dough into 2 pieces, one larger and one smaller.
Cut the dough into 5 pieces.
Form each piece of dough into a ball.
Cut the dough into 6 pieces.
Form each piece of dough into a ball.
cover and leave for 10 minutes.
Roll out the dough with a rolling pin.
Grease the surface with butter.
add the nut mixture.
Do the same with the remaining 5 pieces of dough.
divided into 8 pieces.
Turn the dough over as I show you.
Place the other 6 dough balls in the middle.
cover and leave for 20 minutes.
Mix an egg yolk with a tablespoon of milk.
Decorate with granulated sugar on top.
Bake at 180 degrees Celsius for 30 minutes.
