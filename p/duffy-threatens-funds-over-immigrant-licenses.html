Duffy threatens funds over immigrant licenses

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
U.S. Transportation Secretary Sean Duffy threatened NY federal funds over immigrant licenses. Duffy warned the federal government will take tens of millions of dollars from New York state if they do not 'comply' with rules related to granting commercial driver licenses.
