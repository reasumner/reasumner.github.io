Cadillac Escalade ESV vs NEW 2024 Mercedes-Benz GLS580 - WHO TAKES THE V8 CROWN?

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
The newly refreshed 2024 Mercedes-Benz GLS580 is the German automaker's flagship luxury SUV and today we are pitting it against the king, the Cadillac Escalade, to see which of these two fullsize three-row SUVs is the ultimate luxury family hauler. The 2024 Cadillac Escalade comes equipped with a 6.2L V8, while the GLS580 packs a Twin Turbo 4.0L V8 - enough to make both of these behemoths move rather rapidly.

But with the GLS having just been refreshed and the Escalade still unchanged until the 2025 Escalade will bring a refresh, this might be the best opportunity that Mercedes gets to compete against Cadillac's flagship SUV. With a revised interior and exterior, MBUX, and plenty of luxury tech the GLS want's to take the throne. Which $150,000+ SUV (as tested here in Canada) is the best?
