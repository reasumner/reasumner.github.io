US FORCES AMBUSHED IN SYRIA, RUSSIA SLAMMED IN COUNTEROFFENSIVE! Breaking War News W/ The Enforcer

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
US Forces have been ambushed in Palmyra, Syria with 3 US soldiers now reported dead. The United States has announced immediate reciprocal action against ISIS who was responsible for the ambush earlier in the day. Large numbers of US forces by land and air have been conducting major actions in the area in response, hoping to destroy the ISIS cell responsible for the heinous attack outright. Ukraine has launched a new counteroffensive in Kupyansk against Russian forces, with the counteroffensive appearing to have wildly positive outcomes in the opening hours; Russian forces are now threatened with encirclement in the city. Russia has also been hit heavily overnight by a major drone and missile attack conducted by Ukraine which hit a broad variety of targets throughout the entirety of western Russia. The US is still keeping a larger air presence around Venezuela. The next day has come and passed in Thailand, and it has become clear that there is no ceasefire that was going into effect, with Thai and Cambodian forces conducting significant offensives throughout the day, with the Thai Air Force striking key targets and command posts at their leisure.
