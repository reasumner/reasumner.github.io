In this video, we take you through the journey of swapping the frame of a classic 1966 Chevrolet C10 truck with a 2001 Chevy Tahoe frame! After the hard work of fitting the modern frame, we wrap up the build by troubleshooting and fixing any issues that came up during the swap. Once everything is dialed in, we hit the road for a relaxing drive to enjoy the results of our labor.

What to expect in this video:

The process of frame swapping a 1966 C10 onto a 2001 Tahoe frame
Overcoming challenges, solving problems, and troubleshooting
Final adjustments and testing the truck on the road
A smooth drive with all the new updates and improvements!
