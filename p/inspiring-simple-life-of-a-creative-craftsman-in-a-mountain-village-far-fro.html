Inspiring Simple Life of a Creative Craftsman in a Mountain Village Far From Civilization

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Meet Dmytro, a 67-year-old craftsman and creative soul who lives in the charming mountain village of Smerechka, Sambir district in Ukraine. In this video, we will share a part of his fascinating life, full of art, traditions and inspiration. Find out how the nature of the Carpathians, craft and love of creativity shape this man's unique world. Feel the atmosphere of the picturesque village and discover a real source of inspiration!
