The 2024 Mercedes-AMG S63 E Performance packs a punch like no other S63 that has come before it. With the assistance of electric motors, it achieves 791 horsepower and 1,055 lb ft of torque from it’s V8. All of that wrapped up neatly in a limousine package.

But, it’s not without rival. Audi has the already very established S8, and BMW has their new i7 M70. James and Thomas take the S63 for a drive, and then meet the Audi and BMW for a final showdown at the strip. We hope you enjoy the episode! Subscribe!
