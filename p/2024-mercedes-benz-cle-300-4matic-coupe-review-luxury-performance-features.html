2024 Mercedes-Benz CLE 300 4MATIC Coupe Review | Luxury Performance & Features Breakdown | 2024 CLE

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Get an in-depth look at the 2024 Mercedes-Benz CLE 300 4MATIC Coupe in this features breakdown review. Explore the stunning design and top-of-the-line features of the 2024 CLE model.
The 2024 CLE 300 4MATIC Coupe offers seating for 4 passengers and is equipped with a 2.0L Inline-4 turbo engine featuring a mild hybrid drive, producing 255 horsepower at 5,800 rpm. It comes with a 9G-TRONIC 9-speed automatic transmission, providing seamless shifting and enhanced performance. The coupe accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in 6.2 seconds and offers a cargo capacity of 11.2 cubic feet. In terms of fuel efficiency, it delivers 24 mpg in the city and 34 mpg on the highway.
