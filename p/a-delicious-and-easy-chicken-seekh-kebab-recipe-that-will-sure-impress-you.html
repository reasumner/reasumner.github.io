Indulge in the aromatic delight of Chicken Seekh Kabab, a tantalizing dish that seamlessly blends succulent chicken with a medley of spices, expertly cooked on a hot tawa. This recipe promises to elevate your culinary experience with every bite.

Ingredients:

For the Seekh Kabab:

500g boneless chicken
Ginger, garlic cloves, and 1 onion, finely minced
2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
Fresh coriander, chopped
1 tsp salt (or to taste)
1 tsp red chili powder
1 tsp coriander powder
1 tsp cumin powder
1/4 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp garam masala
1 tsp red chili flakes
1 tbsp desi ghee
3-4 tbsp oil for frying

For the Green Chutney:

1 cup plain yogurt
Fresh coriander, chopped
Mint leaves
1-2 green chilies
Salt to taste
