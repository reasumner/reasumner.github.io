Old Russia 1930s in color [60fps, Remastered] w/sound design added

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
I colorized, restored, and created a sound design for this home movie of a tour in old Russia in the 1930s. a rare window into daily life at the time: citizens walking through lively streets, streetcars rattling past wooden carts pulled by horses, and uniformed soldiers mingling with ordinary people. Markets and small shops line the avenues, where men and women can be seen exchanging goods, chatting, and carrying baskets of produce. captures the rhythm of a society in transition, with both tradition and modernity visible side by side. Horse-drawn carriages share space with early automobiles, trams move steadily through busy intersections, and families stroll together while children play nearby. The blend of military presence, working-class activity, and urban commerce gives an authentic glimpse into the everyday atmosphere of Russia during this fascinating decade.
