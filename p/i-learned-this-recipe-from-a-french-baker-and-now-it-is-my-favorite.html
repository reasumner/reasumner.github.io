I learned this Recipe from a French Baker and now it is My Favorite!

üö©Ingredients and recipe:
300 ml of warm milk.
60 g of sugar.
8 g of dry yeast.
mix well.
1 egg.
20 ml of vegetable oil.
5 g of salt.
mix.
550 g of flour.
mix well with a spatula.
30 g of butter.
cover with foil and let rise for 1 hour.
100 g ground walnuts.
60 g of brown sugar.
1 teaspoon of cinnamon.
mix.
Divide the dough into 4 equal parts.
cover and leave for 10 minutes.
grease with butter.
add the nut mixture.
Make 12 cuts.
cover and leave for 20 minutes.
Brush with an egg.
Bake at 180 degrees Celsius for 20 minutes.
