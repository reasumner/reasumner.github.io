Why is it "IMPOSSIBLE" for Humans to Reach MARS | Michio Kaku

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
Physicist Michio Kaku reveals the shocking truth about Mars missions: with current technology, sending humans to the Red Planet might be genuinely impossible. In this eye-opening 2-hour analysis, discover why Mars remains frustratingly out of reach despite decades of planning.
The challenges are staggering: 6-9 months of deadly cosmic radiation exposure with no protection from Earth's magnetosphere, catastrophic bone and muscle loss from prolonged microgravity, psychological breakdown from extreme isolation, and a communication delay that makes real-time help from Earth impossible.
Professor Kaku examines why proposed solutions fall short: water shielding is too heavy, artificial gravity requires massive rotating structures we've never built, and chemical rockets are fundamentally too slow and inefficient for safe Mars travel.
Even landing on Mars presents an unsolved engineering problem‚Äîthe atmosphere is too thin for parachutes but too thick to ignore, and we've never successfully landed the massive payloads a human mission requires.
This is the uncomfortable reality NASA and SpaceX don't often discuss: the gap between our capabilities and what Mars demands might be unbridgeable with near-term technology.
Topics covered:

Why radiation exposure could be lethal
The unsolved entry, descent, and landing problem
Microgravity's devastating effects on the human body
The psychological impossibility of 900-day isolation
Why chemical rockets can't get us there safely
In-situ resource utilization challenges
The brutal reality of the Martian surface
Why Mars might require 50-100 more years of development
