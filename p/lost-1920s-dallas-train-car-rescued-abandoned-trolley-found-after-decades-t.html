LOST 1920s Dallas Train Car Rescued | ABANDONED Trolley Found After Decades | Turnin Rust

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
After stumbling across a one-of-a-kind piece of history, the guys travel deep into north Texas to explore this 1920s Dallas/Fort Worth trolley. This rare gem has been hidden from the public for years waiting for its story to be told. The team attempts to see what it would take to load this HEAVY railcar on to the trailer for one last ride down the road. With just dad and son, will the two be able to safely get this ol' girl chuggin' back to the shop or has this iron giant grown permanent roots where she sits?
