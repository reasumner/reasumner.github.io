This video shows you how to install a camshaft position sensor on your 2001-2005 Honda Civic. For vehicles with electronically controlled timing, a camshaft position sensor relays important information to the computer to ensure the engine is running properly. A bad sensor may relay incorrect or no information, resulting in poor running conditions and trouble codes.

This repair was done on a 2001 Honda Civic EX 1.7L Sedan 4-Door FWD and the process should be similar on the following vehicles:
2001 Honda Civic
2002 Honda Civic
2003 Honda Civic
2004 Honda Civic
2005 Honda Civic
