The Mercedes Hatchback | 2013 Mercedes-Benz B 250 Review

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
For Valentines Day TestDrive is in the Jupiter Red 2013 Mercedes-Benz B250 BlueEFFICIENCY, and we show you all the features of this W246 B-Class Mercedes. From it's 7-Speed automatic transmission, to the Driving Assistance Package, and Rear-View camera. We'll also go on a road test and give you our opinion about this car.

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz B250 might not be the car for everyone, but it was actually pretty surprisingly nice to drive. The last B-Class we drove was about a 8 years before this and we found it difficult to fit, but the latest generation B Class has a lot more space and might be a good option for people looking for a small car to get around town in.

Our full-tour and review of the 2013 Mercedes-Benz B250 covers all the exterior and interior features, such as the HID Xenon head lamps, leatherette seating, Comand navigation screen, and everything else optioned on this car.
