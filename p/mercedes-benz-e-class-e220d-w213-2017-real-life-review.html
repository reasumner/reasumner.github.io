The Mercedes-Benz E-Class is a range of executive cars manufactured by German automaker Mercedes-Benz in various engine and body configurations produced since 1993, marketed worldwide across five generations. The Mercedes-Benz E-Class (W213 and S213) series represent the fifth-generation of the Mercedes-Benz E-Class range of executive cars, including sedan (W213) and station wagon (S213) configurations. Sold from 2016 (as a 2017 model), it is the successor to the W212/S212 E-Class models. A high-performance E63 AMG version of the W213 and S213 has been available as well from 2016 (as a 2017 model).

Diesel: E 200, 4 cyl./in-line, 1991 cc, 184 hp power, 300 Nm torque, 9G-TRONIC transmission, 240 kmph top speed

Diesel: E 220 d, 4 cyl./in-line, 1950 cc, 194 hp power, 400 Nm torque, 9G-TRONIC transmission, 240 kmph top speed, 12 kmpl average

Diesel: E 350 d, V6, 2987 cc, 258 hp power, 620 Nm torque, 9G-TRONIC transmission, 250 kmph top speed

3 Variants: E 200, E 220 d, E 350 d

5 Colors: Black, Silver, Brown, Grey, White

Price range: Rs. 54.57 lakh - Rs. 67.24 lakh (Ex-showroom, New Delhi)
