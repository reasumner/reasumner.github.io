Abandoned VW GTI First Wash in 10 Years! Insane Barn Find Restoration

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
This is a 1991 MK2 GTI that was abandoned outside a barn for 10 years. My buddy Jeff at The Common Gear found it as I've been looking for the perfect GTI to add to the AMMO fleet. Over the course of 5 weeks, I document the process of a complete detail at the AMMO Studio, a full Montana Green repaint at J&B Bodyworks, Mechanical Restoration thanks to VW Garage, BillT Industries, Ace Performance, and FCP Euro. The interior and audio feature custom work from DID Upholstery and AutoMaxx. All with the goal to drive to the Sunday Motor Meet at Lime Rock Park. Enjoy the amazing work and showcase of automotive talent with some of the best tradesmen in the industry. Thank you to the entire J&B Bodyworks team, Cory, Bill, Tom, Tom Sr, Eric, John, Jade, Marissa, Chris, Yuchao, Jeff, Vredestein Tires, Milltek Sport, and FCP Euro.
