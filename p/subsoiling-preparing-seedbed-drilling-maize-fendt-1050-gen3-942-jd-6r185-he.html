Subsoiling - Preparing Seedbed - Drilling Maize | FENDT 1050 Gen3 - 942 - JD 6R185 | Heavy Clay Soil

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Subsoiling, Preparing Seedbed and Drilling Maize on the Agrimm farms near Amsterdam and Schiphol Airport in the Netherlands.
First we see a Fendt 942 Gen7 with 3.5mtr wide Sumo Trio subsoiler preparing the land, after that a Fendt 1050 vario Gen3 is preparing the seedbed for maize with a 7,5 meter wide Steketee front-cultivator and 8 meter wide Steketee Supersprint. When the seedbed is ready a John Deere 6R185 with Vaderstad 12 row planter comes to drill the maize on 50cm of row spacing.

Land klaarmaken en mais zaaien bij akkerbouwbedrijf Immink uit Amstelveen met hun Fendt 1050 met 8 meter brede Steketee Supersprint en een John Deere 6R185 met 12 rijige Vaderstad precisie zaaimachine.
