Getting My Ford All Bundled Up And Ready For Winter

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
This episode is a handful of stuff I wanted to get done on my daily driver 1965 Ford before winter gets here. I finally replaced my temporary fuel system so that no rubber lines would get snagged off. I built a skid plate incase I slip into a curb on the ice, and I dealt with a few other small things that needed to be done for a while. I also rustproofed it for the salty roads. Despite all my preparation, almost no snow has fallen here in Minnesota. Last year we ran out of space to put the dang stuff. Oh well.
