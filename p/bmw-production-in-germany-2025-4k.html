BMW Production in Germany [2025] [4K]

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
This is how BMW manufactures cars, SUVs, and EVs in Germany! Take a tour of some of BMW's largest factories in Europe, building the BMW 7 Series, BMW 5 Series, BMW 4 Series, BMW 3 Series and BMW 2 Series. We will also look at the production process of the BMW iX2 and iX electric SUVs and the BMW i4 electric saloon. And finally the manufacturing of components like EV motors, battery modules, and battery cells.

Factories shown: Dingolfing, Leipzig, Munich, Parsdorf and Regensburg.
