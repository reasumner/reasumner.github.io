Osaka's BMW Batmobile - The Coutner 6 Series

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Get your merch at: https://dinodc.co

How about this for something different, yet impossibly Japanese. On my latest trip to Osaka this wild E24 from Coutner Japan was one car that I had high on my list of rides to shoot. The big coupe is one of my favorite from BMW and what Watanabe-san, the owner and the man behind the brand, has done is simply stunning. Injecting some E9 CSL Group 5 vibes into the 635CSi has turned it into something very soecial.

Hit that play button and find out everything about the build!
