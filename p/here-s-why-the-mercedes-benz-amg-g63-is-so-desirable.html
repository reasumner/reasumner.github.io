Here’s Why The Mercedes Benz AMG G63 Is So Desirable

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
In this video I talk about why I think the Mercedes Benz AMG G63 is so desirable. The Mercedes G-Class has been around for several decades and Mercedes recently gave it a refresh. Beyond the new looks, the G63 still looks like an old school, military inspired, boxy SUV, which is a great thing. Its great because the AMG G63 G-Class looks like nothing else on the road and offer an experience that cannot be matched by the competition.

With its big, torquey, V8 and thunderous quad exhaust, the AMG G63 is also a very quick SUV. Given its size, it can definitely get up and go! The interior was also vastly improve in this generation and this example in the review has the most expensive interior upgrade you can get. Is the G-Glass AAMG G63 desirable?
