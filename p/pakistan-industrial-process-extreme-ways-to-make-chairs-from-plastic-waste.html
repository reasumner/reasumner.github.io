Pakistan Industrial Process - Extreme Ways to Make Chairs from Plastic Waste - @AsianWorker-v2g

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
In this video, we take a comprehensive look at the process of manufacturing plastic chairs in Pakistani industries. This process begins with the processing of plastic waste and used sacks, and ends with the recycling process, which then proceeds to the Pakistani manufacturing stage, which utilizes various modern machinery such as extruders, mixers, and injection molders. Pakistani workers process the used materials into high-quality plastic pellets before finally molding them into sturdy, market-ready chairs. This process demonstrates how Pakistani factories are able to create high-quality products using recycled materials and simple yet efficient technology. This is how to create a plastic chair that is truly "Made in Pakistan," and become part of the growing recycling business. @AsianWorker-v2g
