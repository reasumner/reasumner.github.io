HEMI Swapping My 1970 Dodge Challenger in 15 MINUTES!

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
This is my year-long 1970 Dodge Challenger build in just 15 minutes. I bought this glorious Mopar as a barely running shell in the Arizona desert and did a complete restoration of the body and interior. After that was done, I decided to embark on a gen 3 Hemi swap restored build. I pulled this 5.7L Hemi out of a 2005 Durango and stripped it down, cleaned it up, and upgraded the camshaft. I swapped the engine with a 6-speed manual transmission from a 2015 Challenger R/T and converted my car to manual. I rebuilt the Challengers rear diff with new gears and a True Trac post, added a front sway bar, and did some other suspension mods to get it track-ready. I had a custom drive shaft built for the TR6060 and set up the Hemi on a Holley Terminator X EFI ECU and harness. After a dyne tune, I took this bad boy on a 2,500 mile road trip up the west coast. Daily driver, drag racer, and track weapon.
