BRABUS 800

High-performance luxury SUV ‚Äì outstanding performance with strong, confident looks. The brand new BRABUS 800, based on the Mercedes-AMG GLS 63 4MATIC +, is the latest BRABUS super sport SUV that pushes the boundaries, characterized by an incomparable driving experience, a unique appearance and an extra spacious interior. Equipped and with a full carbon aerodynamic-enhancement concept, 24-inch forged wheels, a valve-controlled sports exhaust system for the fitting, acoustics, a tailor made BRABUS fine leather interior and a 4.0 liter twin-turbocharged V8 producing 588 kW / 800 hp, this hallmark BRABUS conversion leaves nothing to be desired!

Design & Exterior.

The carbon exterior design of the BRABUS 800 impresses with its immediate wow-factor. In addition, the characteristic exposed carbon body parts ensure optimal aerodynamics. In the front, the BRABUS front spoiler gives the BRABUS 800 an unmistakable face and reduces front-axle lift at high speeds, resulting in improved handling stability. In the rear, the BRABUS diffusor insert perfectly highlights the four BRABUS carbon tailpipes while adding to the optimal aerodynamic balance. Optimized air inlets on the sides of the front bumper round off the sporty appearance. The air inlets in the radiator grille as well as an illuminated BRABUS emblem in combination with the red signature stripes ensure the unmistakable BRABUS look.

Power & Sound.

Prestigious performance ‚Äì the BRABUS 800 is a real luxury mega liner, a vehicle for every purpose, a daily driver with the performance of a supercar and space for up to 7 people. With its extremely high-performance 4.0 liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine and special high-performance turbochargers, this super SUV produces 588 kW / 800 PS and thus offers excellent performance. The 1,000 Nm power is transmitted via a nine-speed automatic transmission, which can be shifted either automatically or manually ‚Äì through it, the BRABUS 800 accelerates from 0 to 100 km / h in just 3.8 seconds. The top speed is electronically limited to 280 km / h.

Additionally, the BRABUS quad stainless-steel high-performance exhaust system with actively controlled valves ensures the proper acoustics and further perfects power delivery by reducing the exhaust backpressure. It offers active sound management by enabling the driver to switch between a throaty V8 in ‚ÄòSport‚Äô mode and a subtle whisper in ‚ÄòComing Home‚Äô mode at the touch of button.

Wheels & Chassis.

The bigger, the better ‚Äì The exclusive design of the BRABUS 800 is emphasized by newly developed, high-tech 24-inch BRABUS Monoblock "PLATINUM EDITION" M forged wheels, which, through the use of state-of-the-art manufacturing processes, offer a unique look and maximum strength. In combination with the BRABUS suspension module, this power SUV is as agile as it is stunning, underlined by limitless driving fun. The optimal high-performance tires are provided by BRABUS technology partner: Continental.

Interior.

Matching the sporty, elegant appearance and the uncompromising performance of the BRABUS 800, the interior of this extra-spacious sports SUV is also in a league of its own, characterized by a tailor-made BRABUS Fine Leather design.Our master upholsters created an impeccable design from especially soft and breathable black leather with gray contrasting topstitching matching the black paint finish. The seat surfaces and inner sections of the door panels were quilted in our unique Arrow Wave design with pinpoint precision. The meticulously perforated sections are backed with red fabric to match the red BRABUS highlights on the engine cover and the bodywork, giving the interior a very special look.

Furthermore, the sporty characteristics of the BRABUS 800 are emphasized with naked-carbon elements on the dashboard and door panels as well as with aluminum pedal and door pins. Special aluminum paddle shifters on the steering wheel in BRABUS RACE design make choosing the right gear even more precise when operating the SPEEDSHIFT nine-speed automatic transmission manually. Naturally, the interior design can be customized based entirely on personal preference!
