Fish pulao recipe
Recipe in English:


Ingredients
- 1-1/2 kg fish
- 1-1/2 tsp salt each side
- 2 tsp garlic paste
- Then put in the fridge for 2 hours
- first fish marination
- 3 tbsp vinegar
- 2 tbsp garlic paste
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp turmeric powder
- 4 tsp coriander powder
- 1 tbsp cumin powder
- 1 tbsp red chilli powder
- 2 tbsp chicken tikka masala
- Mix well
- Pinch of red food colour
- 1 tsp mix spices powder
- 1/4 cup of water
- Marinate the fish with mixture properly
- Add cooking oil
- fry on mid flame
- Fish ready
