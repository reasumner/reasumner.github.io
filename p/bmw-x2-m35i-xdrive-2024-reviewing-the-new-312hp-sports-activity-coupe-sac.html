BMW X2 M35i xDrive 2024: Reviewing The New 312HP Sports Activity Coupe (SAC)!

Description

The 2024 BMW X2 M35i xDrive (U10) is completely redesigned, transforming into a true Sports Activity Coupe with an aggressive, sloping roofline. As the performance flagship, its 2.0-liter M TwinPower Turbo engine delivers 312 horsepower to all four wheels via xDrive, achieving 0-60 mph in a quick 5.2 seconds (approx.). We examine the M-specific upgrades, including the quad exhaust tips, adaptive M suspension, and M Sport brakes. Inside, the M35i features the latest Curved Display and iDrive 9 software. Find out if the new design and potent performance make this compact crossover a compelling choice over rivals like the Mercedes-AMG GLA 35.
