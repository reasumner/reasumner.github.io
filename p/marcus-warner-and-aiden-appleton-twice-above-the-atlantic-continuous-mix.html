Marcus Warner and Aiden Appleton - Twice Above the Atlantic (Continuous Mix)

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
Five years in the making, Twice Above the Atlantic is a coming-of-age documentary celebrating the spirit of youth, challenges of travel, and pursuit of the great American road trip. This is the soundtrack, written in collaboration with long-time friend Aiden Appleton, featuring eight artists from across the US, UK, and Canada. The film is scheduled to come out later this year.

Stream/Download: http://marcuswarner.com/twiceabovetheatlantic.html

I wanted each to give me the feeling of being back there in some small way, as my year in America is a story (and time in my life) that is very special to me, and there are times when I miss it beyond belief. Hopefully this is embodied in the music you are listening to and if so, I hope you enjoy my year in America.
