Published at : 13 Dec 2025
1 Hour 4K Plane Spotting Compilation at Vancouver Airport. Watch Airplane Landings and Takeoffs from very close up. YVR is the second busiest Airport in Canada and is Air Canada's Asia/Oceania gateway. Aircraft types you will see at Vancouver Airport include: B747's, B777's, B787's, B767's, B757's, B737's, A340's, A350's, A330's, A320 family, Dash 8's, CRJ's, Ejet's and more. Active runways: 08L, 08R (some clips may had 26 ops over the city).
