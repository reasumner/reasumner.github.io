This 12,000 HP Race Car Clutch Must Be Rebuilt In 1 Hour!

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Join us at the 2025 New England Nationals in Epping, NH, as Chad Green's Nitro Funny Car team works hard to get their car back in race-ready condition. This video primarily focuses on what goes into rebuilding the nitro funny car clutch, giving viewers an up-close and detailed look at the entire process. Experience the intense behind-the-scenes action as the team trades parts and makes precise adjustments, showcasing the craftsmanship and teamwork involved in motorsports. Don't miss this in-depth look at the effort that goes into preparing a top-tier Nitro Funny Car for race day!
