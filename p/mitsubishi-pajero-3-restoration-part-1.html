Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Welcome.
Please see my first movie ever. During this series I will try to restore Mitubishi Pajero 3, 2004 model with 3.2 DID engine and 5 speed automatic gearbox.
In this episode I will do the basics, exchange power steering pump, change oils in transfer case, gearbox and differentials.
