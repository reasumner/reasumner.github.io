Development Mercedes-Benz M-class w163

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
This video shows the process of the development of first-generation Mercedes-Benz M-Class w163 - design process, assembly of prototypes aerodynamic tests, system tests, crash tests, driving tests of prototypes. Hundreds of hours, years of work spent on something that was born the new model Mercedes.
