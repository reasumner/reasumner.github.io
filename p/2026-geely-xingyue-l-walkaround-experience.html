2026 Geely Xingyue L Walkaround Experience

The 2026 Geely Xingyue L, also known as the Geely Monjaro in certain export markets, is a versatile SUV offered in both pure gasoline and hybrid powertrains with options for front-wheel drive and all-wheel drive. The gasoline lineup includes a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine co-developed with Volvo, available in two versions: the 2.0TD-T4 Evo, producing 218 hp and paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission for FWD models, and the more powerful 2.0TD-T5, which delivers 238 hp with an Aisin 8-speed automatic transmission for AWD models. Hybrid options include the Hi-X with a 1.5-liter three-cylinder engine and electrification for 242 hp, the Hi-P plug-in hybrid with a 41.2 kWh battery providing 207.5 km of pure EV range under WLTC, and the Dongfangyao hybrid variant featuring a 2.0TD engine with electric motors for a total of 272 PS, available in both FWD and AWD setups.

Built on the Compact Modular Architecture (CMA) platform co-developed with Volvo, the Xingyue L balances modern design with strong structural engineering. Measuring 4,770 mm in length, 1,895 mm in width, and 1,689 mm in height with a wheelbase of 2,845 mm, the SUV offers a commanding presence. It features a bold vertical grille, sleek LED headlamps, and a panoramic sunroof. Handling is enhanced with a MacPherson strut suspension at the front and an independent multi-link system at the rear, while the intelligent BorgWarner 6th-generation AWD system provides a torque split capability of up to 50:50 for improved off-road and all-weather performance.

Inside, the Xingyue L emphasizes premium comfort and advanced technology. The cabin is powered by Geely’s Galaxy OS 2.0, running on a Qualcomm Snapdragon 8155 chip, supporting multi-screen connectivity, including an optional IMAX-style panoramic triple-screen setup. A 10-speaker BOSE sound system with headrest speakers and advanced soundproofing elevates the audio experience, while customizable 72-color ambient lighting and eco-friendly interior materials add to the luxury. Seating includes heated and ventilated multi-functional seats with driver memory functions, accommodating up to five passengers in a spacious SUV layout. With 26 smart storage compartments, the Xingyue L combines practicality, cutting-edge features, and refined luxury.
