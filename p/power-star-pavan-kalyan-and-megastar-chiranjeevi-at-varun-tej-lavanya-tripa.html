Power star Pavan Kalyan and Megastar Chiranjeevi at Varun Tej Lavanya Tripathi Wedding #varunlav

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Power star Pavan Kalyan and Megastar Chiranjeevi attended Varun Tej and Lavanya Tripathi wedding.

Mega Prince Varun Tej, son of Nagababu and Padmaja Konidela, and Lavanya Tripathi, daughter of Deoraj and Kiran Tripathi, have got married today. It’s a splendid and majestic destination wedding at the Borgo San Felice Resort in Tuscany, Italy.

For the wedding, Varun Tej dressed in a cream-gold sherwani that was designed by Manish Malhotra. Lavanya Tripathi wore a Kanchipuram saree designed by Manish Malhotra. Ashwin Mawle and Hassan Khan have done the styling for the couple.

Megastar Chiranjeevi, Power Star Pawan Kalyan, Meha Paower Star Ram Charan, Icon Star Allu Arjun, Sai Dharam Tej, Allu Sirish, Vaishnav Tej, Nithiin and their respective families attended the wedding which was graced by the entire mega family, Lavanya’s family, and also close friends of the couple.

Varun and Lavanya met for the first time during the shooting of the Telugu film, Mister in 2017. They became friends and gradually came close to each other. Both are well-known faces of the Telugu film industry.

Post the wedding, a reception will take place in late evening, which has been themed as glitz and glamour. The guest list has around 120 people including Varun and Lavanya’s relatives and friends. The guest list also includes superstar Pawan Kalyan and Chiranjeevi.
