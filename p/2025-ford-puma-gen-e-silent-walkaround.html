2025 Ford Puma Gen-E | silent walkaround

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
This is a detailed, silent walkaround of the brand new 2025 Ford Puma Gen-E. Using a head-mounted camera for a realistic first-person perspective, we explore every angle of this exciting new electric crossover in a standard trim level. No talking, just the pure visual and sound experience of the car.

We'll cover the redesigned exterior, dive into the practical and modern interior, and check out passenger space in the rear. A key highlight for any EV is storage, so we'll take a close look at the massive rear boot with its famous GigaBox and, of course, the new front trunk (frunk).

This video is perfect for anyone wanting a close and honest look at what the base model of the electric Puma offers. Don't forget to like, subscribe to CzechCarGuy, and let me know your opinion in the comments!
