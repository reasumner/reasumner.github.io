2007 Hampton Bay Landmark Plus (remake), which is no longer in here

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
UPDATE: This fan has been replaced with Seagull Lightig Main Street, as of Summer 2015: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SZxeS82kB38, unfortunately the comment section on this video is gonna remain disabled too, but the video here will stay public (especially because I set my older video of this fan private, because of the camera quality and buzzing sound to combine with eachother which I yet feel like it'd be better not to let any new fan collectors see anymore, as of Summer 2020).

Recorded on July 22 2012) This is a remake video of my big sister's Hampton Bay Landmark Plus 42" white ceiling fan in her room, which was dated May of 2007, and my dad and I bought it from the Home Depot about like a year after this fan was made, and this fan has been there for like a little much more than 4 years now.

I did a remake video of it, because on my first video of this fan running on all 3 speeds, the camera capture motion is not really great (although I can't believe it's so popular on YouTube, lol). Anyways, I reversed it (while it's on high) for the video (for a lot of people who are interested to see it).

It's no longer in this room anymore, it's been replaced with my old ceiling fan (the Encon Monarch), as of July 26th (at night), and much better than this fan.

For iluupoohbear97: I hope you like this video better than my first video of it running on all speeds. :D
