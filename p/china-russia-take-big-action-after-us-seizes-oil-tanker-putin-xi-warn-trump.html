China & Russia Take BIG ACTION After US SEIZES Oil Tanker... Putin & Xi Warn Trump

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
The United States has seized a Venezuelan oil tanker in a dramatic escalation of Trump's pressure campaign against President Maduro. China's Foreign Ministry immediately condemned the action as "illegal unilateral sanctions" lacking any basis in international law. Hours later, Vladimir Putin called Maduro directly, reaffirming Russia's full strategic support and pledging to accelerate joint energy and economic projects. As Washington tightens its grip on Venezuela's oil lifeline, Moscow and Beijing are closing ranks in open defiance.
