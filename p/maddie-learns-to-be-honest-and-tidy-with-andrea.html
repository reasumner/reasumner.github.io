Maddie Learns to Be Honest and Tidy with Andrea

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Andrea and Maddie are in for an adventure you won't want to miss! Maddie finds herself in a bit of a pickle when she tries to avoid cleaning up by telling a fib. Uh-oh! Andrea, being the wise and caring friend she is, helps Maddie realize that lying isn't the way to go. Instead, Maddie learns the important lesson that honesty is the best policy, and cleaning up is part of being responsible. By the end, not only is the room sparkling clean, but Maddie's conscience is too!

Lesson Learned:
The big takeaway from this episode is that honesty is really important. Lying can create more problems than it solves, and it's always better to be upfront and take responsibility for your actions.
