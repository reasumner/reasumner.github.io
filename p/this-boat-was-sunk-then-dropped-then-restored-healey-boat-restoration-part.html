This Boat Was SUNK then DROPPED then RESTORED | Healey Boat Restoration Part 10

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
Take a look through the process of restoring this classic Healey Boat that was sunk for a long period of time, then dropped on a concrete floor and then poorly repaired as an insurance job.

This video runs through the entire process of its restoration from a major structural re build to a re finish, including the installation of a new deck and fittings. Follow the journey of the boat being brought back from the brink of destruction!
