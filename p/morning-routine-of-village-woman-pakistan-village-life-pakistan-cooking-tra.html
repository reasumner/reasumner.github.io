Morning Routine of Village Woman Pakistan | Village Life Pakistan | Cooking Traditional Breakfast

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
This video shows the ancient village life of desert village Pakistan in 4K 60FPS VIDEO QUALITY. I also Pervide SUBTITES in Differnt Languages.
In this video, a tour of a desert town has been done.People still live here in mud houses.This area is very close to Pakistan india border. People here still cook ancient traditional food.
In this video, especially the morning routine of the desert woman is also shown.A desert woman makes butter and lassi from fresh curd first thing in the morning routine.Making fresh butter and lassi is a daily routine in the desert village which these people eat for breakfast.The drink that remains after making butter from curd is called "Lassi" in the local language.After making butter, the old desert woman makes fresh parathas for the children and the members of her family. Sometimes these people also make a sweet recipe for breakfast that includes sweet rice.These people raise a large number of livestock in the desert, including cows, sheep, goats, etc.They obtain milk from their animals and then sell the milk to meet their needs.They obtain milk from their animals and then sell the milk to meet their needs.
