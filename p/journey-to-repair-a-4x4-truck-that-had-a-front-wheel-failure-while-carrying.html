Journey To Repair A 4x4 Truck That Had A Front Wheel Failure While Carrying A Load

During a transport trip, the truck suddenly suffered a critical failure: the front drive shaft broke, causing the wheel to fall off right on the road. This dangerous breakdown left the vehicle unable to move and required immediate on-site repair.

In this video, we document the entire emergency rescue process — from assessing the damage, recovering the detached wheel, removing damaged parts, welding and reinforcing the broken drive shaft, to reinstalling components and making sure the truck can safely continue its journey.

If you are interested in real mechanical work, heavy-duty truck repairs, and authentic roadside rescue missions, this video is for you.
Work Included:

Assess front drive shaft failure
Recover fallen wheel
Remove damaged shaft and hub assembly
Welding and strengthening the broken shaft
Reinstallation of bearings, bolts, and alignment
Final safety check before getting the truck back on the road
