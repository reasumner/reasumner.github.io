Diy Duil DC Motor Project | Motor Throw The Stick #shorts #trending #dcmotor

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
In today's DIY project, we will be using two DC motors to create a dual motor system that can throw a stick. This project is perfect for anyone looking to experiment with motor control and create a fun outdoor activity.

Materials Needed:
- 2x DC motors
- 3.7V battery
- Stick (for throwing)

* Connect the two DC motors to the motor. Make sure to connect the positive and negative terminals of each motor to the corresponding terminals on the controller

**Power the Arduino board using the 3.7V battery.

*** Attach the stick to the motors using a secure mounting system. You may need to create a mechanism that allows the stick to be thrown when the motors are activated.

****Test the system by activating the motors and observing how far the stick is thrown. You may need to adjust the motor speed and direction to achieve the desired throw distance.

And there you have it, a DIY dual DC motor project that can throw a stick. Have fun experimenting with motor control and creating your own outdoor activity with this project!
