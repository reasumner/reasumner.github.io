As BMW celebrates 50 years of automotive production in Lower Bavaria in 2023, the Dingolfing Plant has introduced its third fully electric model in just two years, following the BMW iX and BMW i7. The move towards electric vehicles is part of BMW's strategy to offer flexible architectures and multiple drivetrain variants to meet diverse customer demands, ensuring efficient use of plant capacity and alignment with market trends.

This transition to electric and hybrid models is supported by significant investments in the production network, with over one billion euros invested in integrating new models like the BMW iX, BMW 7 Series, and BMW 5 Series into the Dingolfing vehicle plant. This approach reflects BMW's adaptability to changing customer preferences and its commitment to innovation and sustainability in the automotive industry.
