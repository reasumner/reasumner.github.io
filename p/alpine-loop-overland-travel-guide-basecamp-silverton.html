Alpine Loop - Overland Travel Guide - Basecamp Silverton

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Follow along as we return to the Alpine Loop region with Drake (the REDARC Power Your Wild Sweepstakes Winner) and Crew as we film and explore the endless spectacular trails. We will be doing a lot of the alpine passes we didn't get to the previous year. Using Silverton as a basecamp we hit multiple passes each day using our new HUCK Cycles Overland AT Electric Motorbike as a production bike, Gladiator, Subaru Crosstrek and Rav4. Braxton from Welcome To The Outdoors was our second shooter on this one and killed it! The San Juan Mountains never disappoint! For the second year in a row we had amazing weather.
