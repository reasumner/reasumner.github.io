The NEXTORCH ND30B brings professional-quality distraction capabilities to civilian users, perfect for training scenarios, home security applications, and emergency signaling needs. This compact powerhouse shares the same impressive 11,000-lumen output and 130dB acoustic capability as its professional counterpart, but in a more user-friendly design with enhanced grip surfaces.

The versatile four-mode system makes the ND30B adaptable to any situation. Use single or double bursts for attention-getting signals during emergencies or outdoor activities. The 9-burst and 12-second continuous modes provide extended deterrent effects for home security applications or specialized training scenarios. The enhanced rubber grip zones ensure positive control in all weather conditions, while maintaining the durability of aerospace-grade aluminum construction.

Perfect for airsoft enthusiasts and tactical training groups, the ND30B provides a safe, reusable platform for force-on-force exercises without the cost and complications of disposable devices. The IPX7 water resistance rating means you can train in any weather, while the 3-meter impact resistance handles the bumps and drops of intense scenarios.

Power management is hassle-free with USB-C charging and capacity for 4,400 activations per charge. The 70-meter effective range provides ample standoff distance whether you're using it as an emergency beacon or as part of your home security plan. At just 265g and 127mm in length, the ND30B easily stores in a bedside drawer, vehicle emergency kit, or outdoor pack.
