Mercedes-Benz GL-Class. When Mercedes-Benz introduced its first luxury SUV, the M-Class, it enjoyed solid sales but lacked a proper third row of seats a hot commodity for anyone with a large family. The GL-Class fills the need for that extra passenger space by providing the luxury expected of a Mercedes while delivering the functionality of a full-size seven-passenger SUV.



Welcome to our comprehensive Kelley Blue Book review of the Mercedes-Benz GL-Class.



The Mercedes-Benz GL-Class is a four-wheel-drive dynamo, ready to take on anything from a run to the supermarket, to a trip to the mountains, to some off-road fun. A true SUV, the GL is not as large as a full-size American SUV, but it is very refined both inside and out with a well-appointed interior, unassuming exterior and a smooth, comfortable ride.
