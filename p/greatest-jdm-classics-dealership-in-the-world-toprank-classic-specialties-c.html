Greatest JDM Classics Dealership IN THE WORLD: TopRank Classic Specialties | Capturing Car Culture

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
If you've ever played Gran Turismo, you've probably dreamed about what your virtual garage would look like in real life if you actually owned all those cars. About an hour north of Tokyo, Japan, lies such a garage: TopRank Classic Specialties, a dealership that exclusively sells iconic JDM cars, as well as other prominent brands. If you can think of it, they've either sold it or currently have it in stock. Tune in as Larry Chen meets up with his friend Masa for an exclusive tour of this breathtaking dealership.
