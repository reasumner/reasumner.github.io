Mercedes-Benz Actros F Plus 2548 L 6x2 Swap-Body Lorry Truck (2023) Exterior and Interior

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
2023 Mercedes-Benz Actros F Plus 2548 L 6x2 Swap-Body Lorry Truck seen from outside and inside. Gross vehicle weight 25.0 tons. Front axle 8.0 t, air suspension. Rear axle, crown wheel 440, hypold, 13.0 t, trailing axle, 8 t, steered, liftable. Wheelbase 4,900 mm. L-cab StreamSpace, 2.50 m, tunnel 120 mm. Multimedia Cockpit Interactive. Engine is 3rd generation OM471, inline 6, 12.8 l, 350 kW (476 hp), 2,300 Nm. Engine design Euro VI, E. Transmission is PowerShift Advanced, G 281-12/14.93-1.0. Axle ratio i=2.412. Wear free brakes Secondary oil retarder; High Performance Engine Brake. Configuration and packages (some optional): MirrorCam, LED main headlamps, TrendLine, LED ambient lighting, driving and living, Economy Package, Predictive Powertrain Control (PPC). Safety systems (some optional): Lane Keeping Assist, Proximity Control Assist, Attention Assist, Traffic Sign Assist, Active Brake Assist, Tyre pressure monitor.

The truck was presented at IAA 2022, Hannover, Germany.
