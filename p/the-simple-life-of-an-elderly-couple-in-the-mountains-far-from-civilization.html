The simple life of an elderly couple in the mountains far from civilization.

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Hello everyone! I am Mykhailo, and in this documentary you will see the last footage of grandfather Joseph and his wife Lyudmila, unfortunately grandfather Joseph died, so Lyudmila moved to live in a village closer to civilization. In this film I remembered all the moments of life, and some of them I documented, so I will share them with you. All the facts in this documentary are true and correspond to reality. In the future, I would like to stay in this house located in the "Wild Forests of the Carpathians" far from civilization, raise livestock and grow crops, there is only one problem here - there is no electricity.
