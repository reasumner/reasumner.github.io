Driving on a dangerous rural road in the Tibetan area - Sichuan Province, China - 4K

Driving Overview:
Starting Point: Huaniu Village, Meiwo Township, Xiaojin County, Aba Tibetan and Qiang Autonomous Prefecture, Sichuan Province
End Point: Xiaojin County, Aba Tibetan and Qiang Autonomous Prefecture, Sichuan Province
Mileage: 16.9 km
Photography Date: June 2025

Starting Point:
Huaniu Village in Xiaojin County is a vibrant and distinctive village! It is located in the northwest of Meiwo Township, Xiaojin County, Aba Tibetan and Qiang Autonomous Prefecture, consisting of three village groups, with 111 households and a population of 377. The village's leading industry is the cultivation of traditional Chinese medicinal herbs.
1. Geographical Location and Natural EnvironmentHuaniu Village is situated on the plateau, with a suitable climate and fertile soil, providing exceptional natural conditions for the growth of medicinal herbs. Surrounded by mountains, the village is shaded by green trees, and the air is fresh, truly earning the title of a "natural oxygen bar."
2. Traditional Chinese Medicinal Herb Cultivation IndustryIn recent years, Huaniu Village has vigorously developed its traditional Chinese medicinal herb cultivation industry, relying on its ecological resource advantages. Currently, there are nearly 1,100 acres of medicinal herb cultivation in the village's 1,200 acres of arable land, with key varieties including Fritillaria cirrhosa, Nepeta tenuifolia, Rubus chingii, and Rhubarb, among others.Fritillaria Cirrhosa Cultivation: The cultivation base for Fritillaria cirrhosa in Huaniu Village has welcomed a harvest season in recent years. Villagers have achieved a bountiful yield through meticulous planting and management, producing well-formed and high-quality Fritillaria cirrhosa. It is reported that the value of 1 acre of Fritillaria cirrhosa can exceed 1 million yuan, making it a "golden industry" that helps villagers increase their income and wealth.Other Medicinal Herbs: In addition to Fritillaria cirrhosa, Huaniu Village also widely cultivates Nepeta tenuifolia, Rubus chingii, and other economically viable medicinal herbs with short growth cycles. These medicinal herbs not only have good ecological benefits but also bring substantial economic income to the villagers.

Destination:

As of June 2023, it governs seven towns and 11 townships. By the end of 2024, the county's permanent population was 64,100.
It's home to the stunning Siguniang Mountain, often referred to as the "Oriental Alps," which offers breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks and lush valleys. The county also boasts other scenic spots like Jiajin Mountain, a significant site in Chinese Red Army history.
Culturally, Xiaojin County is a melting pot of Tibetan and Qiang traditions, with vibrant festivals, traditional dances, and exquisite handicrafts.
Economically, it leverages its agricultural and tourism resources, producing high-quality apples, grapes, and matsutake mushrooms, while attracting visitors with its red tourism and eco-tourism offerings.
