My Mercedes GLS 63 AMG 2021 POV, Start up, Rev Off, Acceleration, Launch Control

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
My Mercedes GLS 63 AMG 2021 POV video with Start up, Rev Off, Acceleration & Launch Control.

The new Mercedes GLS 63 is a fantastic 4x4 4 litre twin turbo V8 monster from AMG. Despite its size, it drives like a Supercar, the suspension is much improved compared to the previous GLS 63 AMG, and irons out the bumps on any road nicely, even in Sports Plus mode! The AMG V8 engine has 625 BHP, when you put your foot down it feels like a rocket has launched. 0-60mph in 4.2 seconds and a top speed of 174mph.

I really love this Mercedes GLS 63 AMG 2021 model, it fits perfectly in my collection, a proper work horse. I have ordered another one which will arrive towards the end of the year.

Hope you enjoy this POV video of Start up, Rev off, Acceleration & Launch Control of my AMG monster.

Will post a video of speccing my next Mercedes GLS 63 AMG soon.
