In many parts of Russia, a unique form of housing from the Soviet era still remains: the "Barak." These pre-fabricated communal buildings, once temporary solutions for workers, are now the permanent homes for some families.

This video offers a quiet look into the daily life of one family living in such a building. We explore the reality of their living conditions and the adaptations they've made to their home.

In this video, you will see:

¬∑ The layout and condition of a typical barak apartment.
¬∑ How the family manages cooking, heating, and water.
¬∑ Their perspectives on the present and the future of their housing situation.
