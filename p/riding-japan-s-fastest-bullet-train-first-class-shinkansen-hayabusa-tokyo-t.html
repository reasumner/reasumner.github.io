Riding Japan's Fastest Bullet Train First Class - Shinkansen Hayabusa | Tokyo to Niseko

Published at : 14 Dec 2025
Today we will be taking a Gran Class (First Class) seat on the Shinkansen Hayabusa, Japan's fastest bullet train. We will travel from Tokyo to Shin-Hakodate-Hokuto in Hokkaido on the Hayabusa Shinkansen, then transfer to another train to Niseko.
