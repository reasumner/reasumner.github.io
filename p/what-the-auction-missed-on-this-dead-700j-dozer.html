What the Auction Missed on This Dead 700J Dozer

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
We picked up a dead John Deere 700J dozer at auction — the kind you know has a story behind it. It ran, but it wouldn’t move. Someone had been in it before us, parts were thrown together, valves were backed off, and something critical was missed. In this video, we tear into the pumps, PCP valves, electronics, calibration, and fuel system to track down why this 10,000-hour 700J refused to drive — and what it actually needed to get back to work.

If you like real shop diagnostics, auction equipment repairs, and fixing machines everyone else gave up on, you’re in the right place.
