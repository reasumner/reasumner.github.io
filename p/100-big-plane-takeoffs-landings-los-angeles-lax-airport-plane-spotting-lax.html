Welcome to Los Angeles International Airport, a global hub of aviation located on the west coast of the United States. LAX is a huge freight hub along with the gateway into the USA for the Asia-Pacific region. The Airport has 4 active parallel runways, all of which have been filmed in this video. Clips featured in this video range from the year 2018-2024, some aircraft featured are no longer flying in active service.

Enjoy a no commentary, long form LAX plane spotting video. No talking, just pure aviation! This video is filmed all over the airport, some locations in this video are popularised by live-streamers and others are a little more unique and unknown to keep LAX plane spotting fresh and exciting for your viewing experience!
