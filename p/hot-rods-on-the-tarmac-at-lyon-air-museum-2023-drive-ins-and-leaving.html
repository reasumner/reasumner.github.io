Hot Rods on the Tarmac at Lyon Air Museum (2023) - Drive-Ins and Leaving

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
So glad that this show returned this year at the Lyon Air Museum in Santa Ana, California! Hot Rods on the Tarmac highlights traditional hot rods and early customs, all gathered together at the Lyon Museum airplane hangar at John Wayne Airport. Big thanks to our good friend Ray Dunham for all the hard work he put in to organizing such a great event. And thanks to our friend Eric Justus for the ride into the museum!
