Can We Revive This Barn Find CB750 And Ride It Home?

Published at : 12 Dec 2025
Can we revive this barn find Honda CB750 and ride it home? In this video we definitely give it a try with buying an awesome candy gold 1972 CB750 that had sat in this barn for 42 years. Our goal is to get it running and ride it home over 200 miles away!
