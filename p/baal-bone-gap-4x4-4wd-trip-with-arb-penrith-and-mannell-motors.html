Baal Bone Gap, 4x4 4WD Trip with ARB Penrith and Mannell Motors

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
Join us on this trip with the team from Mannell Motors and ARB Penrith through to Baal Bone Gap. Filmed on one of their popular customer drive days, this trip features a range of 4WD vehicles tackling the easy and not so easy parts of the trail up to the Gap and back.

On this trip is a Nissan Patrol, Isuzu Dmax, Ford Ranger, Holden Colorado, Toyota Landcruiser, Toyota Prado, Nissan Navara, Toyota FJ, Cruiser, Mitsubishi Triton, Jeep Wrangler, Toyota Hilux and Suzuki Vitara.
