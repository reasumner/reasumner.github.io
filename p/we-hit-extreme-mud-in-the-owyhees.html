Published at : 13 Dec 2025
I take a group of folks from my Patreon Community on an off road adventure in the Idaho and Oregon Owyhees and we experience some of the very best views the area has to offer as well as some of the very worst mud. This off-road camping trip is a way I get to meet supporters of the channel and show them some of my favorite dispersed camping spots in Idaho and Oregon.
