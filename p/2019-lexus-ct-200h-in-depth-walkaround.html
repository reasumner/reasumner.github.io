2019 Lexus CT 200h in-depth Walkaround

The 2019 Lexus CT 200h boasts impressive specifications across its various categories:

Engine & Performance:
Under the hood, the Lexus CT 200h houses a robust powerplant that delivers both efficiency and performance. With an engine specification that includes a power output of 100 kW, translating to 136 horsepower (134 bhp), the vehicle showcases its dynamic capabilities. The engine reaches its maximum power at 5,200 rpm, while torque is equally noteworthy at 207 Nm (153 lb-ft), peaking within the range of 2,800 to 4,400 rpm. The engine is designed to run smoothly with synthetic oil, offering options such as 0W-20, 5W-20, and 5W-30, with an oil volume of 4.2 liters. The combustion configuration features a displacement of 1,798 ccs (1.8 liters) spread across 4 cylinders with 4 valves per cylinder. The engine configuration is inline, and synchronized with a timing chain for optimal performance.

Dimensions & Weight:
The exterior dimensions of the Lexus CT 200h emphasize its compact yet well-proportioned design. The vehicle measures 4,350 mm in length, 1,765 mm in width, and 1,455 mm in height. The front track spans 1,520 mm, while the rear track matches closely at 1,525 mm, ensuring stability during maneuvers. The wheelbase spans 2,600 mm, contributing to the vehicle's agility, and the turning circle measures 10.4 meters. The ground clearance stands at 135 mm, balancing style and practicality. In terms of weight, the curb weight ranges from 1,485 to 1,540 kg, with a gross weight capacity of 1,790 kg. The vehicle offers a cargo capacity of 250 to 305 kg and can support a maximum roof load of 75 kg.

Transmission & Drivetrain:
The Lexus CT 200h features a front-wheel-drive (FWD) drivetrain configuration, coupled with an automatic continuously variable transmission. This transmission setup contributes to a smooth and efficient driving experience, ideal for both urban commuting and longer journeys.

Fuel:
Running on petrol, the Lexus CT 200h is equipped with a 45-liter fuel tank, ensuring ample range for various driving scenarios.
