How to use Philips 1200/2200/800 Automatic Coffee Machine

Published at : 13 Dec 2025
In this 5 minute beginner's guide on how to use the Philips 800 1200 and 2200 series automatic espresso machine I will show you how to make espressos, black coffee, americanos and cappuccinos. I will show you how to use all the features, how to steam milk for latte art, how to set the grinder and how to adjust it based on taste, as well as what's important for a good tasting coffee and more.
